Sport in Panama

Sports in Panama have contributed much in shaping its culture. Though most of their sports are influenced by the U.S., they have successfully made their mark to be a globally competitive country.

Baseball is Panama’s national sport, influenced by their close ties to the USA. Their national team, called Panama National Baseball team, are a highly ranked baseball team in the world. Bruce Kastulo Chen is one of the notable baseball players of Panama.

Panamanians are not as passionate about football (soccer) as are many other Central American countries, though this may change after their national team qualified for the FIFA World Cup for the first time in 2018, after 10 failed qualification campaigns. Basketball is also gaining popularity among the citizens. They have regional teams that compete internationally. The most prominent basketball player they have had was Rolando Blackman who was a four-time NBA-All Star.

Baseball is a very popular sport in Panama

Another pride for Panama is boxing, and they currently have two reigning world boxing champions namely, Guillermo Jones and Anselmo Moreno, while Eusebio Pedroza, Roberto Duran, Isamel Laguna and Panama Al Brown are members of the International Boxing Hall of Fame. The Gulf Coast Triathlon is also a great sports event in Panama that started around 1982 and has been held annually since then. This event features a swim in the gulf of Mexico, a bike in Bay County, and a run through Panama City Beach.

Panama Sports trivia
