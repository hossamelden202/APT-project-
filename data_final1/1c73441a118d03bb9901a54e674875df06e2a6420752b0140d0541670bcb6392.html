The PGA Championship Venues

The US PGA Championship tournament is currently played annually in the USA in August. Here are the venues that have been used each year (and future years too!).

2021 US PGA at the Kiawah Island Golf Resort

The 2016 tournament was scheduled earlier than usual to avoid conflict with the Olympic golf tournament in Brazil.

The 2022 event was originally scheduled to be played at Trump National Golf Club Bedminster in New Jersey, but was moved to Southern Hills following the storming of the US Capitol earlier that year.
