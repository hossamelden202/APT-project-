    Explore Lab Safety Courses

    Browse Learning Opportunities >

    On-demand

    Foundations of Chemical Safety and Risk Management
    $0.00

    Undergraduate students with general and some organic chemistry laboratory experience will apply RAMP principles to laboratory operations in this 17-hour course. Chemistry departments can also incorporate this course into their curriculum as a stand-alone course.

    On-demand

    Foundations for Storing, Organizing and Disposing of Chemicals in Educational Settings
    $0.00
    Secondary school educators or graduate students who manage chemical storerooms or teach with chemicals will find this course useful. Concepts of safe chemical management are highlighted using the RAMP risk management system in this 10-hour course.

    In-person

    Laboratory Safety and Health

    Starting at $2,095.00

    Laboratory and pilot plant employees and those seeking to take the chemical hygiene officer certification exam will find this course helpful. Learn best practices to minimize personal injury, health impairment, property loss, regulatory fines, and liability in this two-day course.

    In-person

    Laboratory Safety

    Starting at $2,095.00

    Chemical safety professionals at universities and those seeking to take the chemical hygiene officer certification exam will find this course helpful. Explore the fundamental topics necessary to maintain safety and regulatory compliance in chemical laboratories in this two-day course.

    Safety is an ACS Core Value

    Safety is a core strategic value for the Society and it is highlighted in the ACS Strategic Plan.

    “We support and promote the safe, ethical, responsible, and sustainable practice of chemistry coupled with professional behavior and technical competence. We recognize a responsibility to safeguard the health of the planet through chemical stewardship.”

    Read Policy on “Safety in The Chemistry Enterprise”

    Develop Your Skills

    From student, to research team leader, to small business entrepreneur, safety skills and knowledge require conscious development and commitment from everyone involved in the chemical enterprise. The ACS Center for Lab Safety supports and promotes the safe, ethical, responsible, and sustainable practice of chemistry through easy access to authoritative tools, education, training, and guidance. Through our courses you can learn how to:

    Employ lab safety skills & best practices
    Manage risks & issues
    Evaluate systems & processes
