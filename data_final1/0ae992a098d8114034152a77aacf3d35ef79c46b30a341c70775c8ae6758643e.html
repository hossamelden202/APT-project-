Supplements for Post-Workout

Why You Need To Recover After A Workout

Working out is a science; it impacts the body in a way we may not see, but it is happening. To put it in the most basic terms, when you work out,  you are tearing your body. Post-workout recovery builds it back up. Cardio workouts are more aimed at cardiovascular endurance and simply burning off calories to promote total fat loss. In turn, however, you really put a serious dent in your body's energy stores. With strength training, you are building muscle, and that involves repair and growth. Meanwhile, endurance training ropes you down to base level, as your glycogen is all but depleted, and you are fatigued to no end. Thanks to all these, you need to rebuild what you have lost and, that is where recovery comes in.

What In Your Body Needs To Recover and What It Needs

The parts of your body that put in the most work when you do a workout are typically:

Muscles: You need a ton of protein to repair those and help build them as they recover from the tearing you just put them through.

Joints: For this, you need something to ease soreness and reduce inflammation like anti-inflammatory compounds,  like Omega 3.

Plus you also lose some important liquids mixed in with minerals in the form of sweat, and to remedy that you need loads of water with electrolytes thrown in. 

You also need fuel to keep you going and can get that from the carbs in a hearty meal after a workout.

How Supplements Can Help With Recovery Post Workout

The truth is most people don’t get the full amount of nutrition they require during the day. This is why supplements are such a handy-dandy tool for overall health and post-workout recovery!   • Protein Powder: This is not just for the “gym-bros.” This helps to feed those muscles you tore down and now comes in many delicious forms that you can turn into pancakes and shakes!
  • BCAAs (Branched-Chain Amino Acids): Now, you know that intense pain after a workout that gets worse the next day? With BCCAs, you can turn down the dial on it as they help you recover faster.
  • Electrolyte Supplements: Sweat is salty because you are not just losing water from your pores; you are losing some minerals that you really need as you work out. So, you need electrolyte supplements to get back all the stuff you lost.
  • Anti-Inflammatories: We cannot forget the joints and anti-inflammatory supplements like Omega-3 help to take down that inflammation that comes with an intense workout
