IPL Tournament Winners List

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is an annual professional Twenty20 cricket league in India where teams from different cities compete. Here are all the winners since the first IPL tournament in 2008.

The 2024 tournament was held from March 22nd until the final on May 26th.
