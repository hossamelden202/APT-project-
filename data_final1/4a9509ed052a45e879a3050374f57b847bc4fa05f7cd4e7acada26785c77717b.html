National Rugby League (NRL)

The National Rugby League (NRL) is the top professional league for men's rugby league teams in Australasia, currently with teams from Australia and New Zealand. Here are the winners from every season since the first in 1908.

The Penrith Panthers have won the last four Grand Finals, becoming the first team to win four straight Premierships since St George's 11 year run from 1956 to 1966.
