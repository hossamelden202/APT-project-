Greatest Athletes In The World - From All Sports

"You, sir, are the World's Greatest Athlete" King Gustav V of Sweden to Jim Thorpe after he won the decathlon at the 1912 Olympics in Stockholm. 

Who is the greatest athlete of all time? There have been numerous lists of the world's greatest athlete, and not surprisingly they do not agree. We believe the greatest athlete is not just the fittest, or the most skilled, but some combination of these and other factors which makes them stand out in their sport. Some of the greatest athlete lists are limited to a certain sport, region, or time period. Comparing athletes across sports and over time to find the greatest athlete ever is very difficult, and possibly impossible. Still, many have tried, including us!

Below are some of the lists and winners of such lists, from various sources, plus information about our own quest to find the world's greatest athlete of all time. See also our discussion and lists of the world's fittest athletes.

Our List

We are into the first stage in creating the Topend Sports list of the greatest athletes of all time by voting for the greatest athlete from all the major sports, then from this list deciding on the greatest of the greatest.

Greatest Athletes Lists

  • Associated Press The Sports 100: The 100 Greatest Athletes of the 20th Century, published in 1999, listed Babe Ruth as the greatest.
  • Sports Illustrated Greatest Athletes of the 20th Century - this book, published in 1999, listed 25 athletes, with Muhammad Ali the first on the list.
  • ESPN SportsCentury list of the 100 greatest athletes of the 20th century — Michael Jordan topped this list that was limited to athletes from North America.
  • NY Daily News in 2012 listed the top 12 athletes of the 21st century (so far), with Michael Phelps the winner.
  • Men's Journal in 2003 created a list of who they thought were the Best Athletes in the World. Their winner was American quarterback Michael Vick.
  • Wall Street Journal - a panel of sport science experts in 2008 rated the world's greatest male athlete as decathlete Roman Sebrle from Czechia.
  • Clay Parker in 2011 ran a virtual competitioncomparing current great athletes from around the world, with decathletes coming out on top.
  • Cricket Fans - Greatest Sportsperson Match-Up Competition, the 98 nominees were whittled down until Ali was declared the winner.
  • 7 Wonders — our list of the top-7 greatest athletes of all time.
