NFL Combine Testing Results for 2025

Here are the top-10 results for each event of the 2025 NFL Scouting Combine, which was held from February 24 until March 3, 2025 at the usua location, the Lucas Oil Stadium in Indianapolis.

  • Thursday, Feb. 27 – Linebackers and Defensive Line
  • Friday, Feb. 28 – Tight Ends, Cornerbacks, and Safeties
  • Saturday, March 1 – Quarterbacks, Running Backs, and Wide Receivers
  • Sunday, March 2 – Offensive Line

Over 300 top prospects were invited to participate at the combine. The data below is compiled from the top performers list published on NFL.com. See also the listings of top results from every year of the NFL Combine. For a description of each of these tests, see the page about the NFL Combine.
