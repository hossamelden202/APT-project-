Yo-Yo Intermittent Recovery Test Levels

The Yo-Yo Intermittent Test is a test of the ability to undertake intermittent exercise - there are two variations, below is the description of the recovery version and there is also the endurance version.

The participants in this test have a short 10 seconds active break after each 40m (2 x 20 m runs), with the speed increasing at intervals (see a full description of the Yo-Yo Intermittent Test). There are two versions of the Yo-Yo Intermittent Recovery Test: the Level or Stage 1 test starts at 10 km/hr and the Level or Stage 2 test starts at 13 km/hr. The Yo-Yo Intermittent Endurance Test has different speeds and only 5 second recovery between each 40m bouts. The Yo-Yo test recording can be performed using the Team BeepTest software.

The results normally report the speed level plus number of shuttles.
