WWE Survivor Series (November 18, 2023, Wells Fargo Center, Philadelphia, PA)

WWE Survivor Series is an annual pay-per-view event produced by the WWE. It is one of the "Big 4" pay-per-view events, and it is typically held in November. The 2023 Series will take place on November 18th at the Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia, PA. 

History of WWE Survivor Series

The event's origin dates back to 1987, when it was first held in Richfield, Ohio. Since then, it has become one of the year's biggest and most important events. Over the years, many notable moments have taken place at Survivor Series, such as The Undertaker’s debut in 1990, the formation of the nWo in 1996, and the first-ever Elimination Chamber match in 2002. 

2023 WWE Survivor Series Card

The Philadelphia WWE event is set to be one of the biggest live wrestling events in professional wrestling history. With the return of the annual pay-per-view spectacular, usually held on the third Sunday of November, Survivor Series will be the first event in the Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

The traditional Survivor Series format will be in full effect, with teams of five wrestlers from both Raw and SmackDown going head-to-head in a series of elimination matches. The winning team will be the last one standing, with the individual wrestlers from the winning team advancing to the finals. As with any major event, the stakes are high, and the competition will be fierce.

The main event of Survivor Series will feature WWE Champion John Cena defending his title against Universal Champion Seth Rollins and Intercontinental Champion Randy Orton in a triple threat match. This match will surely be an all-out war, as all three men will be putting their all into the match to win the championship.

WWE match between Ray Mysterio and Chris Jericho, 2008

The card is also set to feature a tag team turmoil match, where two teams will compete in a series of elimination matches until one team is left standing. The winning team will be crowned the Tag Team Champions and will also be in contention for the prestigious WWE Tag Team Championship.

Also on the card is a Divas tag team match, where the WWE Women's Champion, AJ Lee, will team up with the NXT Women's Champion, Paige, to take on the team of Divas Champion Natalya and her partner. This match will surely be a show-stealer as the Divas will pull out all the stops to prove who the best female wrestlers in the business are.

Conclusion

Aside from the matches, Survivor Series will also feature a plethora of special attractions. The Wells Fargo Center will be decked out with a special video wall, pyrotechnics, and live bands to create an electric atmosphere. There will also be several special guests appearing throughout the night, and many surprises are sure to be in store.

One thing is certain: Survivor Series will surely be an event to remember. With the annual event's return, WWE fans worldwide can get tickets for this live wrestling event online. They should expect an amazing night of professional wrestling action and a chance to see some of the biggest stars in the business going head-to-head. The Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia will be packed to the rafters, and the atmosphere will be electric.
