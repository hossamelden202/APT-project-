Netball World Cup

The Netball World Cup, previously known as the Netball World Championships, has been held every four years since 1963. It is coordinated by the International Federation of Netball Associations. The tournament has been dominated by the Australian and New Zealand teams, with Australia winning the last World Cup in 2023 held in South Africa.
