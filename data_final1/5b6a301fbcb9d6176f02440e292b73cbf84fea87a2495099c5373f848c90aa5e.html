Popular sports of The Soviet Union

  • Football (Soccer), Gymnastics

The Soviet Union Sporting Success

  • At six of its nine appearances at the Summer Olympic Games between 1952 and 1988, the Soviet team (USSR) ranked first in the total number of gold medals won, it was second on the other three occasions.

Great athletes from The Soviet Union

  • In 1964, Soviet gymnast Larissa Latynina won six medals for the third time in a row; becoming the Olympic athlete with the most medals (18) (it has now been surpassed)

The Soviet Union Sports trivia

  • Aleksandr Ditiatin of the Soviet Union earned a record 8 Gold medals in gymnastics at the 1980 games.
