NFL Combine Testing Results for 2022

Here are the top-10 results for each event of the 2022 NFL Scouting Combine, which was held from March 1-7, 2022. This year the combine returned to its usual structure with all testing conducted in Indianapolis, Indiana. Last year the pandemic caused the fitness assessments to be held as part of the individual college pro days, and the interviews held virtually.

Over 300 top prospects were invited to participate at the combine. The data below is compiled from the top performers list published on NFL.com. See also the listings of top results from every year of the NFL Combine. For a description of each of these tests, see the page About the NFL Combine.
