The World's Highest Paid Athletes List 2020

The top earner for 2020 was tennis player Roger Federer, with earnings totaling 106.3 million US dollars. He is the first tennis player to rank No. 1 since the list was first created in 1990. Last year's number one, Lionel Messi, dropped to third. The football players who were the top three last year all dropped down the list due to corona-virus impacted salary cuts. For the first time since 2016 there are two women in the top 100 list, Naomi Osaka (29th) and Serena Williams (33rd).

Below is the top 10 of the highest earning sportspeople for 2020, taken from the annual list “The World's 100 Highest-Paid Athletes” published by Forbes magazine in June 2020. The earnings totals (in US dollars) are derived from salaries, bonuses, prize money, appearance fees, licensing and endorsement income in the 12 months from June 1, 2019 to June 1, 2020. Taxes or agents' fees are not factored into the figures. See also the Forbes top-earning lists from other years.
