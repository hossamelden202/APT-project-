2013 World Sporting Event Calendar

Here is a list of the major sporting events that were held in 2013. Not every sporting event is listed here, though we have tried to include all the big sporting events of the major sports, mostly the international competitions.

Major events for this year included the America's Cup in yachting, rugby union 6-nations tournament, and the Summer and Winter Universiade, See a summary of the world of sport for 2013 including selected results.
