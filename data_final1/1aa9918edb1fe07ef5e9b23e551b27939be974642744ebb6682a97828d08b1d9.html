Birmingham 2022 Commonwealth Games Medal Tally

Here is the final medal count for each country at the Birmingham Commonwealth Games in 2022. Australia has continued its dominance at the top of the table. Since 1990, Australia has finished on top at all Commonwealth Games except one. See the medal results for each Commonwealth Games.

2022 Final Ranking

Australia clearly won the most gold medals, but based on total medals, only beat England by three medals. The order is not based on total medals won. As per convention, ranking is based first on the number of gold medals, then silver and bronze.
