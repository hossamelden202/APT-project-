Reaction Time Tests

Reaction time tests measure the time it takes for an individual to respond to a stimulus. These tests are diverse in scope, ranging from simple online exercises to complex laboratory assessments. Online reaction time tests typically involve auditory or visual cues, prompting participants to react as quickly as possible, often by clicking a button.
