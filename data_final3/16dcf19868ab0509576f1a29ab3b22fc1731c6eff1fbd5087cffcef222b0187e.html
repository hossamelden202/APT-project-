AFL Draft Combine Results 2024

Here are the top fitness test results from the 2024 NAB AFL Draft Combine. There were 67 players invited to the national combine held in Melbourne on October 4-6, and (see the full list of invitees). Club interviews and medical screenings were conducted over the first two days. The 2km time trial was completed at the AIA Centre on Friday, and the remainder of the tests on Sunday October 6 at the Melbourne Sports and Aquatic Centre (MSAC). All testing was conducted by Rookie Me.

An additional 62 players were invited to state draft combines in Victoria, South Australia, and Western Australia. The Victorian State Combine took place on the same Sunday, with the South Australian and West Australian State Combines on the following weekend, Saturday October 12 and Sunday October 13 respectively. (see details and results).
