ACSM Conference

More than 6,000 professionals from around the world attend this comprehensive sports medicine and exercise science meeting, held annually in the USA. If your field is sport science or sports medicine, this is the premier conference that you should try and attend.

The very first ACSM annual meeting was held in New York on April 22, 1954, held in association with the American Association for Health, Physical Education.
