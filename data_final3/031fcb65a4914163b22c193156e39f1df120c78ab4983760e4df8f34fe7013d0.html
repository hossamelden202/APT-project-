2017 NHL Entry Draft Combine Results

This was the 24th time the annual NHL Draft Combine had been held. The combine assessment involves interviews, medical screenings, and fitness tests over a four-day period.

The world's top ice hockey prospects are invited to the combine, with over 100 draft-eligible players from North American and Europe being tested (the complete list of 2017 combine invitees).

The Combine was held for the second time in Buffalo, from May 29 to June 3 at KeyBank Center and Harbor Center.

Below are the results of the fitness assessments for the 2017 NHL Scouting Combine (see details of the testing procedures). See also a summary of previous NHL Entry Draft Combine Results.
