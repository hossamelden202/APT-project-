AFL Draft Combine Results 2016

Here are the top fitness test results from the 2016 AFL draft combine. The testing was held at Etihad Stadium from October 6 to October 9, 2016, with 80 or so players tested (see player list) including some internationals.

See also the top draft camp results for the top performers from each year since 2004. Additional players will be tested in Melbourne, Adelaide and Perth at a later date as part of the 2016 state based testing.

As per the last few years, the tests were conducted at Etihad Stadium in Melbourne, held under the direction of the staff from the Australian Institute of Sport. The athletic testing was conducted on a purpose-built sports floor that was installed on a wing of the ground, with the kicking and handball tests taking place on the turf.
