Predicted 2025 Ballon d'Or Winners (Collated)

The Ballon d'Or Award is an annual award for the best international football player, awarded by a panel of selected journalists from the top football playing countries. During the year leading up to the Ballon d'Or in 2025 there is a lot of speculation about who will win. We have scoured the web and found the following predictions for the men's and women's award.

Men's Ballon d'Or for 2025

The 2025 award will be decided over the 2024/25 season, as well as the 2025 international tournaments. The 2024 Ballon d'Or winner was Rodri, who will not have chance to defend crown after tearing ACL.

How do the top football websites rank the contenders for the men's Ballon d'Or in 2025? We have collated the opinions of a few of the top football websites to compare.
