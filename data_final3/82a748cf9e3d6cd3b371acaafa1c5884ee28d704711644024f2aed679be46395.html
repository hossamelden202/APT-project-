All-Time Medal Tally at the Commonwealth Games

Here's a list of the total medals won from all Commonwealth Games including results from Birmingham 2022. Australia has the most gold medals in total, and the most medals overall. Australia has dominated the table recently, finishing in the top position for every Commonwealth Games since 1990, except when it was hosted in England in 2014.

This table includes some countries that no longer participate in the Commonwealth Games. 50 countries have won at least one gold medal, and 64 Countries have won at least one medal of any colour.
