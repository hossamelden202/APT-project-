Sport in Czechia (Czech Republic)

Below are details of sports, sporting events and sports people related to Czechia (also known as the Czech Republic). See also Sport in Czechoslovakia. Czechia is a Central European country that is landlocked and bordered by Germany (west), Austria (south), Slovakia (south-east) and Poland (north-east).

Most Czechs are loyal supporters of various sports like soccer, basketball, volleyball, kickball and tennis but ice hockey is by far the most popular sport. Their national ice hockey team is one of the very best in the world. They regularly compete in the World Championships as well as the Winter Olympics Games and even the Euro Hockey Tour. As a matter of fact, their very first Winter Olympic gold medal was from ice hockey in 1988 held in Nagano, Japan.
