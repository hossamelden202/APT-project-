Allegiant Stadium, Paradise, Nevada USA

Allegiant Stadium is located in Paradise, Nevada USA, essentially in the suburbs of Las Vegas. It is a domed multi-purpose stadium which opened in 2020. It is the home of the Las Vegas Raiders of the NFL. The stadium has a capacity of 65,000 for NFL games, (expandable to 71,835).

Allegiant Stadium, Paradise, Nevada USA

Major Events Held at Allegiant Stadium

Here are just some of the major events that have been held at Allegiant Stadium:

  • Vegas Kickoff Classic - annually in early September
  • Las Vegas Bowl - annually in December
  • 2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup final
  • 2024 Super Bowl
  • 2024 Copa América

Allegiant Stadium Trivia

  • The stadium cost $1.9 billion, at the time was the second-most expensive stadium in the world.
  • There is a large 85-foot / 26 meter torch containing a flame in honor of Al Davis, the late longtime owner of the Raiders. In 2019, the torch was reported to be the largest 3D printed object in the world.
  • Two matches in the first round of the 2024 Australian National Rugby League (NRL) season will be played at Allegiant Stadium in March 2024, the first time official matches are played in the United States.
