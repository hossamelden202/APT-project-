International Football Awards

Below are some of the major international soccer / football awards. See also list of league winners and other football winners lists, and FIFA World Cup Awards.

  • Winners of the Ballon d'Or award (since 1956)
  • Winners of the Women's Ballon d'Or award (since 2018)
  • FIFA Player of the Year - men — from 1991 to present.
  • Player of the Year - World Soccer — from 1982 to present
  • FIFA Player of the Year - women — from 2001 to present.
  • FIFA Team of the Year — from 1993 to present.
  • FIFA Best Mover of the Year — from 1993 to present.
  • FIFA Pusk?s Award — annual award for the goal of the year. From 2009 to 2023 it was for both male and female players. Since 2024, it is a male only award.
  • FIFA Marta Award — annual award for the goal of the year by a female player.
  • FIFA Best Goalkeeper Award (male and female) — from 2017 to present
  • FIFA Coach of the Year — since 2010.
  • FIFA Fair Play Award — since 2010.
  • Ballon d'Or Coach of the Year — since 2024.
  • Ballon d'Or Club of the Year — since 2021. Awarded to the club with the most Ballon d'Or nominees.
  • Ballon d'Or Striker of the Year — since 2021.
  • Socrates Award — since 2022.
  • Kopa Trophy — annual award for the best young men's player.
  • Ballon d'Or Dream Team — based on fan votes in 2020, it's a team that brings together the greatest talents football has ever seen.
  • European Golden Shoe — for the leading goalscorer from the top division of the European national leagues.
  • Yashin Trophy — annual award for the best male goalkeeper.
  • Club Champions from Europe — for all seasons.
