NFL Veteran Combine 2015

The NFL's inaugural Veteran Combine was held on March 22, 2015, at the Arizona Cardinals' Training Facility in Phoenix, Arizona. This event was open to free agent players that were previously eligible for an NFL Draft. In 2016, the Veteran Combine was canceled due to lack of interest from teams.

At the 2015 event there were over 100 participants (see below).
