The Greatest ICC Champions Trophy Moments

The ICC Champions Trophy has witnessed some of the most electrifying performances in cricketing history. From underdog triumphs to dominant displays by cricketing powerhouses, every edition of the tournament has had its share of drama and brilliance.

Memorable Wins That Shaped Cricket History

2025: India Clinches Their Third ICC Champions Trophy

The latest edition of the ICC Champions Trophy 2025 saw India lifting their third title after a thrilling final against New Zealand in Dubai. With Rohit Sharma leading the side, India showcased exceptional batting depth and bowling precision, securing a historic victory that cemented their status as one of the tournament’s most successful teams.

2017: Pakistan’s First ICC Champions Trophy Victory

Pakistan’s moment of glory came in 2017, when they stunned the cricketing world with a dominant victory over India in the final at The Oval, England. Led by Sarfaraz Ahmed, Pakistan posted a mammoth total before their fiery bowling attack, spearheaded by Mohammad Amir, decimated India’s batting lineup, securing their maiden ICC Champions Trophy win.

2013: MS Dhoni’s Tactical Brilliance

India’s win in 2013 under the calm and calculated leadership of MS Dhoni was a testament to his cricketing genius. The rain-affected final at Edgbaston saw India edge past England, with an exceptional performance from the young bowlers, proving why Dhoni remains one of the greatest captains in cricket history.

Australia’s Unstoppable Reign (2006 & 2009)

During the 2000s, Australia was a dominant force in cricket, and their back-to-back ICC Champions Trophy victories in 2006 (India) and 2009 (South Africa) under Ricky Ponting showcased their sheer dominance. Their all-round performances and relentless aggression made them one of the most feared teams in the tournament’s history.
