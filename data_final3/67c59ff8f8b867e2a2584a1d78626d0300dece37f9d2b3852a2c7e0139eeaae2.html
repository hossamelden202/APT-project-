2015 Ballon d'Or Results

The Ballon d'Or award is an annual football award for the best international player from the previous year. Between 2010 and 2015 the Ballon d'Or and the FIFA Player of the Year awards were merged to create the FIFA Ballon d'Or.

Messi's Ballon d'Or from 2015

Below are the nominees in the final order after voting for the 2015 award. The winner was Lionel Messi, the fifth time he has won this award. See the list of all Ballon d'Or winners.
