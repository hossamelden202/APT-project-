2011 World Sporting Event Calendar

Here is a list of the major sporting events that were held in 2011. Not every sporting event is listed here, though we have tried to include all the big sporting events of the major sports, mostly the international competitions.

Major events for this year were the Women's Football World Cup, and other World Cups for Baseball, Rugby, and Cricket. See a summary of the world of sport for 2011 including results.
