Venues of the 2021 Tokyo Olympic Games

Here are all the venues to be used for the Tokyo Olympic Games, with the events to be held at each venue listed. There are over 40 sporting venues spread over several zones.

List of Venues

  • Olympic Stadium (Japan National Stadium) - The new National Stadium has been built for the 2020 Summer Games. At this main stadium, the athletics, football, opening and closing ceremonies will be held. The Olympic cauldron used at Tokyo 1964 has been preserved at the stadium. See our list of all Olympic Stadiums.
