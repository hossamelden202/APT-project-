2021 NBA Draft Combine Results

A series of 'Athleticism Tests' are performed at the NBA pre-draft camp combine held annually. In 2021, the combine returned to a single venue, after last year's coronavirus affected combine was held at the payer's home location. The 2021 combine was held from June 21 to 27, at Wintrust Arena and the Marriott Marquis in Chicago. See the list of the 2021 participants.

Below are the top-10 results for the major tests conducted. Figures are based on official NBA documents published online. See a description of the tests, and the best results for each year since 2000.
