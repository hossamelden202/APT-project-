IPL Most Valuable Player Winners List

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is an annual professional Twenty20 cricket league in India where teams from different cities compete. Here are all the winners of the Most Valuable Player Award since the first IPL tournament in 2008. From 2008 until 2012, the award was called the Man of the Tournament. Since 2013, it is the Most Valuable Player award. The winner is based on a points system, based on sixes and fours hit, dot balls bowled and catches made.

Notes

  • The tournament has been held since 2008.
  • For every four hit is 2.5 points, a six is equal to 3.5 points. Every dot ball bowled gets 1 point. Every catch taken or stumping is equal to 2.5 points.
