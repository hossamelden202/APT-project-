Women's Football

Goal 8 of FIFA President Gianni Infantino's Strategic Objectives for the Global Game: 2023-2027 set out to deliver the greatest FIFA Women’s World Cup™ ever in 2023 to help drive further development in women’s football, and the tournament in Australia and Aotearoa New Zealand exceeded all expectations. FIFA’s long-term approach is to ensure that women’s football development continues at an accelerated pace with competition reform, the modernisation of women’s development programmes and the continued professionalisation of the women’s game.
