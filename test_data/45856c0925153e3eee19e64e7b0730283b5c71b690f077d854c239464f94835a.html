World Antimicrobial Awareness Week 2021

World Antimicrobial Awareness Week is a major global campaign observed every year from 18 to 24 November 2021. The slogan of this year’s campaign is “Antimicrobials: Handle with Care” under the theme “Spread awareness, stop resistance”.
