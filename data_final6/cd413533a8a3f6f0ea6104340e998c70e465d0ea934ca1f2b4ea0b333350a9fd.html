AFL Draft Combine Results 2020

Here are the top fitness test results from the 2020 NAB AFL Draft Combine. The usual annual national draft Combine that has been held in Melbourne for the past decade was not held due to restrictions due to the coronavirus pandemic. Instead, there was a series of 2020 state based testing combines.

Below is our top lists combining all the known state-based results (some tests/states missing). See also the men's top draft camp results for the top performers from each year since 2004.

A women's combine was held, with testing also conducted at the players' home states. See the invited player lists for the men and women.
