NFL Combine Invite List 2023

Here is the official list of over 300 NFL combine participants, for the event held from Feb 28-Mar 6, 2023. The NFL Scouting Combine is open to college seniors immediately following their final year of NCAA eligibility, or to underclassmen who have been out of high school for three years and satisfied all the NCAA and NFL requirements.

See the test results from the 2023 Combine.
