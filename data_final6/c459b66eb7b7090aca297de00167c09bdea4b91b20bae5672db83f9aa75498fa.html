2024 NBA Draft Combine Results

A series of 'Athleticism Tests' are performed at the NBA pre-draft camp combine held annually. The 2024 combine was held May 12-19, at Wintrust Arena and the Marriott Marquis in Chicago. See the list of the 2024 participants.

Beginning in 2024, participation in the combine will be mandatory for a player to be eligible for the draft.

Below are the top-10 results for the major tests conducted. Figures are based on official NBA documents published online. See a description of the tests, and the best results for each year since 2000.
