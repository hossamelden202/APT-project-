Advertising Rates on Topend Sports

The Topend Sports website is a popular information source for sports, fitness and nutrition. The Topend Sports website (www.topendsports.com) has been online for over 20 years, and has become the number one resource for those interested in sports, science and fitness information. The sections on fitness testing, training and sports nutrition are particularly popular with those in the fitness and health industry seeking authoritative information. The site was developed and is maintained by Rob Wood, who holds a PhD in exercise physiology.

For businesses wishing to advertise on Topend Sports, there are a few different options summarized below.

PLEASE NOTE: we do not accept text link placements within the content of my site. If you wish to advertise it must be clear that it is an ad, and preferably in one of the sections listed below

Newsletter

Advertising is available in the fortnightly topendsports newsletter, which currently has over 50,000 subscribers. Prices start at $100 per newsletter. See more details about Advertising in the Newsletter. Please contact me to discuss your requirements.

Guest Posts

We occasionally accept guest posts on Topend Sports. Prices start at US$250. No gambling or adult content ads or anything like that. See more about Guest Posting.

Banner Advertising

Sorry, banner advertising is not available at this time.

Advertising Conditions

  • An initial payment must be received prior to any ads being displayed.
  • Payment is in US dollars. The preferred payment method is via PayPal (most credit cards accepted).
  • Advertisers must specify one URL link which will be referenced in a new window by clicking on the text link.

Contact and Bookings

If you have any questions, or to book your online advert, please contact Rob Wood.
