2015 International AFL Combine

Here is information about international Australian Football combines in 2015. We have details about a USA combine and one in New Zealand. These international combines use the same tests as conducted at the AFL Draft Combine.

USA Combine

This was the fourth year the AFL has conducted a combine in the USA. Here are the top results from the three day event held at IMG Academy in Sarasota, Florida in April 2015. The players tested were 15 selected basketballers from colleges all across America. At least two of these will be selected to attend the AFL Draft Combine in Melbourne later in 2015.

New Zealand Combine

Regional combines are held throughout the year, with selected players being invited to the national combine, to held 23 April 2015.
