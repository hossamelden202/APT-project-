Amazon Stores FAQ

Most of the products for sale in the online stores found throughout this site are processed through the worldwide power of Amazon.com, and as such have the secure booking, worldwide shipping and trusted service that it is known for. Exceptions are items in the Topend Sports Ebay Store, such as the Slim Guide Fat Caliper.

The following answers may cover any general issues you may have. If the answer to your question is not here, use the Amazon links once you proceed with the order, or else contact me. This information is just a brief summary of the Amazon policies. For the most up to date and more comprehensive information about these details and Amazon policies, please refer directly to their website.

Can I get the contact details to finalize my order?

Many businesses will need to have the phone, fax and address details of the company to process their purchase order. The advertisers on this website are in no way associated with Topend Sports, there is a large number of different suppliers, and we do not know any contact details. You will have to contact the provider directly. If there are no contact details in the product description, there may be more information provided as you proceed with your order. Otherwise you will need to find the company details though other sources.

Will You Ship to ... ?

Amazon.com can ship to virtually any address in the world. Warranty issues and manufacturer restrictions prevent shipping certain products to all geographical locations. You'll be notified while placing your order if they are unable to ship specific items to the address you have selected. There is usually a choice of shipping methods which will have different costs. You will have to go through the checkout procedure to find out the details and prices.

How much will shipping be?

You will have to go through the checkout procedure to find out the details and prices. You will not have to finalize your order until you have reviewed and accepted these details.

What if I don't want it, or need a refund?

The standard policy at Amazon is to accept most returns within 30 days from the date of delivery. You may return new, unopened items sold and fulfilled by Amazon.com within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The items should be returned in their original packaging. They will also pay the return shipping costs if the return is a result of an error. See details when you are making the purchase.

Do you have more information about the product?

As all the products are handled through Amazon, I am not able to answer specific questions about each product. There is already some information on this site about some products, such as the skinfold caliper buying guide, guide to buying a treadmill. On each product page you will find more information about the product, and there are usually some reviews which will give reader's opinions about the product.

Do you accept credit cards?

Amazon accepts all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard/EuroCard, Discover Network, American Express, Diner's Club (U.S. billing addresses only), JCB, Visa or MasterCard check cards or ATM cards.

Is the order process secure?

The checkout section of the store is handled by Amazon, where you can safely enter your entire credit card number via their secure server, which encrypts all submitted information.

Why don't I just order straight though Amazon?

Yes, you can go to the Amazon website and order the same items, and you will get the same price and same service. On this website, I have done some of the searching for you and have categorized the items and made it easy for you to find and order the products you are after. If you wish, visit the Amazon Store, where all of these items plus more are available.

For the most up to date and more comprehensive information about these details and Amazon policies, please refer directly to the Amazon.com website.
