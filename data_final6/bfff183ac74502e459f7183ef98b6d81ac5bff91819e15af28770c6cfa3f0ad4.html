Super Bowl Host Stadiums [2025 Update]

Here is a list of the stadiums which have hosted the Super Bowl. The stadium which has hosted the Super Bowl the most number of times is the Caesars Superdome, in New Orleans, Louisiana. Some stadiums have changed names over time, the latest known name is used in the table. Former names of the Caesars Superdome’s are Louisiana Superdome and Mercedes-Benz Superdome.
