AFL Draft Combine Results 2014

Here are the top fitness test results from the 2014 AFL draft combine, as published on the official AFL website. The testing was held at Etihad Stadium from September 30 to October 3, 2014, with nearly 100 players tested (see player list) including some internationals.

See also the top draft camp results for the top performers from each year since 2004. Additional players were be tested in Melbourne, Adelaide and Perth at a later date as part of the 2014 state based testing.

As per the last few years, the tests were conducted at Etihad Stadium in Melbourne, held under the direction of the staff from the Australian Institute of Sport. The athletic testing was conducted on a purpose-built sports floor that was installed on a wing of the ground, with the kicking and handball tests taking place on the turf.
