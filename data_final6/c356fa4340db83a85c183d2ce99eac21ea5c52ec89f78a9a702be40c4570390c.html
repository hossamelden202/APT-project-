Sport in Mauritania

The Islamic Republic of Mauritania is a North African country located in the Maghreb region. It is one of the largest countries in Africa and as with most of Africa, Mauritania has been plagued with civil unrest for the most part of its history but one of the things that have brought the people together is sports.

And when it comes to sports, Mauritania simply loves the football. Their national football team is actively seeking approval in the international stage by playing against various teams and wishes to secure a spot in the African Cup of Nations.

Basketball is also quite popular I the region even among the women. Although women also play football, their national women's football team has never played in any FIFA match. But as far as football athletes are concerned, Mauritania do boasts of a few golden nuggets like Adama Ba. This Mauritanian-born football player has earned a place in the international stage and is now an attacking midfielder for the Auxerre in the French Ligue 2.
