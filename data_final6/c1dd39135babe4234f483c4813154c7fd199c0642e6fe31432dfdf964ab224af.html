Basketball Demonstrations at the Olympics

Basketball was a demonstration event at the Olympic Games in 1904 in St. Louis. It has also been an official sport from 1936 onwards. There was also basketball played as part of the children's games and sports demonstration at the 1924 Olympics.

There were four different basketball events in Saint Louis in 1904; Amateur championships, College basketball, High school basketball, and Elementary school basketball. The Amateur Athletic Union (AAU) national basketball tournament served as a demonstration sport at the St. Louis Olympics. It was won by Buffalo German YMCA.
