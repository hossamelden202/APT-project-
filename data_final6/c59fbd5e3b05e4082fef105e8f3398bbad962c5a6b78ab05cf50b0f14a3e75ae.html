Winners of the ICC T20 World Cup

The ICC T20 World Cup is held approximately every two years. Seven tournaments have so far been played since the first in 2007. The current title holders are India, who will be the co-host of the next event in 2026 along with Sri Lanka.

England , West Indies and India are the three teams to have won the tournament more than once.
