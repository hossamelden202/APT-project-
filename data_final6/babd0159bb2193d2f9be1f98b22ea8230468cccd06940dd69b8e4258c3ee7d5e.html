The Immersive Tutankhamun Exhibition

The Grand Egyptian Museum
From
November 21, 2023 - 09:00 AM
To
January 21, 2024 - 04:00 AM

The Grand Egyptian Museum opened the Immersive Tutankhamun Exhibition, through which visitors can embark on a journey with the golden King Tutankhamun, who dazzled the world with his tomb.
Through an interactive experience, using state-of-the-art digital projections, visitors will be able to transport themselves to ancient Egypt, and they can embark on an audio-visual journey dating back more than 3,400 years ago.
