Fitness Testing using Walking

There is a range of fitness tests using walking as the primary exercise mode. As walking is a low weight-bearing form of exercise, it is utilized in many tests of the elderly or obese.

Here is a list of fitness tests that use the walking exercise mode, from our list of hundreds of fitness tests.

Aerobic Walking for Time or Distance Tests

  • 1/2 mile walk test
  • 1 Mile walk test
  • Rockport Walk Test Ś walk one mile as fast as you can.
  • 1 Mile Endurance Run / Walk Test Ś complete one mile in the fastest possible time.
  • Eurofit 2-Km Walk Test
  • 6-Minute Walk Ś a fitness test for seniors, walk for 6 minutes for maximum distance.

Aerobic Beep Type Tests Involving Walking

  • 10m Beep Test Ś 10m multi-stage walking shuttle test for children with cerebral palsy.
  • 10m Shuttle Walk Test Ś 10m multi-stage walking shuttle test designed for patients with COPD.
  • Groningen Walk Test Ś a variant of the maximal multistage 20m shuttle-run test, involving walking around a rectangular course, adapted to suit an elderly population.
walking treadmill test

Treadmill Walking Tests

  • Balke Test (treadmill) Ś walking on a treadmill to exhaustion, at a constant speed while gradient/slope is increased every one or two minutes.
  • Chester Treadmill Police Walk Test Ś walking at 6km/hr on a treadmill, increasing gradient by 3% every 2 minutes.
  • Chester Treadmill Test Ś walking at 6.2km/hr on a treadmill, increasing the gradient by 3% every 2 minutes.
