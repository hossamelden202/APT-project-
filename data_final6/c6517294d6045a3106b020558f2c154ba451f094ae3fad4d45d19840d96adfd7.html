World Series Winners List

The following table lists the past winners and runners up of the World Series, from the first in 1903 when the Boston Pilgrims beat the Pittsburgh Pirates. See also the winners of the most valuable player (MVP).

The winner of World Series has been determined through a best-of-7 playoff - except for in 1903, 1919, 1920, and 1921, when the winner was determined through a best-of-9 playoff. The winning team is awarded the Commissioner's Trophy.
