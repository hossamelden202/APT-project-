2001 Ballon d'Or Results

The Ballon d'Or award is an annual football award for the best player from the previous year. From 1995 until 2007 the award was open to all players from around the world, as long as they had been been active at European clubs during that year.

Michael Owen's Ballon d'Or from 2001

Below are the nominees in the final order after voting for the 2001 award. The winner was Englishman Michael Owen, who was only aged 22 years. He was the first Englishman to win this award since Kevin Keegan in 1979. See the list of all Ballon d'Or winners.
