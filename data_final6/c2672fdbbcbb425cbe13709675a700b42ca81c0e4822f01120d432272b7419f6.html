Membership

Why We Support Science

Science leads to breakthroughs that improve lives, fuel economies, and tackle global challenges. Our community of dedicated scientists advances knowledge and invites everyone to succeed. ACS remains resolute in support of chemists and the chemistry enterprise.

At ACS, we work every day to support scientists who are doing innovative, life-saving, and inspiring work. We champion science policy by engaging with political leaders and collaborating with other scientific societies. See how we support our community and join us in advocating for science policy.

Access the Proactive Membership Assistance Program

A job loss can disrupt your day-to-day life and completely redefine what normalcy and stability mean to you and your family. ACS is here to help. The Proactive Membership Assistance Program is an initiative that supports those experiencing career disruption.
