Home of Disc Sports

There are a range of sports or games played with flying discs, often called by the trademark name Frisbee. There are quite a few different official Disc Sports. The most popular are:

  • Ultimate (Frisbee) — a team game played with a disc (Frisbee), with points scored by passing the disc to a teammate in the opposing end zone.
  • Disc Golf — Frisbee golf, the objective is to traverse a course from start to end with the fewest number of throws of the disc.
  • Freestyle Flying Disc — creative, acrobatic, and athletic maneuvers with a flying disc.

Other disc sports include:

  • Guts — where teams alternate between throwing a disc as hard as they can, and trying to catch such throws.
  • Double Disc Court (DDC)
    — a little like tennis with frisbees.
  • Discathon — a competition which resembles disc golf with time constraints.
  • Other Disc Events: Accuracy, Distance, MTA (Maximum Time Aloft) and TRC (Throw, Run and Catch).
  • Self Caught Flight
  • Fricket — a ‘two-on-two’ flying disc game. Also known as disc cricket, cups, suzy sticks and crispy wickets.
  • DiscHoops — combines elements of Ultimate Frisbee and basketball [New Sport]
