Sports at the 1932 Olympic Games

The 1932 Olympic games were held in Los Angeles, USA. There were 14 sports on the program, the same number of sports as in 1928, however one sport was added (shooting) and another dropped (football).

List of sports for the 1932 Olympics

  1. Aquatics (Swimming, Diving and Water Polo)
  2. Athletics
  3. Boxing
  4. Cycling (Road & Track)
  5. Equestrian
  6. Fencing
  7. Field Hockey
  8. Gymnastics (Artistic)
  9. Modern Pentathlon
  10. Rowing
  11. Sailing
  12. Shooting
  13. Weight Lifting
  14. Wrestling (Greco-Roman and Freestyle)
