Sporting Institutes, Academies and Elite Training Centers

Listed here is information on the many Sporting Institutes, Academies and Elite Sports Training Centers from around the world, dedicated to the excellence of sport, sorted by country.

List of countries with elite training centers or similar

Click on the country names for more information and links. 

  • Australia — Australian Institute of Sport, Victorian Institute of Sport, Western Australian Institute of Sport, South Australian Institute of Sport, Northern Territory Institute of Sport, New South Wales Institute of Sport, Queensland Academy of Sport, Tasmanian Institute of Sport, Hunter Academy of Sport, ACT Academy of Sport, Olympic Winter Institute of Australia, Western Sydney Academy of Sport, Western Region Academy of Sport.
  • Austria — Institute for Sports, Medicine, and Science / Olympia Centre.
  • Canada — PacificSport British Columbia, Canadian Sport Institute Pacific, Calgary, Saskatchewan, Manitoba, Canadian Sport Institute Ontario (CSIO), Montreal, Atlantic, Canadian Winter Sports Institute.
  • Cuba — National Institute of Sport, Physical Education and Recreation (INDER)
  • Ecuador — Centro Internacional de Alto Rendimiento en Altura (CIARA), Centro Ol?mpico de Alto Rendimiento (ECU).
  • England — English Institute of Sport, Derbyshire Institute of Sport, Durham Institute of Sport, National Centre for Sport & Exercise Medicine.
  • Finland — Sport Institute of Finland Olympic Training Centre, Vuokatti Sports Institute, Pajulahti Training Centre, KIHU research institute for Olympic Sports.
  • France — National Institute of Sport and Physical Education
  • Hong Kong — The Hong Kong Sports Institute
  • India — Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports, National Institute of Sports Science & Medicine, Inspire Institute of Sport (IIS)
  • Ireland — Irish Institute of Sport, Sports Institute Northern Ireland
  • Jamaica — Jamaican Institute of Sports (INSPORTS)
  • Japan — Japan Institute of Sports Sciences, ASICS Institute of Sport Science (ISS) - Kobe, Japan
  • Kenya — Kipkeino High Performance Training Centre, Eldoret, Kenya
  • Korea — Korean Institute of Sports Science
  • Malaysia — Malaysia National Sports Institute (ISN)
  • Mexico — La Loma High Performance Altitude Training Center
  • Morocco — The National Sports Center Moulay Rachid, Salé
  • Netherlands — Amsterdam Institute of Sport Science (AISS), Olympic Training Centre Papendal
  • New Zealand — High Performance Sport New Zealand
  • Norway — Norwegian Olympic Sport Centre (Olympiatoppen)
  • Peru — Instituto Peruano del Deporte (IPD)
  • Poland — Institute of Sport, Warsaw, Poland (Instytut Sportu w Warszawie)
  • Qatar — Qatar ASPIRE Academy
  • Romania — National Research Institute for Sport (Institutul National de Cercetare pentru Sport)
  • Scotland — Scottish Institute of Sport
  • Serbia — Institute for Sport of the Republic of Serbia
  • Singapore — Singapore Sports Institute, Singapore Sports School
  • Slovakia — Narodne Sportove Centrum, Slovakia
  • South Africa — The Sports Science Institute of South Africa, Eastern Cape Academy of Sport, HPC University of Pretoria
  • Spain — Centre d'Alt Rendiment (C.A.R.), Barcelona, Madrid and Sierra Nevada
  • Sri Lanka — National Institute of Sports Science
  • Taiwan — National Sports Training Center (NSTC)
  • USA — U.S. Olympic Training Centers (Colorado Springs, Colorado, Chula Vista, Califorina, Lake Placid, N.Y. and Marquette, Michigan, Native American Recreation & Sport Insititue, The National Institute for Fitness and Sport.
