Ballooning Demonstration in 1900

A ballooning event (aérostation) was an unofficial sport at 1900 Olympic Games in Paris. There were prizes in distance, duration, elevation, and target with and without stopping. In each of these events, there were three series: 1. Balloons of equal volume, handicapped in terms of ballast, 2. balloons of any volume, handicapped, 3. no handicap. The kite flying event was also part of the ballooning competition.
