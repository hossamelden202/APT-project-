Multi-Sports

Of the many different sports played around the world, there are some that are made up of combining two or more current sports or events to create a new sport, usually performed consecutively. See also Hybrid Sports.

Two Sports Combined

  • Biathle Ś consists of swimming and running as part of a single race. It is usually over a shorter distance than the similar Aquathlon.
  • Aquathlon Ś a continuous race involving swimming followed by running. It is usually over a shorter distance than the similar Biathle.
  • Aquabike Ś a shortened version of triathlon, with no running leg. Athletes swim followed by cycling.
  • Duathlon Ś similar to triathlon, running first, then cycling, then running again.
  • Swimrun Ś involves multiple swim and run stages, while carrying all equipment and with no clothing changes.
  • Biathlon Ś a combination of cross-country skiing and rifle shooting.
  • Summer Biathlon Ś a combined sport of roller skiing (or trail running) and rifle shooting.
  • Biathlon Orienteering Ś a combination of the navigation sport of orienteering with rifle shooting.
  • Laser Run Ś four legs of laser pistol shooting, each followed by an 800m run.
  • Ski Archery Ś involves skiing and shooting arrows at targets.
  • Chess boxing Ś a hybrid sport that combines the sport of boxing with games of chess in alternating rounds.

Three Sports Combined

  • Triathlon Ś a race consisting of three events, usually swimming, cycling, and running in consecutive order.
  • Nordic Combined Ś a Winter Olympics sport in which athletes compete in a combined event of Cross-Country Skiing and Ski Jumping.
  • Winter Triathlon Ś athletes compete in running, mountain biking and cross-country skiing.
  • Indoor Triathlon Ś swimming in an indoor pool, cycling on a stationary bike, and running on an indoor track or treadmill.
  • Cross Triathlon Ś a variation of the traditional triathlon, with a swim stage, mountain-biking stage and a trail-running stage.
  • Triathle Ś a shortened version of the Modern Pentathlon, athletes compete in shooting, swimming and running events as a part of a single race.
  • Paratriathlon Ś for disabled athletes, involving a 750 m swim, a 20 km bike with handcycles, bicycles or tandems with a guide, and a 5 km wheelchair or running race.

Four Sports Combined

  • Quadrathlon Ś an endurance sports event composed of the four individual disciplines of swimming, kayaking, cycling and running - also called quadriathlon.
  • Tetrathlon Ś composed of 4 out of the 5 disciplines of Modern Pentathlon: Swimming, Running, Shooting and either Fencing or Equestrian.
  • Racketlon Ś a combination sport, where players compete in the sports of table tennis, tennis, squash, and badminton.
  • Ironman Surflifesaving Ś combines four major aspects of surflifesaving into a single race: swimming, board paddling, ski paddling, and beach running.
  • Medley Swimming Relay Ś a swimming race in which teams of four race consecutively.

Five Sports Combined

  • Modern Pentathlon Ś an event in the Olympics that comprises five events: fencing, 200m freestyle swimming, show jumping (to be replaced by an obstacle run?), and a combined event of pistol shooting and 3200m cross-country run.
  • Ancient Pentathlon Ś the ancient Olympic pentathlon was an athletic competition which consisted of five events, running, javelin throw, discus throw, long jump, and wrestling.
  • Athletics Pentathlon Ś a no longer held event comprising long jump, javelin, 200-meter run, discus, and 1500-meter run.
  • Military Pentathlon Ś a competition involving a shooting phase, obstacle run, obstacle swimming, grenade throwing, and cross-country running.
  • Naval Pentathlon Ś comprises an obstacle race, life-saving swimming race, utility swimming race, seamanship race, and an amphibious cross-country race.
  • Winter Pentathlon Ś was a demonstration sport at the 1948 Winter Olympics, which involved a 10km cross-country ski race, pistol shooting, downhill skiing, fencing and a horse jumping equestrian course.

Many Sports Combined

  • Heptathlon Ś a women's track and field multi-event comprising 7 events.
  • Decathlon Ś a men's track and field event comprising 10 events.
  • Aeronautical Pentathlon Ś despite the name, the sport has 6 events: shooting, fencing, orienteering, basketball skills, obstacle course and swimming.
  • Adventure Racing Ś an event combining two or more endurance disciplines, such as Orienteering, Cross-Country Running, Mountain Biking, Paddling and Climbing. It is also called Expedition Racing.
  • Icosathlon Ś a double decathlon consisting of 20 events, including the 10 traditional decathlon events with 10 additional track and field events.
  • Tetradecathlon Ś a double heptathlon consisting of 14 events, including the 7 traditional heptathlon events with 7 additional track and field events.
