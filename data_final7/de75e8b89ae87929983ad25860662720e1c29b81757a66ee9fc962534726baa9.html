Top Sport Science Universities

Here are lists of the top sport science universities and other institutions. See also our list of sport science institutions around the world.

  • The top-ranked university Sport Science Schools and Departments for 2017, headed by Deakin University (Victoria Australia, followed by the UK's Loughborough University. Read more
  • A 2015 project by Japan's The University of Tsukuba ranked the Universities around the world to find the best in Sport Sciences. Loughborough University in the United Kingdom was found to be best overall, followed by the University of Calgary (Canada) and the University of Tsukuba (Japan). Read more
  • The Complete University Guide publishes each year the top UK university sport science courses. See the top-10 for 2017, 2016, 2015, 2014, 2013, 2012, 2011, 2010.
  • The UK's Telegraph found that the top UK Sports Science Courses, based on data of the percentage of students who found employment within six months of graduating, were from University of Edinburgh Next was Leeds Trinity University College and Sheffield Hallam. Read more
