List of International Sports Federations and Recognized Sports

Here is a list of international sports federations, which serve as a non-governmental governing body for the sport, and administers the sport at a world level. This list forms the foundation of the complete list of sports, which includes many more world sports than are listed here.

The role of the international sports federation includes regulating rules, promotion of the sport, developing players, and organizing championships. Some international sports federations may oversee multiple sports.

There are governing bodies which have become the first to achieve observer status with that are not yet officially recognised as sporting federations. In 2017, the Global Association of International Sports Federations (GAISF) gave 'observer status' to a few possible new official sports, such as armwrestling, dodgeball, footgolf, kettlebell lifting, poker, pole sports and table soccer.
