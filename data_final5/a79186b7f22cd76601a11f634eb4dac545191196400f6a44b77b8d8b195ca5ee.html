Major League Soccer Combine 2018

The 2018 MLS Player Combine was held at the Orlando City Stadium from January 11-17. An initial list of 60 NCAA Division I college players were invited.

Results

The table below lists the top 10 performances in each of the three tests. The standout athlete was Western Michigan's Brandon Bye, who led the way as the only player to finish in the top 10 for all three tests. Justin Fiddes' 3.84sec in the 30m sprint was the second best time we have on record, only behind the 3.81 by Khiry Shelton in 2015. See the all time best performances.
