English Super League (ESL)

English Super League (ESL) is the top-level professional rugby league club competition in the Northern hemisphere, with most teams coming from England. The competition started in 1996, though the national UK competition held before that time, the Rugby Football League Championship First Division, is considered a direct lineage of the competition.

ESL & League Championship Winners List

The first Championship game was in 1896, won by Manningham. In some years there was no Championship final, the team finishing on top of the league table was declared the winner. The club with the most Championship wins is St. Helens, with next best Leeds Rhinos and Wigan Warriors.

The 2024 Grand Final will be played between Wigan Warriors and Hull Kingston Rovers on October 12.
