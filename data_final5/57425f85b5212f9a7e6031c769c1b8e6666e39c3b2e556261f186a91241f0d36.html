FISU World University Games Host Cities

There has been a long history of multi-sport events for students, starting way back in 1905 when the first international university games were held in the USA. The major multi-sport international event for University students is the FISU World University Games (previously named Universiade).

Below are tables of all the known international student sporting events, organized by various groups, both summer and winter events.
