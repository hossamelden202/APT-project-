Winter Olympics Host City List

The Winter Olympic Games were first held in 1924, beginning a tradition of holding them a few months earlier and in a different city than the summer Olympic Games. Beginning in 1994, the winter Olympic Games were held in completely different years (two years apart) than the summer Games. The Winter Games were held just two years apart in 1992 and 1994 to achieve this.

Future Games

  • The next Winter Olympic Games, the 2026 Winter Olympics will be held in Italy.  
  • The French Alps will host 2030 Winter Games.
  • Salt Lake City-Utah will host the 2034 Winter Olympics (they also hosted the 2002 Winter Olympics).
  • Switzerland are in dialogue with the IOC for a bid for the 2038 Winter Olympics.

Host City Trivia

  • The Winter Games has been held in the USA four times (the most of any country): 1932, 1960, 1980 and 2002 (and will again in 2034). The US has also hosted the Summer Games four times (1904, 1932, 1984 and 1996) - and will again in 2028.
  • France has hosted the Winter Games three times (1924, 1968 and 1992 - and will again in 2030), and the summer games three times (1900, 1924, 2024).
