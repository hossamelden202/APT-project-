Hard Rock Stadium, Miami, Florida, USA

Hard Rock Stadium is a sporting stadium located in Miami, Florida, USA. The stadium was opened 1987. Hard Rock Stadium’s former names are Joe Robbie Stadium, Pro Player Stadium, Dolphin Stadium, and Sun Life Stadium. It currently has a capacity of 64,767.

aerial view of the Hard Rock Stadium in Miami

The stadium was used for the FIFA World Cup in 2026, and was the venue for the third place playoff.

Major Events Held at Hard Rock Stadium

Here are just some of the major events that have been held at the Hard Rock Stadium.

  • 1997 World Series
  • BCS National Championship Games (2001, 2005, 2009, and 2013)
  • 2003 World Series
  • 2009 World Baseball Classic
  • 2010 Pro Bowl
  • 2012 WrestleMania XXVIII
  • Orange Bowl annually
  • Super Bowls (XXIII, XXIX, XXXIII, XLI, XLIV, and LIV)
  • 2024 Copa America final
  • 2025 FIFA Club World Cup
  • 2026 FIFA World Cup

Hard Rock Stadium Trivia

  • It was the first multipurpose stadium ever built in the United States that was entirely privately financed
