Test Your Reaction Time

INSTRUCTIONS

  • This test measures the time taken for you to press the stop button after the background color changes.
  • You can select the color of the background will change to.
  • Click the start button, and the background will change within 20 seconds.
  • Click the stop button as quickly as possible after the background color changes.
  • Your time taken will be displayed in a pop up box.
  • Try it a few times (beware, it can get addictive!) and then enter your results, and see how you compared to others who have tried this previously.
  • Now you are ready to try the Reaction Test Timer
