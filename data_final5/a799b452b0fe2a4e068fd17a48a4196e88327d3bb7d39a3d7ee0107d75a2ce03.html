Proactive Membership Assistance Program

Life-changing events, such as job loss or natural disasters, can disrupt your day-to-day life and completely redefine what normalcy and stability mean to you and your family. This is why the American Chemical Society wants to ensure that you are aware of all the resources at your disposal to help you through tough times.

At ACS, we strive to enrich the value of your membership by offering support throughout your professional career in chemistry in times of success, and in times of difficulty. Some of these benefits are outlined below.

Dues Waivers

In certain circumstances, ACS will waive your membership dues. To be eligible you must be an ACS Member or Society Affiliate, and contact ACS Membership Services via phone or e-mail: service@acs.org, Toll-Free in the U.S.: 1-800-333-9511, International: +1-614-447-3776.

  • Geographic Hardship
    If you live in an area that is experiencing a natural disaster or conflict, ACS may waive your national dues for one year. ACS attempts to proactively notify its global community in known regions undergoing difficulty but if you have not heard from us and are dealing with extenuating circumstances, please contact Membership Services.  
  • Unemployment
    If you have become unemployed and have been an ACS Member or Society Affiliate with at least one year of paid dues, ACS may waive your national dues for one year.

Dues Discounts

ACS offers discounts on national dues to a variety of individuals based on certain qualifying categories. To determine eligibility and apply for these discounts please contact our Membership Services team. These categories include:

  • Recent graduates
  • National service (such as U.S. Armed Forces)
  • Disability
  • Family Care Provider
  • Select International regions

Awards & Grants

In addition to the financial relief provided by dues waivers or discounts, ACS offers a variety of awards, scholarships, and grants. You can also reach out to your Local Section and ACS Divisions for additional resources and opportunities. Many of these opportunities go unfilled each year so please make sure you apply!

Discover exciting awards and grant opportunities within the ACS database with just a click!

  • Explore all scholarships, awards and grants
  • Search Local Section scholarships, awards and grants
  • Search ACS Division scholarships, awards and grants

Job Loss and Unemployment

We understand that losing your job or being in a period of transition can be especially stressful. In addition to the unemployment dues waiver mentioned above, ACS offers extensive options for career assistance and professional development. Here is a list of some of the most useful resources for those seeking work or development.

Career Counseling

Arrange one-on-one appointments with a certified ACS Career Consultant.

Resume Review

Pick up tips for building a stronger resume.

LinkedIn Learning

Access thousands of expert-led courses to build your skill set.

We also encourage you to reach out to your Local Section and ACS Divisions for any additional assistance programs or direct opportunities that these networks might be able to provide.

If you have extenuating circumstances that fall outside of these pre-established programs, please contact membership services to discuss your situation.
