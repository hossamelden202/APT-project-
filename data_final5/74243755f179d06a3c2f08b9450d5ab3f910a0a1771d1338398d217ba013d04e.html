2025 FIFA Club World Cup: USA

The FIFA Club World Cup is an international men's association football competition involving club teams from around the world. The 2025 FIFA Club World Cup will be held from 15 June to 13 July, 2025, in the United States.

The 2025 tournament is the first with a revamped format. The number of participating teams has increased from 7 in 2023 to 32 teams in 2025, and from then the tournament will be held every four years instead of annually.

Teams

The 32 teams will be divided into eight groups of four teams with the top two teams in each group qualifying to the knockout stage. Manchester City are the defending champions (see all winners).

Venues

The tournament will be held at twelve venues in eleven cities.

  1. Lincoln Financial Field in Philadelphia
  2. Audi Field in Washington D.C.
  3. Lumen Field in Seattle
  4. Rose Bowl in Pasadena
  5. TQL Stadium in Cincinnati
  6. Bank of America Stadium in Charlotte
  7. Mercedes-Benz Stadium in Atlanta
  8. Hard Rock Stadium in Miami Gardens
  9. Geodis Park in Nashville
  10. Camping World Stadium in Orlando
  11. Inter&Co Stadium in Orlando
  12. MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey

The final will be held at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey.
