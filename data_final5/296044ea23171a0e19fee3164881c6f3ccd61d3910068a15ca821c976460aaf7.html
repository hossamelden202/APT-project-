Paralympics Medal Tables

We have listed the top medal tally nations from every Summer Paralympic Games: 1960, 1964, 1968, 1972, 1976, 1980, 1984, 1988, 1992, 1996, 2000, 2004, 2008, 2012, 2016, 2021 and 2024. On an individual level, we have also listed the athletes with the most total Paralympic medals.

Which country has topped the medal tally?

China has dominated the gold medal table since Athens 2004, with Britain often in second place and the United States, Russia and Ukraine battling it out for third.
