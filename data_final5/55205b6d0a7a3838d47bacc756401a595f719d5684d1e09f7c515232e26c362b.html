Paralympic Games Participating Countries

At the first Paralympic Games, held in 1960 in Rome, 209 athletes from 18 countries countries participated. At the Tokyo Paralympics in 2021, there were over 4000 athletes from 135 countries represented, including a team of refugees. Twelve countries have been represented at all Summer Paralympic Games: Argentina, Australia, Austria, Belgium, France, Great Britain, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Netherlands, Switzerland, and United States.

Paris 2024 Participating Countries

179 countries are expected to participate in the Paris 2024 Paralympic, with the following countries to make their Paralympic debuts: American Samoa, Chad, Equatorial Guinea, Eswatini, Guam, Kiribati, Kosovo, Palau, South Sudan and Tuvalu.

The Countries That Participated at the 2021 Tokyo Paralympics
