2024 World Sporting Event Calendar

Here is a list of the major sporting events for the year 2024. Not every sporting event can be listed here, though we have tried to include all the big sporting events of the major sports, mostly the international competitions, that are known so far. See our list of top-10 major events of 2024 you shouldn't miss.

Major events for this 2024 calendar is the Paris Olympic Games, Euro 2024, America's Cup Sailing, African Cup of Nations, Copa América, ICC World T20 (men), African Games, as well as the usual annual events.
