2017 Most Popular Athletes - ESPN World Fame 100

This is the second year that ESPN have been calculating the world's most popular athletes, using a ranking based on a proprietary formula which factors online searches, endorsements and social media followers. The most famous athletes this year was the same as in 2016, footballer Cristiano Ronaldo. We have also listed the top-ranked athlete from each sport and from each country that was on the list.
