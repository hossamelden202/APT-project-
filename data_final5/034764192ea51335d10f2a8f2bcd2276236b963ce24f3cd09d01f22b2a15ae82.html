Why Participate in a Chapter

ACS International Chapters provide professional and personal connections and networking, with the backing of a global organization, its vast resources and a global member community of over 200,000. 

As an ACS Member, advance your career and chemistry by becoming a member or leader of an ACS International Chapter in your region.  

Be empowered to:

  • Network and make valuable connections
  • Build leadership skills
  • Enhance your professional development
  • Engage in your local chemistry community
  • Increase your access to ACS funding and awards
