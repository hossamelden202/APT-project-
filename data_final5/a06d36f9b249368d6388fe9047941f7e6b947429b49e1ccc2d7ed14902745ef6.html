The World's Highest Paid Athletes List 2023

There are still plenty of athletes earning big bucks. The top earner for 2023 was one of the biggest names in football, Cristiano Ronaldo, with earnings totaling 136 million US dollars. Lionel Messi, who was top of the list last year, was in second place. The only woman on the list was Serena WIlliams who was 49th on the list with $45.3M in earnings.

See also the Forbes top-earning lists from other years.
