List of Venues for the 2024 Paris Olympic Games

The complete list of venues to be used at the 2024 Paris Summer Olympic Games, highighting amazing sites around Paris, including the world-famous Champ de Mars, Palace of Versailles and Eiffel Tower, to the stunning beauty of Teahupo’o in Tahiti.

  • Aquatics Centre - Artistic swimming, Diving
  • Bercy Arena - Artistic gymnastics, Men's and women's Basketball
  • Bordeaux Stadium - Football/Soccer
  • Champ de Mars Arena - Judo, Wrestling
  • Chateauroux Shooting Centre - Shooting
  • Eiffel Tower Stadium - Beach volleyball
  • Elancourt Hill - Mountain biking
  • Esplanade des Invalides - Marathon finish
  • Geoffroy-Guichard Stadium - Football/Soccer
  • Golf National - Golf
  • Grand Palais - Fencing, Taekwondo
  • Hôtel de Ville - Marathon start
  • Le Bourget Sport Climbing Venue - Sport climbing
  • Les Invalides - Archery, Start of the cycling road time trials
  • Lyon Stadium - Football/Soccer
  • Marseille Marina - Sailing
  • Marseille Stadium - Football/Soccer
  • Nantes Stadium - Football/Soccer
  • National Velodrome - track cycling
  • Nice Stadium - Football/Soccer
  • Palace of Versailles - Equestrian, Modern pentathlon
  • Parc des Princes - Football/Soccer
  • Parc Urbain la Concorde - 3x3 Basketball, Breaking, BMX freestyle, Skateboarding
  • Paris La Défense Arena - Swimming
  • Pierre Mauroy Stadium - Handball
  • Pont Alexandre III - finish of the cycling road time trials, Marathon swimming, Triathlon
  • Porte de La Chapelle Arena - Badminton, Rhythmic gymnastics
  • Roland Garros - Boxing, Tennis
  • Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines BMX Stadium (inside the National Velodrome) - BMX racing
  • South Paris Arena - Table tennis, Volleyball, Weightlifting
  • Stade de France - Athletics (Track and field), Rugby
  • Tahiti Teahupo’o - Surfing
  • Trocadéro - Race walking, men’s and women’s cycling road races
  • Vaires-sur-Marne Nautical Stadium - Canoe, Rowing
  • Yves du Manoir Stadium - Field hockey
