US Open Venues

The venue of the US Open Golf Championships changes each year. Most frequent venue is the Oakmont Country Club with 9 opens: in 1927, 1935, 1953, 1962, 1973, 1983, 1994, 2007, and 2016 (also to be held there in 2025).
