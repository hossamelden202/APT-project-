Tee Off in Style: The Best Golf Courses in Tenerife

Tenerife, the largest of the Canary Islands, is one of the most sought-after destinations by golf enthusiasts. Its mild climate throughout the year and spectacular landscapes make it an ideal place for those seeking an exceptional playing experience. With courses that wind along the coast, routes set amidst subtropical vegetation, and layouts offering panoramic views of Teide, Tenerife offers a range of options for every level of play.

golf course in Tererife

The island is home to some of the best golf courses in Europe, designed by internationally renowned architects and equipped with top-notch facilities. Some are chosen for international tournaments, others for their exclusive design and luxurious experiences.

For those looking to discover the most exclusive courses, we present an overview of the best golf courses in Tenerife, selected for their technical quality and scenic appeal, truly a benchmark for enthusiasts in the field.

Golf los Palos: An Exclusive Golf Experience

Golf los Palos, located in the southern part of the island, is one of the most appreciated courses for the quality of its layout and attention to detail. Designed to accommodate both professionals and players seeking a relaxing experience, it offers an impeccable environment where every element is crafted to ensure maximum comfort.

The course unfolds on a flat terrain, ideal for refining technique and perfecting short game skills. The well-maintained fairways, artificial lakes, and strategically placed obstacles make it a challenging course for golfers of all levels. Beyond the quality of the layout, Golf los Palos stands out as one of the best options for players of every ability. The elegant clubhouse with its gourmet restaurant, private lessons with certified instructors, and dedicated training facilities make it an ideal choice for both experienced golfers and those looking to improve their skills in a professional setting.

The facility is also a reference point for high-level golf events, hosting competitions that attract both professional golfers and amateurs in search of a top-tier experience. It has been repeatedly chosen for official events thanks to the quality of its facilities and impeccable organization. Throughout the year, it hosts prestigious tournaments that attract players from all over Europe, establishing itself as one of the preferred destinations for those who wish to combine sport and luxury.

The Best Golf Courses in Tenerife

In addition to Golf los Palos, the island offers a selection of renowned courses noted for the quality of their facilities and the charm of their surroundings. Among the most prestigious are:

  • Abama Golf Resort: Designed by Dave Thomas, it is one of Tenerife's most exclusive courses. Every hole has been designed to harmoniously integrate with the landscape, offering technical challenges and spectacular ocean views. The course is accompanied by a luxury resort with a spa and Michelin-starred restaurants, combining the sporting experience with a high-end stay.
  • Buenavista Golf: Located in the northwest, it stands out for its scenic position on cliffs, with the sea serving as a backdrop for many of its holes. The design, signed by Severiano Ballesteros, makes use of the natural shape of the land, with well-placed obstacles and fairways that require strategic precision.
  • Amarilla Golf & Country Club: A course that blends sport and scenery, with some holes directly overlooking the ocean. The combination of constant winds and natural obstacles makes the game exciting and technical, perfect for those seeking a complete challenge.

Distinctive Features of the Courses

Each course in Tenerife has distinctive features that enhance its value. Abama Golf Resort stands out for its sustainability and the use of advanced technologies in maintaining the greens. Buenavista Golf has received accolades for the quality of its design and the sustainability of the course, distinguishing itself as one of the most environmentally respectful facilities in Tenerife. Golf los Palos is appreciated for the quality of its training facilities and its meticulous attention to detail in event organization.

Exclusive Tournaments and Events

Thanks to its variety of courses and top-notch facilities, golf in Tenerife represents a unique opportunity for those who wish to play in a high-level setting. Every year, the island hosts international competitions that attract both professional golfers and enthusiasts in search of new challenges. Golf los Palos is one of the venues that hosts some of the most prestigious events, ensuring impeccable organization and an exclusive atmosphere.

One of the most anticipated events is the Open de Tenerife, a competition that gathers talents from all over Europe. During the event, the best golfers challenge each other on highly technical courses, providing a top-level sporting spectacle.

Amateur players can also participate in open tournaments, sign up for local competitions, or attend international events as spectators. Many tournaments offer registration packages and networking opportunities with instructors and industry professionals. Numerous events include coaching sessions with certified instructors, making the experience even more comprehensive and educational.

Tenerife continues to establish itself as one of the most internationally appreciated golf destinations, offering top-level facilities and prestigious tournaments that make it a benchmark for enthusiasts of the sport. Amid challenging courses and panoramic views, golf becomes an integral part of the experience for those visiting the island.
