Which of these 8 sports should be included into the Olympics?

Here are the archived results from a poll that ran on this site in 2013. Wrestling was dropped from the core sports list in early 2013, and joined seven other sports on a shortlist for inclusion: roller sports, karate, softball & baseball (joint bid), squash, sport climbing, wakeboard and wushu. The poll asked which sport you thought should be the next to get the nod? To have your say on the next sport to be included in the Olympic program, see the similar current poll about sports to be added to the Olympics.

Which of these sports should be included into the Olympics?

Based on this poll of 1291 people, karate was the clearly most popular sport to be included next into the Olympic program - but it did not make the shortlist of Baseball-softball, squash and wrestling. In September 2013 wrestling was announced as the sport to be put on the program, meaning that it was never off it! See the latest poll on which sports should be added to the Olympics.

A similar online poll

Which sport do you think the IOC should vote to include on the Olympic program for 2020? This poll was included on the www.insidethegames.biz website in May 2013, and the results are shown here. There are many more votes here than in our poll above, and the results were a little different. Ultimately wrestling was the sport to be put on the program.

Wrestling - 40.1% (110,805 votes)
Squash - 33.9% (93,667 votes)
Karate - 17.9% (49,462 votes)
Baseball-softball - 3.2% (8,828 votes)
Sport Climbing - 2.2% (6,118 votes)
Roller Sports - 1.9% (5,212 votes)
Wushu - 0.5% (1,261 votes)
Wakeboarding - 0.3% (742 votes)

Total votes: 276,095
