2024 Paralympics Medal Tally

Here is the current top-ranked countries based on medals won at the Paris 2024 Paralympics. As per tradition, the ranking order is based first on the number of gold medals, then silver and bronze. See more about the Paris Paralympic Games in 2024.

The leading country on the 2024 Paris medal table was China, as they have done so since 2004. There were 79 countries that won at least one medal, and 65 countries that won at least one gold medal.

Mauritius, Nepal, and the Refugee Paralympic Team won their first-ever Paralympic medals.
