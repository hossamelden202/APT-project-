NFL Combine Testing Results for 2017

Here are the top 10 results for each event at the 2017 National Invitational Camp (NIC), more commonly known as the NFL Scouting Combine, which was held from February 28 to March 6, 2017 at the Lucas Oil Stadium in Indianapolis.

The 330 top prospects were invited to participate at the combine in Indianapolis, Indiana. The data below is compiled from the top performers list published on NFL.com. See also the listings of top results from every year of the NFL Combine. For a description of each of these tests, see the page About the NFL Combine.
