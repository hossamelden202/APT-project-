2017 NBA Draft Combine

A series of 'Athleticism Tests' are performed at the NBA pre-draft camp combine held annually. See a description of the tests, and the best results for each year since 2000. The combine was held on May 9-14 at the Quest Center in Chicago, Ill., leading up to the NBA Draft the following month.

Below are the top 10 results for the major tests conducted at the 2017 NBA Combine. See the list of the 2017 participants. Figures are based on official NBA documents published online. No official results are available for the bench press test.
