Women's Cricket ODI World Cup Results

Women's WC Home | Results | History | Trivia

Here is a summary of the results of each Women's Cricket ODI World Cup tournament. Australia has appeared in nine of the telve finals, and has won the most tournaments, winning seven, with England next with four wins. The first two tournaments were contested in a round robin format, so there was no final match. See also the results of the Women's T20 World Cup.
