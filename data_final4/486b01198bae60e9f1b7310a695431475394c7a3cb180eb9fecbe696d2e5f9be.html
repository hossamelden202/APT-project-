A to Z of Unusual Sports

Here is a list of 26 weird and unusual sports, from A to Z. See our whole list of unusual sports.

A to Z of Unusual Sports

Apple Race Ś racing apples down the river, where else but on the apple isle of Tasmania, Australia.

Beard & Moustache Championships Ś who has the best facial hair.

Cheese Rolling Ś get yourself and the cheese to the bottom of a hill as fast as you can.

Dog Dancing Ś choreographed dog and owners routines.

Egg Rolling Ś an Easter tradition even played at the White House every year.

Finger Jousting Ś try to poke your opponent without being poked yourself.

Gurning Contest Ś pull a face and win.

Hairiest Back Ś who has the most hair on their back.

Irish Road Bowling Ś look out cars, bowling along the roads of Ireland.

Japanese Botaoshi Ś a tough and crazy game like capture the flag played in Japan.

Kissing Competitions Ś unusual kissing events such as underwater and basketball kissing.

Land Diving Ś dry land bungy jumping.

Moonrock Throwing Ś not just any rock throwing, but moonrocks (not from the moon)

Nude Beach Olympics Ś held at the nudist section of Baker Beach San Francisco

Orange Races Ś racing oranges down a road.

Pooh Sticks Championship Ś not so sticky game played by Pooh Bear.

Quidditch Ś the sport created for the Harry Potter books.

Running of the Nudes Ś the naked alternative to the Pamplona bull run.

Shin Kicking Ś kick them in the shins until they fall down.

Two-Handed Tennis Ś an unusual variation of the popular game.

Uppies and Downies Ś a version of Medieval Football with about 1000 players on each team who attempt to get the ball to the opposing team's goal.

Volata Ś a type of football created by fascist Italy.

Worm Charming Ś bring as many worms to the surface as you can.

X-treme Ironing Ś Who said ironing is difficult? When you take your iron and board to extreme places it cannot get any harder.

Yabbie Races Ś slow races for the Australian freshwater crustacean

Zorb Ball Ś rolling down the hill, with padding!.
