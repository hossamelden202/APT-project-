The FA Cup Winners List

The FA Cup has witnessed countless exhilarating moments, heart-stopping upsets, and heroic performances on the hallowed grounds of English football. From the early days of its inception in 1872 to the present day, here is a list of all the winners of the FA Cup. See also the women's FA Cup Winners.

With their victory in 2024, Manchester United are now 13-time winners of the FA Cup, only one win behind Arsenal who has the most FA Cup titles by a single club.

The 2025 FA Cup Final will be held on May 17 at Wembley Stadium, London.
