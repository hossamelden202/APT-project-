2015 Sport Science Conferences

Conferences in the area of sport science are held regularly around the world. Here I have tried to list the major events that I could find for the year 2015.

The information about these conferences have been submitted by conference organizers, visitors to this site and collected from various sources online. Let me know if you think something is missing or needs updating. Please go to the official websites about each conference for accurate and up to date details.
