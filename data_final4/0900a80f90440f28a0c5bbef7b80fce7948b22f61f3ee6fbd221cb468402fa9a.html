2009 Ballon d'Or Results

The Ballon d'Or award is an annual football award for the best international player from the previous year.

Below are the nominees in the final order after voting for the 2009 award. The winner was Argentinian Lionel Messi, his first of many Ballon d'Or awards. See the list of all Ballon d'Or winners.
