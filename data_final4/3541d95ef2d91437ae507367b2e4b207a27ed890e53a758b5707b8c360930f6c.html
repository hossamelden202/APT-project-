2017 World Sporting Event Calendar

Here is a list of the major sporting events for the year 2017. Not every sporting event can be listed here, though we have tried to include all the big sporting events of the major sports, mostly the international competitions.

Major events for this year included the World Baseball Classic, America's Cup yachting event, the IAAF World Championships, Summer and Winter Universiade as well as the usual annual events. See a summary of the world of sport for 2017 including results.

See the current Major World Sporting Events Calendar.

Let me know if you think something is missing or needs updating (note, we mostly list major international events).
