The Greatest Tug of War Athlete at the Olympic Games

Who is the greatest Tug of War competitor of all time based on Olympic Games performances? We have used a weighted total medal count of each male Tug-of-War athlete from all modern Olympic Games to rank their overall performance, using data from all Olympics that the sport was included.

The best all-time performing Tug of War athletes at the Olympic Games were Frederick Humphreys, Edwin Mills and John James Shepherd with two gold medals and one silver. See the table below to see how other athletes ranked.

See also the best athlete from each sport, and the best overall.
