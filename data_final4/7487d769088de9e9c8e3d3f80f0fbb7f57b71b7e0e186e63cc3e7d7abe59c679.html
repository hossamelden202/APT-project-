Home of Pickleball

Pickleball is a paddle sport that combines elements of tennis, badminton, and table tennis, which is becoming increasingly popular. It is typically played in doubles, although singles play is also an option. The objective of the game is to hit a perforated plastic ball over a net and into the opponent's court in a way that they are unable to return the ball.
