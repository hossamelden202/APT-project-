Tour de France Events

Here is some information about a few of the Tour de France events, starting from the first event held in 1903. Due to war, the tour was not held between 1915-1918 and between 1940-1946, and the 2020 event was postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic, but fortunately still held later in the year.
