Olympic Games Gymnastics All-Around Winners (Women)

The All-Around competition for women's gymnastics is a true test of versatility and skill, requires athletes to excel across multiple apparatuses, showcasing their strength, agility, and precision. All Around refers to the sum of the scores achieved on each of four events: vault, uneven bars, balance beam, and the floor exercise.
