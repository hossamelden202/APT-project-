The World's Highest Paid Athletes in 1996

Here is the top 10 list of the highest earning sports people from 1996. It is a little old, the list would be much different today. A prominent sports person from future lists, Tiger Woods, placed 26th on the extended list. The German Michael Schumacher was the only non US athlete to be in the top 10.

All estimates (US dollars) are Forbes estimates for the 1996 calendar year. Totals include salaries, bonuses, prize money, endorsements and appearance fees. See also the top earning lists from other years.
