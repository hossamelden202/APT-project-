Discontinued Olympic Track & Field Events

There are many the Track and Field events that were one featured at the Olympic Games but are no longer part of the program.

Discontinued Events for Men

  • 60 Meters 1900-1904
  • 200 Meters Hurdles 1900-1904
  • 1600 Meters Medley Relay (200m + 200m + 400m + 800m) 1908
  • Steeplechase 1900 (2500m & 4000m), 1904 (2590m), 1908 (3200m)
  • 5 Miles 1906, 1908
  • Team Race 1900 (5000m), 1904 (4 mile), 1908 (3 mile), 1912 (3000m), 1920 (3000m), 1924 (3000m)
  • Cross Country (individual & team) 1912-1924
  • Shot Put, Both Hands (aggregate) 1912
  • Javelin, Both Hands (aggregate) 1912
  • Javelin, Free-Style 1906, 1908
  • Discuss, Both Hands (aggregate) 1912
  • Stone Throw (6.4 kg) 1906
  • Ancient (Greek-Style) Discus 1906, 1908
  • 56 lb. Weight Throw 1904, 1920
  • Standing Long Jump 1900-1912
  • Standing High Jump 1900-1912
  • Standing Triple Jump 1900, 1904
  • Walk: 1500m (1906), 3000m (1906, 1920), 3500m (1908), 10,000m (1912, 1920, 1924, 1948, 1952), 10-Mile (1908)
  • Triathlon 1904 (long jump, shot put, 100-yard run)
  • Pentathlon 1906 (standing long jump, ancient discus, 192-meter run, javelin, Greco-Roman Wrestling)
  • Pentathlon 1912-1924 (long jump, javelin, 200-meter run, discus, 1500-meter run)
  • Pentathlon (women) 1964 to 1980.
  • All-Around (the original decathlon) 1904 (100 yards, shot put, high jump, 880 yards walk, hammer throw, pole vault, 120-yard hurdles, 56 lb. weight throw, long jump, one mile run)
  • Tug-of-War 1900-1920

Discontinued Events for Women

  • 80 Meters Hurdles 1932-1968 (replaced by 100 meters in 1972)
  • 3000 Meters 1984-1992 (replaced by 5000 meters in 1996)
  • Walk: 10km Road Walk 1992-1996 (replaced by the 20 km event in 2000)
  • Pentathlon 1964-1980 (100 meters hurdles [80 meters 1964-1968], shot put, high jump, long jump, 200-meter run). Replaced by the Heptathlon in 1984.
