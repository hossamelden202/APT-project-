Major World Sporting Events

For the sports fan, the major sporting events that only come around once or year or every few years are highly anticipated, and worth waiting for. There are major events held for each of the hundreds of major sports around the world. The top three world sporting events are apparently the Olympic Games, FIFA World Cup and the Rugby World Cup, though there are many more major sporting events.
