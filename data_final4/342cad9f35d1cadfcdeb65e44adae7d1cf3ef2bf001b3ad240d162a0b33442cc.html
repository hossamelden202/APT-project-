2023 NBA Draft Combine Results

A series of 'Athleticism Tests' are performed at the NBA pre-draft camp combine held annually. The 2023 combine was held from May 15-21, at Wintrust Arena and the Marriott Marquis in Chicago. See the list of the 2023 participants.

Below are the top-10 results for the major tests conducted. Figures are based on official NBA documents published online. See a description of the tests, and the best results for each year since 2000.
