Pole Vault World Records

The pole vault is one of the athletics (track and field) jumping events, requiring a competitor to leap over a bar using a long pole.

The current pole vault world record (as of March 2025) for men is held by US-born Swedish vaulter Armand Duplantis set in February 2025 with a vault of 6.27 m (20 ft 7 in). For women, the record is held by Russian Yelena Isinbayeva, set in 2009, with a leap of 5.06 m (16 ft 7 in). The world record in pole vault can be set either indoor or outdoors (it has not always been that case).
