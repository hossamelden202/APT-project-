SoFi Stadium, Los Angeles, California, USA

SoFi Stadium is a sporting stadium located in Los Angeles, California, USA. It is located in Hollywood Park, on the site of the former Hollywood Park racetrack. The stadium was built in 2020 with a capacity of 70,240, though this is expandable up to 100,240 for major events such as the Super Bowl and Olympic Games.

The stadium will be used for the FIFA World Cup in 2026 and the Olympic Games in 2028. The stadium is also home for the NFL teams Los Angeles Rams and Los Angeles Chargers.

SoFi Stadium, Los Angeles, California (image source: Google Maps, 2022)

Major Events Held at SoFi Stadium

Here are just some of the major events that have been held at SoFi Stadium, or will be in the future:

  • 2021+ LA Bowl
  • 2022 Super Bowl LVI
  • 2026 FIFA World Cup
  • 2027 Super Bowl LXI
  • 2028 Summer Olympic Games

SoFi Stadium Trivia

  • The stadium was originally awarded Super Bowl LV (2021)
