Winners of the Most Tennis Grand Slam Titles

Here is a list of the players with the most grand slam titles, that is players who have won the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon or US Open. A Tennis Grand Slam is achieved by winning all four major tennis tournaments in a single calendar year. Only five players have achieved that feat.

Serena Williams
