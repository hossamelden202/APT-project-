2014 World Sporting Event Calendar

Here is a list of the major sporting events for the year 2014. Not every sporting event was listed, though we tried to include all the big sporting events of the major sports, mostly the international competitions, that are known.

Major events for this year included the Winter Olympics in Sochi, the Commonwealth Games in Glasgow, and the Asian Games in Incheon, as well as the usual annual events. See our summary of the Year in Sport: 2014
