The Challenges of migration

GENEVA CHALLENGE 2025

Every year, students around the world are invited to provide contributions that are both theoretically grounded and offer pragmatic solutions to a specific relevant international development problem stemming from an interdisciplinary collaboration between three to five enrolled master students from anywhere in the world. In 2025, the Development Goals International Contest for Graduate Students aims to present innovative and pragmatic solutions to address the challenges of migration.
