Tobacco Free initiative

Implement warnings on tobacco packs

This page will be updated. Please check back soon for the latest information.

Article 11 of the WHO Framework Convention on Tobacco Control (WHO FCTC) requires Parties to the Convention to implement large, rotating health warnings on all tobacco products packaging and labelling.

This online resource provides copyright/royalty-free access to 58 pictorial health warnings to be used or adapted by countries and Parties to the WHO FCTC for the sole purpose of warning the public about the dangers of tobacco use through graphic health warnings on tobacco and nicotine product packs.

The selection and use of any of these images is exclusively a decision made by Parties, without WHO EMRO bearing any form of responsibility.
