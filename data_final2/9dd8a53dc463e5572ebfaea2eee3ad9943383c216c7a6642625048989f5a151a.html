Soccer Major Events

There are many events in the international calendar for football (soccer), and also significant events in national and regional football competitions. The major tournament is the FIFA World Cup, but there are many more, for men and women.

2010 FIFA World Cup match

Major Football Events - held every 4 years

  • FIFA World Cup — the biggest single sport international sporting event.
  • FIFA Women's World Cup — the most important competition in international football for women. Held a year after the men's version.
  • Olympic Games Football — mens and women's tournament. The men's teams are restricted to players under-23 (+ three over-age players), and no age restrictions for the women's teams.
  • UEFA Euro — competition between the senior men’s national teams of members of the Union of European Football Association (UEFA), to determine the continental champion. There is also a women's tournament.
  • Asian Football Cup — Asian Football Confederation (AFC) competition, the winner plays in the FIFA Confederations Cup.
  • Copa América — a men's football tournament between national teams from the South American Football Confederation.
  • Finalissima — an intercontinental football super cup, contested by the European and South American Champions.
  • FIFA Confederations Cup — an international men's football tournament with eight national teams, the holders of each of the FIFA confederation championships together with the FIFA World Cup holder and the host nation (NO LONGER HELD)

Major Football Events - Biennial

  • African Cup of Nations — main international association football (soccer) competition in Africa, held every two years.
  • UEFA Nations League — a newly-founded competition with 55 European national teams competing in four leagues.

Major Football Events - Annual

  • FIFA Club World Cup — an international men's association football competition involving seven teams; the champion club team from each confederation along with the host nation's national champions.
  • FIFA Intercontinental Cup — an annual international knockout men's association football competition, first held in 2024, featuring the club champions of the six confederations of FIFA.
  • UEFA Champions League — annual football cup competition for the top football clubs in Europe.
  • UEFA Europa League — annual football cup competition for the top football clubs in Europe (2nd tier).
  • UEFA Super Cup — annual football match between the winners of the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA Europa League.
  • FA Cup Final — the last match of the Football Association Challenge Cup between teams belonging to the Football Association in England.
  • European Super League — The Super League is a new annual European competition between 20 top clubs comprised of 15 founders and five annual qualifiers. (did not go ahead)
