2022 NHL Entry Draft Combine Results

The world's top ice hockey prospects are invited to the annual NHL pre-draft combine, with over 100 draft-eligible players from North American and Europe being tested (see the list of 2022 combine invitees). The combine assessment involves interviews, medical screenings, and fitness tests over a four-day period.

After a two-year hiatus due to the coronavirus pandemic, the combine returned to Buffalo. The 2022 NHL Scouting Combine was held from May 30- June 4 at KeyBank Center and LECOM Harborcenter in Buffalo, N.Y. This was the 27th time the annual NHL Draft Combine had been held.

Below are the top 10 results of the fitness assessments for the 2022 NHL Scouting Combine (see details of the testing procedures). See also a summary of previous NHL Entry Draft Combine Results.
