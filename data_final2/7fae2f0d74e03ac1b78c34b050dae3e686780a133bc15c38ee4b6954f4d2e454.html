2020 NBA Draft Combine Invitees

Here is the original list of invited participants at the 2020 pre-draft NBA Combine. As the combine did not go ahead as planned, possibly not all of these players were tested (we have results data from only 45 players). Due to the coronavirus pandemic, there was no single combine event. Interviews were done online and the testing part of the combine was conducted at the NBA team facility closest to their home. See the 2020 Combine Results.
