With the huge amount of information on different training methods out there, there is no consensus on which is the right way to go.

Most training programs will work to some degree to improve your fitness. On a simple level, if you perform a training program in which there is an increased load placed on the body, the body will adapt to the higher load, and you will be become stronger, faster and fitter (see fitness principles).

However, a program tailored specifically for you and your needs can maximize and accelerate the benefits to your health and fitness. See these articles on a variety of fitness training programs, to help you decide what is best for you, and help you achieve your fitness goals quickly.
