Top-10 Voted Most Popular Sports

Here is a list of the top 10 most popular sports, as listed voted for by visitors to the mostpopularsports.net website. Football (Soccer) is the most popular sport here based on the votes as at June 2020 (this order may change as the voting is ongoing). Compare this list to another online poll, which has soccer as the second most popular sport, behind basketball.

Top-10 According to Visitors to mostpopularsports.net

  1. Football (Soccer) 34.29%
  2. Basketball 17.14%
  3. Cricket 16.19%
  4. Tennis 7.62%
  5. Baseball 4.76%
  6. Volleyball 4.76%
  7. Rugby 1.9%
  8. American Football 1.9%
  9. Golf 0.95%
  10. Badminton 0.95%

original source: www.mostpopularsports.net
