Game Schedule for Russia 2018

Home | Schedule | Venues | Results | Goal Scorers

As the host nation, Russia qualified automatically. The top seeds of the qualifying teams are Germany, Brazil, Portugal, Argentina, Belgium, Poland, France.

Full Schedule of Matches

The 32 qualifying countries will be divided into eight groups of four, labelled from A to H. Each side will then play three games with those in their group, with the top two advancing to the second round.
