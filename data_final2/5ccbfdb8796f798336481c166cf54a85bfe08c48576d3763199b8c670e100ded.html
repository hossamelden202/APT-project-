NFL Combine Testing Results for 2014

Here are the top 10 results for each event at the 2014 National Invitational Camp (NIC), more commonly known as the NFL Scouting Combine, which was held from Feb. 19-25, 2014 at the Lucas Oil Stadium in Indianapolis. These results are based on official results published by nfl.com.

The 335 top prospects were invited to participate at the combine in Indianapolis, Indiana. The data below is compiled from the top performers list published on NFL.com. See also the listings of top results from every year of the NFL Combine. For a description of each of these tests, see the page About the NFL Combine.
