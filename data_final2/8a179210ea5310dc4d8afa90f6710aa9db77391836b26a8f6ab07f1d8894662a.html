Who is the All Time Greatest Hockey Player?

We are compiling a list of the greatest athletes from the sport of field hockey, which will then be voted on and the winner placed on the shortlist of the all time greatest athlete ever (all sports). The first stage is to get nominations for field hockey before the vote is started. See the current list below of top athletes, compiled using other published lists and visitor feedback. The list includes both male and female players. If you believe that there is someone missing from this list, please send a message, including any relevant details about the person.

Is it Major Dhyan Chand?

Major Dhyan Chand may well be the greatest hockey players ever. I don't understand why so many comments are asking for him to be excluded from this list - if he really is the greatest, then he needs to be on a list of greatest players ever, and possibly on the top of this list! The greatest recent player is Australian Jamie Dwyer, winner of the FIH international player of the year five times.
