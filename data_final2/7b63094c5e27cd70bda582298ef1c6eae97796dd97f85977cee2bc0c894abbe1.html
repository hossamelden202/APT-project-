2019 NBA Draft Combine

A series of 'Athleticism Tests' are performed at the NBA pre-draft camp combine held annually. See a description of the tests, and the best results for each year since 2000. The combine was held at Quest Multisport in Chicago on Thursday, May 16 and Friday, May 17, leading up to the NBA Draft on June 20.

Below are the top 10 results for the major tests conducted at the 2019 NBA Combine. See the list of the 2019 participants. Figures are based on official NBA documents published online. No official results are available for the bench press test.

Tallest Players (measured without shoes)

The tallest player was UCF center Tacko Fall, who measured off the charts at 7'5.25" without shoes as well as a 8'2.25" wingspan. The shortest player measured was Carsen Edwards at 5' 10.75''.
