Ballon d'Or Top Winners

The Ballon d'Or award for the best male footballer will be presented for the 68th time in 2024. There have been ten players who have won the award more than once, the standout of these is Lionel Messi. He won the men's Ballon d'Or award a record eight times, his last in 2023 (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, 2019, 2021 & 2023). The next best is Cristiano Ronaldo who has won the award five times. Between them, Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo won 13 of the 15 Ballon d'Ors between 2008 and 2023. The most consecutive wins is four, by Messi between 2009 and 2012.

Top Winners of the Men's Ballon d'Or

The women's Ballon d'Or has been held since 2018. There have been two multiple winners, both from Spain. Alexia Putellas won the award in 2021 and 2022, and Aitana Bonmati won in 2023 and 2024.
