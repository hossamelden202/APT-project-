List of Rugby League State-of-Origin Winners (women)

Here are all the winners list of the women's rugby league annual state-of-origin series, since the first in 1999. At first there was just one game between the teams, though in 2004, 2008 and 2023 there was a two game series, and three from 2024 onwards.

Notes

  • Queensland won the first 16 events.
  • Of the 25 years that matches have be played to date, Queensland have won 19, New South Wales 5, with 1 drawn (in 2015).
