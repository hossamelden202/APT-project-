2019 World Sporting Event Calendar

Here is a list of the major sporting events for the year 2019. Not every sporting event can be listed here, though we have tried to include all the significant sporting events of the major sports, mostly the international competitions.

Major events for this year were the FIFA Women's World Cup, Asian Games, African Games, Pan-American Games, Cricket World Cup, IAAF World Championships, Rugby World Cup, the inaugural World Urban Games, as well many more annual events.
