2016 NHL Entry Draft Combine Results

This was the 23rd time the annual NHL Draft Combine had been held. The combine assessment involves interviews, medical screenings, and fitness tests over a four-day period.

The world's top ice hockey prospects are invited to the combine, with over 100 draft-eligible players from North American and Europe being tested (the complete list of 2016 combine invitees).

The Combine was held for the second time in Buffalo, from May 30 to June 4 at First Niagara Center and Harbor Center. The medical examinations took place on June 2, and a majority of the fitness testing at the Harbor Center on June 4.

Below are the results of the fitness assessments for the 2016 NHL Scouting Combine (see details of the testing procedures). See also a summary of previous NHL Entry Draft Combine Results.
