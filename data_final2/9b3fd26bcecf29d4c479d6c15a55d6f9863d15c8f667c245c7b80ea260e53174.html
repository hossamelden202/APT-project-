2025 US Masters Preview – Key Players & Trends

The US Masters 2025 begins on Thursday, April 10 at Augusta National Golf Club. This tournament marks the first major of the year. It concludes on Sunday, April 13. The event brings together players from both the PGA Tour and LIV Golf circuits.

Jon Rahm

Player Profile: Jon Rahm is 30 years old. The Spanish player won the Masters in 2023. He employs a power-based approach that suits Augusta's layout. His game features strong driving distance and precise iron play.

Recent Form: Rahm moved to LIV Golf after his Masters victory. This transition affected his official world ranking. His statistical performance remains solid despite this change. His major results have varied since his 2023 triumph. He finished 45th in the 2024 Masters.

Influence: Rahm maintains global popularity despite reduced television exposure on LIV Golf. His current odds stand at +1500. This places him among the top tier of contenders for the 2025 tournament.

Rory McIlroy – A Career Grand Slam?

Player Profile: Rory McIlroy is 35 years old. He needs only the Masters to complete a career Grand Slam. He finished second at Augusta in 2022. This represents his closest attempt to date. His odds for 2025 stand at +650.

McIlroy maintains an active media presence. He speaks candidly about golf's industry issues. His charitable work extends his influence beyond the course. His status as the second betting favorite reflects his consistent performance level.

The pursuit of the career Grand Slam creates a compelling narrative. This storyline attracts casual viewers to the tournament. His odds reflect both his skill level and the public's betting interest in his quest for the green jacket.

Tiger Woods – A Notable Absence

Player Profile: Tiger Woods will not compete in the 2025 Masters. He suffered a ruptured Achilles tendon in March 2025. This injury required surgery. The 49-year-old's absence removes a historical focal point from the tournament. His 2019 Masters victory remains a significant moment in golf history.

Woods continues to influence media coverage despite his absence. His name affects betting patterns even when not participating. He maintains prominent sponsor relationships and media platforms. These connections preserve his position as golf's most recognized figure.

The Masters 2025 predictions must account for Woods' absence. Some players perform differently when Woods is not in the field. This creates statistical anomalies for bettors to consider when placing wagers.
