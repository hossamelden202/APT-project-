Mental Health and Exercise: From Stress to Strength

Exercise and sports are not just good for your body – they can also have a big impact on your mental health. Whether it's a daily walk, a team sport, or a workout at the gym, being active helps reduce stress, boost mood, and improve overall mental well-being.

mental health

Physical activity releases chemicals in the brain, like endorphins, which make you feel happier and more relaxed. Regular exercise can also help with anxiety, depression, and improve your sleep. In these articles, we’ll explore how staying active can be a powerful tool for improving your mental health.
