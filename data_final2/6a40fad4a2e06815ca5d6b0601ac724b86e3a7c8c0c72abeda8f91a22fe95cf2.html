Nutrition Quiz: Best Weight Loss Method

What is the most effective method for losing weight?

If you are switched on you will notice that this question is not meant to be completely serious. There are many ways that you could lose body weight, and these are just some of them.

We ran this quiz question on Topend Sports for a long time. What do you think? Check the answer below and see how many peope got it right.

Answer

The most popular answer to this poll is exercise, though fortunately people have seen the humor in the question and answered the most correct answer to the most effective way of reducing body weight fast - amputation.
