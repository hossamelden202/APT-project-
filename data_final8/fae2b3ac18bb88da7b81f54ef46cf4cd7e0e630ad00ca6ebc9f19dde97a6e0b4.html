Melbourne Cup Winners

Here is a list of all the winning horses, jockeys and trainers of the Melbourne Cup horse race since the first Melbourne Cup race in 1861 won by Archer, with a first prize of a gold watch and 170 pounds cash.

Only five horses who have managed to win the Melbourne Cup more than once, four of which are double winners and only a single triple champion - Makybe Diva (2003, 2004, 2005). In terms of trainers, Bart Cummingsis is the most successful with 12 Melbourne Cup wins.

In 2024, the winner of the Melbourne Cup received prize money of $4.4 million. See a history of the Melbourne Cup prizemoney.
