2024 NHL Entry Draft Combine Results

The world's top ice hockey prospects are invited to the annual NHL pre-draft combine, with over 100 draft-eligible players from North American and Europe being tested (see the list of 2024 combine invitees). The combine assessment involves interviews, medical screenings, and fitness tests over a four-day period.

The 2024 NHL Scouting Combine was held from June 3 to June 8 at KeyBank Center and LECOM Harborcenter in Buffalo, N.Y, with physical testing conducted on the final day. This was the 29th time the annual NHL Draft Combine had been held.

Below are the top 10 results of the fitness assessments for the 2024 NHL Scouting Combine (see details of the testing procedures). See also a summary of previous NHL Entry Draft Combine Results.
