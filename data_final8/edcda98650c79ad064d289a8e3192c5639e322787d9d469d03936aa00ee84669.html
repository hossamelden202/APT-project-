Complete List of Unusual Sports

Here is an alphabetical list of over 100 unusual sports, which we have discovered around the world. Some of the sports listed below are quite mainstream sports, but I have included them here as I think they are a little unusual, the people who partake in the sport may be a little unusual, or they are just not widely played and some people may be surprised to learn about this quirky sport.

  • Hobby Horsing Ś participants simulate traditional equestrian events while riding a simple object resembling a horse's head mounted on a stick.
  • Hoover Ball Ś a version of volleyball invented by President Herbert Hoover's personal White House physician.
  • Hop Pocket Races Ś races carrying a hop pocket, a large sack into which the dried Hop flowers are compressed and stored prior to being used to brew beer.
  • Horseback Boxing Ś competitors fight each other while riding on the back of a horse.
  • Hot Dog Eating Championships Ś eat as much as you can, as fast as you can. Don't try this at home.
  • Jaffa Race Ś racing jaffa lollies down the world's steepest street.
  • Jolleyball Ś a combination of volleyball and juggling
  • Juggling Triathlon Ś as it says, the sport is a combination of juggling and triathlon.

  • Land Diving Ś dry land bungy jumping.

  • Lizard Racing Championships Ś flat out like a lizard.
  • Makepung Ś racing buffalo competition from Bali.
  • Man vs. Horse Marathon Ś runners and horse riders compete against each other
  • Memory Sports Ś remember to try this
  • Marbles Ś don't lose your marbles
  • Milk Carton Regatta Ś have you had your milk today?
  • Mini-Golf Billiards Ś a pool cue is used to hit the ball instead of a golf putter, and the mini-golf hole is on a table top.
  • Monster Truck Rallies Ś big wheels to get excited about
  • Moonrock Throwing Ś they go further on the moon
  • Mr Ugly Ś Who is the ugliest person in Zimbabwe?
  • Mud Olympics Ś a range of sports for getting down and dirty - faster, higher and muddier!

  • Pooh Sticks Championship Ś not so sticky game played by Pooh Bear.

  • Redneck Games Ś southern alternative to the Olympics including a host of unusual sports.

  • Royal Shrovetide Football Ś all the village involved in this rugby type game.

  • Running with the Bulls Ś mix it up with the bulls in Pamplona in Spain.

  • Rural Games Ś New Zealand event comprising a large range of unusual sports and games.

  • Scottish Highland Games Ś a traditional Scottish sporting event

  • Sheep Races Ś catch me if you can.

  • Sign Spinning Ś participants incorporate breakdancing, gymnastics, and even martial arts-inspired moves while displaying an advertising sign.
  • Skipping Stones Ś the most skips or longest distance
  • Snail Races Ś exciting races with snails.
  • Snow Blower Race Ś competitors push a snow blower through a 20-meter long snow heap.
  • Sporthocking Ś extreme stool tricks
  • Stinging Nettle Challenge Ś why would you eat something with the word 'stinging' in it?
  • Straw Race Ś carrying bales of straw.
  • SUP Jousting Ś a modern twist on medieval jousting, fought with paddleboards and inflatable noodles.
  • Tar Barrels Ś running on fire.
  • Three-Legged Races Ś a children's competition conducted in pairs, in which a leg from each competitor is tied to their partner.
  • Three Sided Football Ś a variation of football in which three teams played instead of just two teams.
  • Thumb Wresting Ś two players use their thumbs to simulate fighting
  • Toe Wrestling Ś battle of the big toes.
  • Tombstoning Ś an extreme daredevil kind of an activity that involves diving into the sea from cliffs.
  • Tough Mudder Ś you need to be tough in this adventure obstacle race.
  • Tuk Tuk Polo Ś a variation of polo, in which players are on vehicles called tuk tuks.
  • Tuna Toss Ś hurling the fish in Port Lincoln, South Australia
  • Two-Handed Tennis Ś a variation of the popular game, a racket in each hand.
  • Ultimate Tazer Ball Ś involves players carrying a real taser gun and using it on opponents to gain possession of the ball.
  • Underwater Cycling Ś s a sport that involves riding a bicycle under water.
  • Unicycle Football Ś a variation of American football in which players move around the field using a unicycle
  • Uppies and Downies Ś a version of Medieval Football played in Workington in West Cumbria, with about 1000 players on each team who attempt to get the ball to the opposing team's goal.
