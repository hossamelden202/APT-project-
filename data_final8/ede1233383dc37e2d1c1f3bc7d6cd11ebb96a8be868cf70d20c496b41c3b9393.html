The Global Esports Games

The Global Esports Games is a major international Esports tournament organized by the Global Esports Federation, based in Singapore.

The inaugural event was held at the Resorts World Sentosa in Singapore on 18 and 19 December, 2021. The competition featured teams from across the world who had reached the finals in Singapore from Regional Qualifier Tournaments.

2021 Games & Results (Singapore)

  • DOTA 2 by VALVE - Open Category (4 teams, 5v5, won by Brazil), All-Women (4 teams, 5v5, won by Singapore)
  • eFootball 2022 by Konami - 1v1, 32 participants (won by Jo?o Victor from Brazil - "CSC_jvictor")
  • Street Fighter V: Champion Edition by Capcom - 1v1, 32 participants (won by - Yu-Lin from Chinese Taipei - "GamerBee")

2024 Games (China)
