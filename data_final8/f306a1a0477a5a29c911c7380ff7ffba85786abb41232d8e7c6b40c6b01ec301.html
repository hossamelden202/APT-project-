This is what an Example Guest Post on Topend Sports may look like

If you want to see what a guest post may look like, here is an example, including some of the guidelines for a guest post. For the complete guidelines, see here.

You need to supply all the content for the article, and it must be well-written, unique and ready for publication. We ask for an guest post article length of at least 500 words.

The Topic Needs to Be Suitable

The article should be related to the theme of the website, usually something about sports, fitness or nutrition. Just one long advertisement would not be acceptable.

Please check the topic idea is acceptable before proceeding with submitting an article. I reserve the right to refuse content I deem to be unacceptable or inappropriate, or just not to the standard of the website. I may make edits to the post to reach an acceptable standard or to conform with these conditions or my website's style.

The article should primarily be a valuable source of information, and not AI generated.

By submitting an article for inclusion, you are agreeing to the above conditions. The article may be modified to fit my guidelines if required.

Provide an image if you have a specific product image you need to include

You do not need to provide images for the article, I will source relevant images and place them appropriately within the article. Buy you can Provide an image if you have a specific product image you need to include.

Provide Just One Link

Only ONE external link is allowed, and it will be a DO FOLLOW link (it will not have rel="nofollow"), and the link destination should be relevant to the article content.

No casino/gambling/sports betting/vaping/CBD or adult content (no exceptions). Essay writing links and links to dating sites are also not accepted. A link to a product or business site is acceptable if it is relevant to the article.

Do not place links to pages on Topend Sports, I will add any relevant internal links within the article later.
