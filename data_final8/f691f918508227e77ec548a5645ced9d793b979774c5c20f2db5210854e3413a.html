AFL Draft Combine Results 2023

Here are the top fitness test results from the 2023 NAB AFL Draft Combine. There were 61 players invited to the national combine held in Melbourne on October 6-8, and (see the full list of invitees). Club interviews and medical screenings were conducted at Marvel Stadium for the first two days, with physical testing held at Melbourne Park on October 8.

An additional 55 players were invited to state draft combines in Victoria, South Australia, and Western Australia (see details and results).

The fitness tests conducted included the standing vertical jump, running vertical jump, 20m sprint, agility test and 2km time trial. The top-10 results from 2023 for each test is listed below. We have also collated the best results from each year, plus the top 10 of all time.
