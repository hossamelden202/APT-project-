NFL Combine Invite List 2021

Here is the official list of NFL 323 combine participants, for the event held from February 23 until March 2, 2021. The top prospects are usually invited to participate at the combine in Indianapolis, Indiana, but this year there was a pandemic influenced change in format, with the fitness assessments being held as part of the individual college pro days, and interviews being held virtually. See the test results from the 2021 Combine.
