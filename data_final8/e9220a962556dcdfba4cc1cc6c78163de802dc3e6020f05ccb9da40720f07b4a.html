Nutrition for Endurance Events

Nutrition is vitally important for success in long-distance running. The specific nutritional strategies that you use will depend on the event.

hydration is vital for endurance running

There are many nutritional considerations for marathon and ultra-marathon running, including the following:

  • Carbohydrate loading — loading up the body's carbohydrate stores
  • Hydration, sweat loss and salt intake - keeping the body well hydrated
  • Timing of food and fluid intake — what you should eat before, during and after exercise
  • Carbohydrate intake during the event - getting the right fuel when it is needed most.
  • Recovery Strategies — replacing fuels and preparing for the next training session
