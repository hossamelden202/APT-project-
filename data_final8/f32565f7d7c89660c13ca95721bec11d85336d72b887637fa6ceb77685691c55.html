NBA Finals Winners List

The NBA playoff is the annual championship series of the National Basketball Association (NBA). There is a series of seven games, the first team to win four games is the champion. Below is a list of all the winners of the NBA Finals since the Philadelphia Warriors won the first in 1947.

The Boston Celtics have won the most titles, 18 out of a total of 23 finals series that they have played in (including 2024). The Los Angeles (Minneapolis) Lakers with 17 wins is only just behind them, though they have appeared in 32 playoffs. The Eastern Conference leads the Western Conference in series won (41¢37). The now defunct Central Division won one championship.
