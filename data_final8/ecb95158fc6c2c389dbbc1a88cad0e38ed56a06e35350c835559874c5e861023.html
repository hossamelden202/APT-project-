About Rob Wood & Topend Sports

My name is Rob Wood, the creator of the Topend Sports website and author of most of the information it contains. This website is about all things sports, health and fitness - areas I am passionate about. The site has also grown from a small university project to a very large and free online resource of over 10,000 pages.

History

This site is began in 1997 as an online collection of fitness tests while I was studying Human Movement at The University of Western Australia (UWA). From there the site grew sporadically when I found time to work on it. Over time the site has been hosted on a couple of different website domains.

  • From 1997 to 1999 it was on the UWA server: http://www.general.uwa.edu.au/u/
    rjwood/Welcome.html
  • In early 2000 it was moved to http://www.fitness.testing.8m.com
  • The site finally reached https://www.topendsports.com in 2001.

This image was an icon of the original version of this website, which started around 1996, and for a long time he was still a part of the logo.

 

Who is Rob?

My name is Rob Wood, and this is my website. I am sports fanatic, sport scientist, and graphic designer, all rolled into one. My qualifications in exercise physiology are Bachelor of Science and PhD from The University of Western Australia. In graphic design, I have an advanced diploma from the Canberra Institute of Technology. Now you can see why I have made this site - it combines everything I love to do. I hope you have found it useful too.
