Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Auburn is approximately 36,720 (1997), 70180 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 13,977 (1990), 26058 (2010).
The amount of land area in Auburn is 51.023 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0 sq kilometers.
The distance from Auburn to Washington DC is 2458 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 37 miles. (as the crow flies)
Auburn is positioned 47.29 degrees north of the equator and 122.21 degrees west of the prime meridian.
The Auburn median home price is $ 103,395 (1994).
Auburn average annual rainfall is 38.48 inches per year
Auburn average annual snowfall is 8.6 inches per year.
The average winter temperature is 45 degrees F.
The average summer temperature is 75 degrees F.
