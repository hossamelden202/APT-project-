BOAT TOUR FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I DO THESE TOURS?
A: Yes, for the most part. The sunset cruises will probably be out for most cruise passengers, but all the daytime tours are available for you.
Q: I'VE NEVER SNORKELED BEFORE, CAN I DO THESE TOURS?
A: Absolutely! Your guides will give you a snorkeling lesson prior to getting in the water, and you'll have access to snorkeling vests or life jackets. It's very easy, even for beginners.
Q: WILL I GET SEASICK?
A: We always recommend to take a seasickness prevention such as Bonine or 24-hour Less Drowsy Dramamine an hour before boarding. Most people never have a problem, but if you're one who might, then it's always better safe than sorry. With proper prevention, very few people ever get seasick. We don't recommend regular Dramamine because it can make you drowsy.
Q: I HAVE A BIG GROUP COMING. CAN YOU SET SOMETHING UP SPECIAL FOR US?
A: Absolutely!  We can arrange any activity for a group of any size.  We can also arrange very special custom tours for couples or any size of group.  Request a Custom Quote.
