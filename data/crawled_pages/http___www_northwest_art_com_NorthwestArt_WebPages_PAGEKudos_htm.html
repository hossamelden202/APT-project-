"Kudos"

While I can't classify myself as famous, I have been around the block a few times so my work has achieved some degree of notoriety. I do not exhibit anymore at the "competitive" levels.I prefer to just keep it simple and connect with people on an artistic level. That means a great deal more than any award. Here are comments from some of those folks along with the more official kudos.
