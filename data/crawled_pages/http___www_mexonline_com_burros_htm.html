HISTORY :
THE FARALLON RESTAURANT AND BAR (as it was originally named) was established in August 1988
by the Brambila family. Later on, when the business was in its first years, it became very popular
for tourists and locals alike, the restaurant's greatest attractions were it's relaxing and casual ambience,
delicious margaritas, ice cold beer, seafood and a little pet donkey (BURRO) which one of the owners rescued.
This pet became the restaurants' icon and best asset.
Thanks to this little burro and tourists, the restaurant got its new name "THE BURROS BAR".
Eventually all the tourists and customers would say from there on,
"LET'S GO TO THE BURROS BAR!"
