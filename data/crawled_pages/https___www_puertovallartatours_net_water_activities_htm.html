VALLARTA WATER ACTIVITIES

There is nothing like a day on the water when in Puerto Vallarta. The sun shines almost every day, the water in the bay is almost always calm, humpback whales leap out of the water off your boat, bottlenose dolphins, manta rays, and sea turtles surround you as you tan in the sun, preparing to witness the magnificent underwater world of Puerto Vallarta. Sail on catamarans, kayak, snorkel, scuba dive, relax on remote beaches, party aboard a booze cruise, enjoy romance under the stars, or catch that massive fish you'll remember forever.
