CC Slaughters Gay Disco and NightClub
 

South Side - 254 Lazaro Cardenas at Ignacio Vallarta street
Tel: 222-3412 

CC Slaughters is located across the street from La Margarita bar and just 
just down the street from the ever-popular nightclub Paco's Ranch. 

The venue is split into two sections - The smaller, front portion is an open-air bar with video screens and windows
that open onto Lazaro Cardenas street. Music is a mix of of pop and dance, with no dance floor per se, though guys do dance.
The air-conditioned inner part of the discotheque is much larger, having booths, tables and a spaciously tiered dance floor,
the DJ spins heavy techno almost constantly and loves to use the smoke machine.

Service: Bar service is generally friendly and efficient. Relatively inexpensive drink prices.
Staff is attractive.

Cover: Entry is usually free, occasional charge on holiday weekends when there are big crowds.

 So, the CC Slaughters PV (facebook) main club features a good-sized dance floor 
with its own bar area off to the side, and occasional entertaining go-go boys center stage.

And there's a separate more relaxing martini bar lounge as well with some video screens.

Drag show every Tuesday night at 1am
Every Wednesday with 2x1 beer bucket special, 10pm-6am
DJs Estefano, Marko (Weds), and Hoolio (Sun)
Daily, 6pm-6am

Gay owned, gay staff, mostly 30-40 something crowd.
Popular with tourists and locals alike
since December 2011.

One of the top late-night entertainment hot spots for the 
gay community in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.
