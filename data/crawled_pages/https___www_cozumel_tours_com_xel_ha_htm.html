XEL-HA ECOLOGICAL PARK

Xel Ha Park is one of the most popular tourist attractions in all of Mexico, and once you're here it's easy to see why.  Xel-Ha is the world's premier natural aquarium, carved by the Gods centuries before.  This enchanting lagoon is a true taste of paradise, where you can relax in hammocks on the beach, snorkel with tropical fish in the warm, tranquil waters, float lazily on an innertube throughout the natural waterways and channels of the subterranean waterways, and even swim with dolphins, a lifelong memory.  Xel-Ha is only available as an all-inclusive, which is fitting for a place like this.  All food and drinks throughout the day from any of the five restaurants and bars is included in your admission.  There are a lot of things to do at Xel-Ha, a wonderful day!

XEL-HA PARK FAQ's

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I VISIT XEL HA?
A: Yes!  Xel-Ha is on the mainland, so you'll need to ride the passenger ferry over to Playa del Carmen, but once there, our Xel-Ha Express will take you directly to the park, where you can spend the whole day.  When it's time to come back, we'll take you directly to the pier.  One low price for your whole group, without the hassle of finding a taxi.

Q: WHICH IS BETTER, XCARET OR XEL-HA?
A: There is no answer to this question - both are absolutely wonderful, and we highly recommend both.  However, we like Xcaret more for families, as there are more attractions like animals to see, etc. and more activities to keep the kids entertained.  Xel-Ha we recommend more for adults, as it's more laid back and with better snorkeling.  Xcaret is about a half-hour closer to the ferry pier, so that is something to keep in mind.

Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
