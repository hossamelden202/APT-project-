BIKE TOURS COZUMEL

One of the most relaxing and rewarding ways to see certain parts of Cozumel Mexico is from an electric bike. You can ride along the coastal highway, with picturesque views of the blue Caribbean Sea beyond. You don't even have to peddle! The battery of these babies will have you gliding through Cozumel with no effort. Each of our electric bike tours features a different side of the island! You can go snorkeling in one of our tours or go Tequila tasting in the other.

COZUMEL BIKING FAQ's

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I DO THESE TOURS?
A: Yes! All these tours take place in the morning and most last around 3 hours. Check if these tours meet your cruise ship schedule.

Q: I AM LOOKING TO DO SOME EXERCISE, IS PEDALLING AVAILABLE IN THESE TOURS?
A: Yes it is! Our Pedego electric bikes can be used automatically or as normal bicycles. You choose! Glide through Cozumel effortlessly or pedal and do some great exercise while enjoying beautiful sights!

Q: CAN I DO THESE TOURS IF I HAVE MOBILITY ISSUES?
A: Sorry, these tours require active exercise from your part. It is very dangerous for you to do these tours if you've sustained any injury that limits your ability to ride a bike. No worries though! Check our other Sightseeing Tours!

Q: ARE THESE TOURS AVAILABLE FOR CHILDREN?
A: Definitely! Our Electric Bike tours are great fun for both kids & adults! Children from 10 years and up can do these tours!

Q: ARE THESE TOURS SAFE?
A: Yes they are! We provide you with all the necessary safety equipment so you'll be well protected! All the tours include a knowledgeable guide that will prioritize your safety in every part of these tours.
