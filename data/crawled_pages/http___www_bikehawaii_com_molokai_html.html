Rainforest to Reef Tour

When looking for Hawaii Vacation Deals 2023, one of the best Things to Do in Hawaii is the “Hawaii Rainforest to Reef tour”.
This is a spectacular full or half-day eco-adventure, starting with a hike through a private tropical rainforest to discover a beautiful waterfall. Then, coast down a paved road through lush Oahu rainforest on comfortable cruiser bikes.  Finish the day with a sail on Kahala Kai catamaran for a relaxing snorkel adventure off beautiful Waikiki.
