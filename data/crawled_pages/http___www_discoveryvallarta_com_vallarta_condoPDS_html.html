Puerto Vallarta Condos at One Beach Street Vallarta - Beachfront and Ocean-Side Rentals on Los Muertos Beach - Ref. PDS
 
 

        

One Beach Street Vallarta (formerly known as the Blue Seas Resort and Playa del Sol) is one of the newer condominium complexes on Los Muertos beach, with three buildings. The largest is the oceanfront Tower A, with seven stories and thirty-one beachfront units. Tower B is ocean-side with partial beach and Bay views, and Tower C is located along Amapas Street, with only street views. All vacation rentals here are one and two bedrooms, with the exception of the luxurious three bedroom penthouse.

Condo amenities: Elevator, reception desk, air conditioning, ceiling fans, equipped kitchen, cable television, wireless internet, 24 hour security and a large ocean-front heated dipping pool and sundeck terrace with beach views. Maid service is usually one (1) time per seven (7) day rental, and additional service may be requested for approximately $25.00 USD. In front of the One Beach Street condominium building is the newly renovated bar and restaurant, the Sapphire Ocean Club.

The condo building is located in the same block as the internationally famous Blue and Green Chairs of the gay beach, and is a short walk to all nearby shops, fine restaurants and a vibrant gay nightlife with its variety of bars, clubs, lounges and fun Mexican cantinas. It's also only a 10-15 minute stroll through scenic Old Town to the pedestrian bridge across the Cuale River and then into downtown Vallarta with its lively art galleries, dining spots, tourist attractions and entertainment venues.
