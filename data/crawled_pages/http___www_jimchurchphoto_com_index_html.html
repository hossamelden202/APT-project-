ABOUT THE COURSE!
Mike & Mike work with you individually and in small groups,
both topside AND in the water, so you can master your equipment.
Learn with multi-media presentations, videos, demonstrations, photo critiques and underwater help.
The 2 Mikes present lessons developed by Jim Church covering topics as:
  • Composition
• White Balance
• Using Strobes
• RAW vs JPEG
• Photoshop Techniques

• Digital Image Editing
• Color Correction
• Sunlight Exposures
• Exposure Meters
• Wide Angle

 • Close-ups
• Video Techniques
• Presentation
• and much more...
