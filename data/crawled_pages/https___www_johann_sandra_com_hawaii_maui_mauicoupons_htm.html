MAUI HAWAII ENTERTAINMENT BOOK

 

The #1 source of coupons for visitors to Maui is the Hawaii Entertainment Book. These coupons, just like your Entertainment Book back home, give you 2x1 discounts on restaurants, activities, tours, attractions, shopping, and much more.   What activities and restaurant coupons are contained in this book, you ask? Well, that's why we've made this page.  Too often, people miss out on these great savings opportunities in Maui because they aren't aware of just how great the savings are.  How much can I really save in a week, many people ask. Well, figure that if you use just one coupon, the book has probably paid for itself.  Do you think you'll eat out more than one time this vacation? Do you think that you might do an activity to go along with it?  Then you'll surely benefit from the Hawaii Entertainment Book. And don't forget, the discounts listed here are only the discounts on Maui - if you're island hopping, you'll want the book for all the discounts on the other islands as well - particularly Oahu.
