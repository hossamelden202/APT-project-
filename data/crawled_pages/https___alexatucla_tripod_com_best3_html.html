BEST OUTDOOR BAR

BIG DEANS: Next to the Santa Monica Pier
Nice white-trash bar (I mean that in a good way!). Rock music blazing! Best to enjoy on Roller-Blades during a sunny
weekend afternoon. If you drink too much, dont try to jump over a trash can --- believe me IT WONT WORK!

BEST OUTDOOR BAR ON THE SUNSET STRIP

RED ROCK: Sit, watch, cruise and count how many foreign cars you can spot racing by within a minute.

BEST GAY OUTDOOR BAR
