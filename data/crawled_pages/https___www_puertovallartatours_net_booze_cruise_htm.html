What makes a Booze Cruise? The one thing that these great boat tours have in common is that they all feature that all-important Open Bar. Puerto Vallarta has several excellent booze cruises to choose from.  Most of the booze cruises in Puerto Vallarta are daytime cruises, where you visit a beach, do some snorkeling, and then have drinks, music, and fun on the way back.  However, there are a few options for night-time booze cruise trips as well. Of course, you can always charter a boat and make your own booze cruise!  For more details on the trips available, click on the individual trip listings below.
