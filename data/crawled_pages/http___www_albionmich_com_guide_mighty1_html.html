Mammoth Michigan List of Links 
Welcome to the newly revised version of the Mammoth Michigan List of Lists! Sorry it took so long to update. This began in 1995 as a little experiment, testing the biggest search engines, looking for just the word "Michigan", and then categorizing all that I found. One thing I learned from the experiment: the search engines are all very very different. 

This version is the first major update since these pages were created. Actually I expected it to die off, but as time has gone on, more and more activity has been occurring, so we re-thought the idea and decided to keep making it better. At the moment the pages have been restructured and the dead wood deleted. Next I will be slowly adding new links, so check back from time to time, watch these lists grow! 

If you know of more links that should be included, please send e-mail to me (NEW ADDRESS: robinja56@gmail.com) and I will eventually incorporate these changes and any corrections whenever I can. Thanks, and enjoy, there are lots of hidden jewels all around the state and the surrounding lakes!
