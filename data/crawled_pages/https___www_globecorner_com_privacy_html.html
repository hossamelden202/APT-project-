Privacy is a right, not an option

Security at Globecorner.com

If you choose to use our on-line secure server to make purchases, all of your personal data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL-encryption before it is transmitted over the Internet. The change from http:// to https:// in the URL indicates that you have entered the Globe Corner Bookstore secure server which is certified by Verisign.

Once your encrypted order information is transmitted, The Globe Corner Bookstore stores this data on a machine which is not accessible from the Internet. Only Globe Corner Bookstore staff in our mail order fulfillment area have access to this data.

We guarantee the security of our system. In the event of unauthorized use of your card, you would first contact your credit card provider in accordance with its reporting rules and procedures. Under U.S. Federal law, your bank can not hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent charges. If your bank does hold you liable for any or all of the maximum $50.00 amount, The Globe Corner Bookstores will reimburse your for that charge up to $50.00 (assuming that the unauthorized use of your credit card resulted through no fault of your own from purchases made at our web site while using our secure server).

Cookies

Our shopping basket does place temporary cookies (small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive) for shopping purposes only. When you exit from your browser, our cookies are automatically deleted.

Since we run no third-party advertising on our site, there is no risk of other companies (ie., advertisers) placing cookies on your computer while visiting our secure server.
