Puerto Vallarta Horseback Riding FAQ's


Q: I'VE NEVER BEEN HORSEBACK RIDING - CAN I STILL DO THESE TOURS?
A: Yes.  These tours are suitable for beginners.

Q: WHAT SHOULD I BRING WHEN GOING HORSEBACK RIDING IN PUERTO VALLARTA?
A: Make sure to bring sunblock, even if it is raining when you leave - the rains are short-lived and normally followed by bright sunshine.  You will want long pants - sounds hot, but they'll protect your legs from rubbing on the horse and the stirrups.  You'll be a lot more comfortable.  Insect repellent is also suggested, along with good shoes that you don't mind getting wet (there are river crossings) and a swimsuit to swim in the waterfalls if you choose that tour.  And of course, most importantly, don't forget your camera! 

Q: IS THERE A WEIGHT LIMIT FOR THESE TOURS?
A: Yes.  The weight limit goes from 200 lbs. to 265 lbs. depending on which tour you choose.  See the tour detail page to see which one applies.

Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
