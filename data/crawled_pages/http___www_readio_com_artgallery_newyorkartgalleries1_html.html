SoHo TriBeCa Art Galleries
SoHo and TriBeCa Art Galleries include Woodward Gallery, Curt Marcus Gallery, Deitch Projects Gallery, Ernst Neizvestny Gallery, Apex Art Gallery, and Ronald Feldman Fine Arts Gallery.

57th Street Art Galleries
57th Street art galleries in New York City are Laurence Miller Gallery, Marian Goodman Gallery, Mary Boone Gallery, Pace Wildenstein Gallery, and Marlborough Gallery.

Chelsea W. 13th - 21st Street Art Galleries
Chelsea Art Galleries : Sperone Westwater Gallery, Alexander and Bonin Gallery, Gavin Brown's Gallery, David Zwirner Gallery, Bill Maynes Gallery, Cristinerose Gallery, and Paula Cooper Gallery.

Chelsea 22nd Street Art Galleries
Chelsea Art Galleries include CRG Art Gallery, Max Protetch Gallery, Friedrich Petzel Gallery, 303 Gallery, and Linda Kirkland Gallery.

Chelsea 24th Street Art Galleries
Chelsea Art Galleries include Charles Cowles Gallery, Gagosian Gallery, Mathew Marks Gallery, Luhring Augustine Gallery, Barbara Gladstone Gallery, Metro Pictures Gallery, & Andrea Rosen Gallery.

East Side Art Galleries
Upper East Side Art Galleries of New York City include Michael Werner Gallery, Gagosian Gallery, TamarindArt, DC Moore Gallery, & M. Knoedler & Co. Gallery.

West 23rd, 25th, 26th and West 29th Street
Chelsea Galleries : Paul Kasmin Gallery, Cheim & Read Gallery, Gallery Henoch, Greene Naftali Gallery, Lehmann Maupin Gallery, Robert Miller Gallery, and Sean Kelly Gallery.

Non-Profit Art Galleries
Non-Profit Galleries include Art in General Gallery, Chashama Gallery, Drawing Center Gallery, Dia Center for the Arts Gallery, Artists Space Gallery, Alternative Museum Gallery, Exit Art Gallery, Sculpture Center Gallery, Grey Art Gallery, and White Columns Gallery.

Photography Galleries
Photography Galleries in NYC include the International Center of Photography Gallery, Julie Saul Gallery, Howard Greenberg & 292 Gallery, Edwynn Houk Gallery, and Yancey Richardson Gallery
