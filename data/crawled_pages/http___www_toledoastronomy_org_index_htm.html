Welcome

The Toledo Astronomical Association is dedicated to the promotion of astronomy as a hobby and as a profession. The Association conducts regular meetings, star parties, and organizes viewing opportunities for schools, youth groups, civic organizations and the general public.

Friends and members of the TAA may subscribe to our moderated mail list by sending a blank email to "taamember-subscribe at toledoastronomy dot org".

To remove yourself from the mailing list, send a blank email to "taamember-unsubscribe at toledoastronomy dot org".
