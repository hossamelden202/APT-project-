MAUI HELICOPTER TOURS

The most fantastic way to see the rugged landscape of Maui is through a  helicopter tour.  The pilots can take you places that would take you days, even weeks to get to on foot, places where no roads exist and some that have never been explored.  You can get close-up views of waterfalls, dash through valleys, take glorious photographs, and take back the memory of a lifetime.

We offer the best Maui Helicopter Tours that you'll find on the web.  We use only the most reputable and professional guides, and we offer big savings over other booking agencies.   Our operators have the fastest and best helicopters on Maui, and know Maui inside and out, giving you a very breathtaking, fun, and interesting look at the island of Maui, Hawaii.

Air Maui Helicopter Tours
Air Maui offers stunning helicopter tours into Maui's lush tropical valleys and some of the world's most beautiful waterfalls! Choose from a variety of Maui helicopter tours - Complete Island Flight, Hana/Haleakala Flight, or West Maui/Molokai Flight.
   

Blue Hawaiian Maui Helicopter Tours
Every year since 1999, the prestigious Frommer’s Guide to Hawaii has named Blue Hawaiian “The Cadillac of helicopter tour companies.” Every year since 1998, the FAA has honored Blue Hawaiian with its coveted “Certificate of Excellence—Diamond Award.”
   

Hana Express Maui Helicopter Tours
Take a helicopter tour soaring above Haleakala Crater and across one the nation’s largest rain forests. Then climb aboard a Polynesian Adventures mini coach for a grand sightseeing tour of the Road to Hana.
   

Sunshine Helicopters Maui Tours
Sunshine Helicopters has been providing Maui helicopter tours since 1985 and is proud to have a perfect safety record. Consider the Molokai flight with landing at Kalaupapa - an unforgettable experience!
   

Horseback Ride & Helicopter Combo Tours
Enjoy the morning exploring Maui on horseback then take in its beauty from above exploring the lush West Maui Mountains and cascading waterfalls on a helicopter tour!
   

Maui Helicopter & Submarine Combo Tours
Descend 100 feet to explore a 25-acre natural Hawaiian coral reef and its inhabitants aboard the Atlantis Submarine and take a helicopter ride to get a spectacular view of Maui’s lush tropical rainforests and deep winding valleys.
