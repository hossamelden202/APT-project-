Puerto Vallarta Emergency and Important Phone Numbers and Information
Dial 911 in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico as the emergency phone number in order to contact the police (polic?a), fire department (bomberos) or for whatever urgent emergency you might have or witness. This is just like dialing 911 in the United States. 

Here below is a list of some other useful important and emergency Puerto Vallarta phone numbers for ambulances, hospitals, airlines, major department stores and supermarkets and importantly enough the local Consular Agents information if you have trouble in paradise.

You can buy the Ladatel phone cards for the public Telmex phones (picture left) at almost any convenience store, Puerto Vallarta pharmacy or grocery store in the city and these cards cost 30, 50 or 100 pesos. It's 3 pesos for an unlimited local phone call now; you can call the US or Canada for around 5 pesos (42 cents) a minute; calling a Mexican cellular phone number starting with 044 is rather expensive by the minute, try not to call collect or use the operator as the rates are rather outrageous. Phone service cost in Mexico is among the highest in the world.

Puerto Vallarta Helpful Telephone Dialing
International Country Code for Mexico is 52
Local Area Code for Puerto Vallarta Mexico is 322, followed by the 7 digit local phone number
Calling Puerto Vallarta from USA or Canada it's 011 + 52 + 322  + local number
To call direct to the U.S. or Canada from Puerto Vallarta, dial 001 + area code + local phone number
To call direct within Mexico, now dial the area code + local number
To dial US 800 numbers from Mexico, dial 001 + 880 + local number
To call direct International within Mexico dial 00 + country code + area code + phone number
To call International, operator assisted, dial 090
To call a cellular phone in PV from a land line here, dial 044 + 322 + local number

To dial to Mexican cell phone numbers from a land line:
In the same area code, Dial 322 (Vallarta area code) + local number (7 digits)
or in a different area code than where you are, Dial the area code + local number (7 digits)

Cellular Telephones - U.S. cell phones can work in Vallarta, but you need to contact your phone company for instructions and setup before arrival in Mexico. Be advised that telephone rates here for cellular phones tend to be rather expensive.

If you are dialing direct to the United States or Canada, rates become lower with Telmex after 7pm except for Sunday, when the lower weekend rates go up again after 7pm

Puerto Vallarta Pharmacies
Many non-narcotic drugs that require a prescription in the United States or Canada do not in Mexico and can be picked up in any pharmacy in town; new regulations starting in 2011 require a prescription now for antibiotics, and of course that has been true always for narcotic drugs. Most medications here are considerably cheaper than in the US.
