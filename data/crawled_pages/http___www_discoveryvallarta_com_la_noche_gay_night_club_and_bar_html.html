La Noche Night Club & Gay Bar
 

South Side - 263 Lazaro Cardena at I. Vallarta street.  Tel: 222-3364
Part intimate bar-lounge with music and subdued lighting "where conversation is encouraged"
and part loud nightclub with entertainment, dancers, drinks and music with DJ Mike.
Daily martini specials, with over 25 choices on the menu.

La Noche includes outside seating on the two-level open rooftop garden bar area,
with views of the town and the Sierra Madre mountains,
so there are two different areas with different styles and ambience.

Increasingly popular among locals and tourists alike, especially earlier in the evening.

La Noche (facebook) has go go boys every Thurs-Sun night at 11:30pm 
from October to April

Resident DJ Frank Bustamane with house music and latin pop remixed. 
Smoking and non-smoking areas, wireless. 
Since January 2002, initially as a small martini lounge. 

Happy hour 2X1 for all cocktails and beers, 1am-3am. 
Daily, 8pm-3am
