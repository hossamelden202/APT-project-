Outside of Puerto Vallarta, along the coastline of the Riviera Nayarit, lies the small surfing town of Sayulita.  Here we will begin our trail ride, mountain biking to secluded beaches, through the lush jungle ecosystem, stopping at various spots of interest.  There are several trails to ride at Punta Sayulita, so you can book this tour more than one day and bike different trails!

This bike trip is designed for groups with different ability levels, so we can be flexible and adapt to your skills as we go.  Because of this flexibility, we can do a shorter, mellow family or beginner ride or we can do a longer, more intense cross country ride.  There are some nice single track options with steep up hills and technical descents, but if you are not so experienced, there are plenty of easier areas to ride as well.

Riders will experience beautiful secluded beaches and get a feeling for the surrounding jungle in the area.  Mountain biking is an amazing way to explore the beautiful landscape of Sayulita and to get into the Mexican nature. See what Puerto Vallarta has to offer!
