This awesome tour combines two of Puerto Vallarta's most thrilling adventures into one unforgettable excursion.  Start out with the safest and most exciting jungle buggy tour in town - our extremely popular Jungle Buggy Adventure, then transition into one of the best zip line adventures in Puerto Vallarta!

We'll drive our jungle buggies along the dramatic Puerto Vallarta coastal highway, gaining panoramic views of Banderas Bay, and Los Arcos Marine Park. Then it's up a winding dirt road to our amazing ecopark, a jungle paradise close to where the Arnold Schwarzenegger movie Predator was filmed. We'll stop for a swim and a drink here, zipping down water slides and getting refreshed for the rest of the tour! Then, you'll fly high above the forest canopy on 8 thrilling lines!

After your zip line activity, you can order lunch (not included in the price) at a riverside restaurant or just enjoy the facilities before heading to our last destination. We'll drive our jungle buggies down the mountain to a small town, where we'll sample locally made tequilas and learn how this special Mexican liquor is produced. You can also learn the right way to drink tequila and buy some specialty tequilas you have probably never seen before!

Finally, we hop back in our buggies and hug the turns of the beautiful coastal highway with the sun in your face and the wind in your hair in our open-air jungle buggies back to Puerto Vallarta. You'll have a blast!
