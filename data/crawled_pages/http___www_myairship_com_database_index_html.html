An other great reference for manned airships is Jane's All the World's Aircraft, a reference book published annually by Jane's Information Group (New York). Called Jane's All the World's Airships at its inception in 1907, this reference has a whole section dedicated to airships only. Pick any year to find out about developments at the time. Pick the current edition to see which projects you didn't hear about. An Airshipper's Delight!
