Most units are basic accommodations, and are simply furnished, but all units with new linens, towels and pillows.  The -01 corner units on each floor are larger, and there are king beds in the these units, (except for the 601 with two double beds), and in unit 605, and those on the 8th floor. Cable televisions in units on the 2-9 floors. Many units from 4th floor up have a CD player and VCR/DVD, as well as a microwave. The building has an elevator and a concierge service for local Puerto Vallarta cruises and tours. Maid service three 93) times a week. English, Spanish and French are spoken fluently by the friendly office staff.

All condo units have air conditioning (most with a/c in bedroom and some in bedroom and living area), double sofa bed in living room (except 902), two bathrooms (except 001, 002, 802, 803, 804 and 901), and kitchens with toaster, coffee pot, pots & pans, flatware and setting for four persons. Units 502, 503, and 504 were recently completely remodeled; 605 and 804 were redecorated. No pets allowed.

Located one block up on the hillside behind the Blue and Green chairs of the Puerto Vallarta gay beach on Los Muertos beach. A fairly short, quiet walk at to all the great South Side gay nightlife of clubs, bars, cantinas and lounges, fun shopping and fine dining spots. Mostly gay clientele. Check in is at 4pm, check out is at noon.
