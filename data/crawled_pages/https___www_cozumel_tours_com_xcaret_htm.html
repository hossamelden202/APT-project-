XCARET ECOLOGICAL PARK

Located on the seashore near Cancun in the Riviera Maya, Xcaret is the place where nature blends with the remains of the Mayan culture in more than 50 exciting attractions. Nature lovers, thrill-seekers, art enthusiasts, or kids – Everyone is happy at Xcaret! Enjoy a traditional Mayan Ball Game, the Rotating Scenic Tower, or the Mexican Cemetery!

At Xcaret, you can also relish the Caribbean Sea: amazing snorkeling tours, underground rivers, haunting caves and Dolphin Swims. You are bound to have a blast!

When the sun comes down, the show starts. Be sure to celebrate Mexico’s rich history at the well known “Mexico Espectacular” show, a display of color, tradition and art from more than 300 Mexican artists that will walk you through Mexico’s past!

XCARET PARK FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I VISIT XCARET?
A: Yes!  Xcaret is on the mainland, so you'll need to ride the passenger ferry over to Playa del Carmen, but once there, our Xcaret Express will take you directly to the park, where you can spend the whole day.  When it's time to come back, we'll take you directly to the pier.  One low price for your whole group, without the hassle of finding a taxi.
Q: WHICH IS BETTER, XCARET OR XEL-HA?
A: There is no answer to this question - both are absolutely wonderful, and we highly recommend both.  However, we like Xcaret more for families, as there are more attractions like animals to see, etc. and more activities to keep the kids entertained.  Xel-Ha we recommend for adults, as it's more laid back and with better snorkeling.  Xcaret is about a half-hour closer to the ferry pier, so that is something to keep in mind.
Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
