About SkiCentral

Since 1995, SkiCentral has been recognized as one of the leading snowsport websites.

Originally developed as a specialty search engine for snowsport enthusiast, the site has since repositioned itself as a leading ski resort guide. We strive to provide the most accurate and up to date information available.

Main content areas include detailed resort coverage such as mountain statistics, resort amenities, resort deals, lift ticket deals, resort ski reports, lodging, trail maps, and location maps.

SkiCentral serves over 1.7 million skiers each season with over 7 million page views. Our primary audience is skiers and snowboarders who are planning ski vacations to destinations across North America. 79% of users are located in the US, 12% are from Canada.

New for the 2015-2016 will be additional tools for doing resort comparisions to assist skiers and snowboarders who are planning ski vacations to new destinations, as well as expanded content.

For more information you can contact us.
