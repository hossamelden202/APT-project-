A great hour or two of total relaxation in Cabo San Lucas, gently sailing and lounging in the sun on the Sea of Cortez, enjoying great photo opportunities of The Arch, Land's End, Medano Beach, and the Cabo San Lucas Marina, among others.  You'll get to sail your own private 2-person catamaran, just your group and your professional guide, then you can return to the beach and lounge, eat lunch, or enjoy one of our fun beach breaks at Blue Marlin Ibiza or The Medano Beach Club.  Your guide can show you how to sail, or can do the work while you relax and take in the sun!
