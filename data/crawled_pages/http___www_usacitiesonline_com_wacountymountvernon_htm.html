Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Mount Vernon is approximately 26,232 (2000), 31743 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 7,167 (1990), 11342 (2010).
The amount of land area in Mount Vernon is 21.835 sq. kilometers.
The amount of land area in Mount Vernon is 11.1 sq. miles.
The amount of surface water is 0.51 sq kilometers.
The distance from Mount Vernon to Washington DC is 2482 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 99 miles. (as the crow flies)
Mount Vernon is positioned 48.42 degrees north of the equator and 122.31 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Mount Vernon elevation is 180 feet above sea level.
Mount Vernon median income is $ 37,999 (2000).
The Mount Vernon median home price is $ 142,000 (2000).
Mount Vernon average annual rainfall is 32.31 inches per year
Mount Vernon average annual precipitation is 35.4 inches peryear.
Mount Vernon average annual snowfall is 16.1 inches per year.
The average winter temperature is 39.7 degrees F.
The average spring temperature is 49.4 degrees F.
The average summer temperature is 62.4 degrees F.
The average fall temperature is 51.0 degrees F.

Location

 in northwestern Washington about 25 miles from Bellingham and 55 miles from Seattle. Other nearby communities include Burlington, Big Lake, Clear Lake, Sedro-Woolley, Lake Ketchum, Bay View, North Stanwood and La Conner.
