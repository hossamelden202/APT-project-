You may have heard of the famous Puerto Vallarta Canopy Tour - now the Costa Azul Xtreme canopy tour brings the same excitement to the wilds of Cabo San Lucas, San Jose del Cabo, and Los Cabos Mexico.

Strap in to one of our secure harnesses, and fly on over three miles of zip lines, high above Costa Azul Canyon and the river far below.  A truly thrilling experience - sure to become a big hit in Cabo San Lucas.  Expert guides will be with you every step of the way taking every precaution, as you enjoy the flight over Los Cabos, and the seeming endless desert scenery.  The last zip line is a double-line, so you can race against others in your group!

After the tour, you can enjoy the snack bar on site where you can purchase refreshments or quesadillas.  As an added bonus, you can also participate in such exciting activities as the Tarzan Swing, Racing Cable Contest, and 180-foot free-rappelling cable!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I let go?  Will I die?
A: No - you're securely fastened in by a rock climbing harness.  Nothing will happen.

Q: I'm scared of heights - can I go on the Canopy Tour?
A: Many people do the canopy tours that are afraid of heights - because you always take off from solid ground, most people overcome their fear quite easily.

Q: Is there a weight limit?
A: Yes - 250 lbs. is the maximum for the zip lines, 220 lbs. maximum for the rappelling activity, and 100 lbs. maximum for the tarzan swing.

Q: Are there brakes?
A: Yes, the guides will show you how to use the brakes.

Q: Is the canopy tour physically demanding?
A: There is some uphill walking, so wear good shoes.  However, most of the tour is just effortlessly zipping across Costa Azul Canyon.
