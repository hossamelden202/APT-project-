          Products and technologies

          From foundational technologies such as qPCR, microarray, and CE to cutting-edge innovations such as digital PCR, we are your one-stop shop for your entire genetic analysis workflow, all manufactured under the highest quality standards and backed by unsurpassed global service and support.

          Digital PCR

          Digital PCR uses molecular counting to provide highly precise and sensitive results. It is ideal for applications such as absolute quantification of differential gene expression and rare-allele detection.

          Learn more

          Real-tme PCR (qPCR)

          We’ve developed powerful assay design algorithms, optimized master mixes, created intuitive data analysis software, and built smart instrumentation to help harness the power of real-time PCR across a rich and diverse set of applications.

          Learn more

          Sanger sequencing
