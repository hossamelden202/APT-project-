  Free Printable Targets for your Shooting Enjoyment  

EASY TARGETS.... If you have had trouble downloading targets off the Internet then you have arrived at the right place for printing targets the easy way. These first four targets are each, one page HTML files. No downloading is required! Click on a target thumbnail you want to print. When it loads on your screen, merely print the page as many times as you like. NOTE: There are no return links on these targets because the extra text would cause clutter on the printed targets. If you want to return to this page, you will need to use your browser's back button. Only the two Benchrest style targets do not have the clear, sharp edges like the high quality download type targets below have, but the process is easy and no special software is required. Good shooting.

Squares. BIG Square.

Benchrest. Modified Benchrest.

TARGETS IN ADOBE PDF FORMAT.... Bill from the Mini Lathe Forum has taken the time to convert many of my Modified Bench Rest style targets to the Adobe PDF Format. These files below when printed generate very high quality targets. You will need the Adobe Acrobat Reader. Free Adobe Acrobat Reader here.
Get all 13 of the .pdf targets below in one file. Download here: alltargets.zip (313Kb).

br100.pdf Similar to a 100 yard Benchrest target.

br12d.pdf A modified Benchrest target with larger squares and circular hole for 12X scopes with a large dot reticule.

br12.pdf A modified Benchrest target with 1" squares and circular hole for 12X scopes with a smaller dot reticule.

br12b.pdf Similar to br12d.pdf without the frames, center dots, and crosses.

br36.pdf A modified Benchrest style with 1/2" squares for 24X or higher powered scopes. Not so much white space for centering the crosshairs. As they said in the movie: "Aim small and miss small" or for here: "Aim small and print smaller groups".

brmc36.pdf The same square-circle aim point as a Modified Benchrest target but with concentric rings.

brmd24.pdf The same aim point as a Benchrest target but for 24X scopes.

targ5.pdf Five aim points without clutter.

targ9.pdf Nine slightly larger aim points if you like to save paper.

grouptargets.pdf Randy Uecker sent me this target and it prints to scale on my Konica Magicolor 2400W printer.

targ22.pdf This is a good target for a scoped 22 LR or Scoped Air Rifles at from 25 to 50 yards. Many aim points.

1squirrel.pdf Thanks to Willie Childers who converted these ground squirrel pictures into files that can be printed.

2squirrels.pdf Two ground squirrels on one page.
