This is the "compromise" dolphin swim at Aquaventuras Park.  It is for those who want to do a fun activity actually swimming with the dolphins, but don't want to spend $159.00 to do it.  You get to do the basic activities like a handshake and free time with the dolphins, plus a very fun belly ride across the pool. You just don't get to do the popular foot push or dorsal tow which are only available with the Royal Swim.

Aquaventuras Park:  Experience the popular water park located in Puerto Vallarta. Enjoy a variety of attractions including water slides, pools, a lazy river, and interactive experiences with dolphins and sea lions. The park is known for its family-friendly atmosphere and thrilling rides.

Zip Line Circuit, Rock Climbing Wall and Challenge Circuit (only 1 time for every activity).  If you want to do these activities more than once you can pay $6.00 in cash the day of your tour at Aquaventuras park.

Dolphin Swim Adventure Highlights

Free Entrance to Aquaventuras Water Park is included in the price of this tour!  You can enjoy the shows, water slides, and much more.  Great family fun for an afternoon in the Puerto Vallarta sunshine!

Get a fast and fun ride across the pool from your dolphin, who will give you a thrilling belly ride!

Interact with the dolphins in the water, feeling their unique skin, and getting a handshake from your dolphin, as they pose for photos!
