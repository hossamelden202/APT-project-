Mission Statement

Shark Reef adheres to high standards of animal care, while providing an entertaining and educational experience, as well as contributing to education and research initiatives.

Conservation

Shark Reef Aquarium is committed to protecting natural resources and aquatic species through support of meaningful conservation initiatives. We operate the aquarium sustainably by recycling, converting exhibit lighting to LED, installing variable frequency drive motors on our pumps, recycling exhibit water, and buying sustainable seafood. Shark Reef participates in conservation and research through organizations that are aligned with our mission and values. Through these partnerships, we have increased the breeding of key species, increased the knowledge of shark husbandry, and rescued three Green Sea Turtles. Learn more about our endangered sea turtles here.

About this show

Take a walk on the wild side with more than 2,000 animals at the Shark Reef Aquarium! Whether you are looking to gaze at the friendly creatures from inside the shipwreck, or thrill your senses diving alongside the sharks, Shark Reef Aquarium is sure to keep you mesmerized from start to finish!

Undersea Explorer VR Theater

Have you ever dreamed of swimming with a humpback whale in Tonga or diving next to a 15-foot tiger shark in the Bahamas? Now’s the time to make that dream a reality. . . virtually.

A 36-seat motion-platform VR theater is at the heart of the experience. Put on your headset, sit back in the motion seats and be immersed in a world of undersea adventure. 360? 3-D virtual reality technology transports you to the South Pacific where a marine biologist takes you on a live-action journey to the nurturing grounds of the humpback whales, where you swim side-by-side with mother humpbacks and their new-born calves. Or if excitement is on your agenda, dive with tiger sharks and great hammerheads in the turquoise waters of Tiger Beach in the Bahamas.

A fully-realized experience that can be enjoyed by the whole family, the Undersea Explorer VR Theater engages the guest from its very start with an immersive and entertaining pre-show area, where guests learn about sharks and whales.

Take a selfie in the hyper-realistic shark cage, learn about the amazing anatomy of the humpback whale, track the movement real-time of sharks around the world and learn the secrets of the humpback whale songs, all as you prepare for your virtual adventure.

Included with Shark Reef Aquarium & Undersea Explorer package. VR films for ages 5 and older, younger children can enter the exhibit but must be accompanied by an adult. During VR experience children under 5 will need to rotate with a guardian or parent.

Education

Shark Reef Aquarium’s formal Educational Programs are for students who are a part of a class enrolled in a school or educational organization. For more information, please email sharkreefeducation@mandalaybay.com

Accreditation

Shark Reef Aquarium is the first and only facility in Nevada accredited by the Association of Zoos & Aquariums. The AZA is dedicated to the advancement of zoos and aquariums in the areas of conservation, education, science and recreation. The AZA accreditation ensures that you are supporting an institution dedicated to providing excellent care for animals. Click here to learn more about AZA Accreditation.
