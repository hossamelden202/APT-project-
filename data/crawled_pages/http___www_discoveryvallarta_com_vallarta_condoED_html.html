Puerto Vallarta Condos Beachfront - Popular Condo Vacation Rental Building - Ref. ED
 

ED is one of the most sought after condo buildings in Puerto Vallarta, with a total of 22 units on Los Muertos beach. The El Dorado condos are one, two or three bedroom units and all are beach-front. The condo building is a short walk to all South Side gay bars, restaurants and shopping and two blocks, a short 2-3 minute stroll, to the Blue and Green chairs of the Vallarta gay beach. The building has an elevator, security gate and reception desk, with telephone service in all units. A dipping pool (heated from Nov 1-May 1) and sundeck terrace with terrific views of the Bay and beach is available for use by all clients. On the beach is the palapa-roofed Boca Bento at El Dorado restaurant which has been one of Puerto Vallarta's favorites for well over forty years. Maid service in the condos is six days a week; there is no service Sundays and national Mexican holidays.

Two bedroom condos are for four (4) guests maximum; one bedroom condos are for two (2) guests max; the three bedroom penthouse condo is for six (6) persons, and all units have air conditioning in the bedroom/s only. No pets are allowed. There is wireless internet service available in the lobby reception area and in the meeting room where there are also several personal computers available for guest use, free of charge. Some individual condos have wireless service.

Note: All accommodations are subject to availability and prices on this website are subject to change without notice until the reservation is confirmed. Condos are now all non-smoking, including the balconies. ED-42 is non-smoking inside but smoking ok outside on small balcony. Other condominiums are non-smoking. All public areas of the building are now non-smoking with the exception of the pool area and the lower deck. No cigars or pipes are allowed in any public area.
