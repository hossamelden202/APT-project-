Lemon Law Information and Sites

Welcome to one of the oldest and most trusted Lemon Law information sites on the Internet with complete coverage of all Lemon Law Statutes in all 50 states and the District of Columbia.

There are essentially three sets of laws that apply to defective vehicles and products in the United States.

    1. The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act is a Federal Law that protects the buyer of any product that costs more than $25 and comes with an express written warranty. Here is a summary of the Act in layman's language, here is a more detailed explanation of the Act from the FTC, and here are the actual documents from the FTC.

    2. The Uniform Commercial Code or UCC has been adopted in all 50 states and covers contracts dealing with the sale of products. The online version of the UCC is available here.

    3. Summary of State Lemon Laws here and individual State Lemon Laws begin here.

Many Lemon Law Attorneys offer free consultations in person, via toll-free phone numbers and/or email to let consumers know what their options are.
