CRUISE SHIP PASSENGER FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (Also see our General FAQs)

Q: what if my ship doesn't port?  do i get my money back?
A: We are proud to feature Puerto Vallarta's only No-Port, No-Pay policy.  If during your sailing, your ship doesn't make port as scheduled due to weather, mechanical failure or other unforeseen circumstances, just give us a call or drop us an e-mail to let us know (please tell us as soon as you know!), and we'll be happy to refund your money 100% - no service charges or other hidden fees.  We know how to take care of our customers - after all, you're the ones taking care of us! See our terms and conditions for complete details. 

Exceptions: Fishing, Private Charters, Dolphins, Las Caletas, Rhythms of the Night are subject our regular cancellation policy.

Q: why should i book my excursions through you?
A: There are so many reasons!  See our General FAQs to see all the great reasons to book your excursions with us.  Among others, you get our local expertise, you can call home for free at our office. We have a great money-back cancellation policy (see our Terms and Conditions for details), we'll have you in for a beer if you want to stop by, we get exclusive privileges for our guests on some tours, like the Pirate Ship, that nobody else can give you, toll-free english-speaking customer support, and of course - you save SO much money!

Q: i'm on a cruise ship - how do I find you or my EXCURSION departure point?
A: When you arrive at the cruise ship port, you will find dozens of taxis waiting for you.  You can take one of these straight to the hotel meeting point or you can walk a short distance out to the highway and hail a cab which will be cheaper than those at the port.  Taxis are easy to come by in Puerto Vallarta - they are everywhere.  You'll receive electronic tickets via e-mail before you leave, so you won't need to find us, just your tour meeting location.  Tell the taxi driver where you're going, and he'll take you directly there.  A taxi normally costs just a few dollars to go anywhere from the port.

Q: ARE YOUR TOURS ON LOCAL TIME OR SHIP TIME?  WHAT TIME ZONE ARE YOU IN?
A: All of our tours are on Puerto Vallarta local time, which is the same as Central time.  Please check with your ship to see what time zone they are reporting your arrival and departure times in, because ships don't always change to local time.

Q: HOW DO I CONTACT YOU ONCE I'M IN PUERTO VALLARTA?
A: You can telephone us at +(52) 322 308-0123 if you have any issues - we'll be happy to help. 

Q: WHAT'S THE CATCH? ARE THESE THE SAME EXCURSIONS THAT MY SHIP OFFERS?
A: No catch.  Just real discounts, all the time.  No timeshare presentation - nothing.  Just saving you money in Puerto Vallarta - that's what we do.  Yes, these are the same tours your cruise ship offers and even more to choose from.

Q: HOW FAR IN ADVANCE DO I NEED TO RESERVE MY SHORE EXCURSIONS?
A: The sooner the better.  A minimum of 24 hours in advance is required, but activities do sell out, so make yours as soon as you know that you want to take it.

Q: THERE'S AN EXCURSION I WANT TO TAKE, BUT I DON'T SEE IT LISTED.
A: No problem - just give us a call at our toll-free telephone number, 1-877-545-3284 , and we can either book it for you at a discount or recommend an alternative that is similar.

Q: HOW DO I BOOK AN EXCURSION THROUGH YOU?
A: On the tour detail page, there are red icons that say "Book Now!".  Click those and the excursion will be added to your shopping cart.  Follow the on-screen prompts to complete your checkout.  You will receive an e-mail receipt immediately, and within 24 hours you'll receive an electronic ticket that you'll give to the operator on the day of your tour.  You can also call us toll-free at 1-877-545-3284 anytime.

Q: I'M THINKING OF BOOKING 2 EXCURSIONS DURING MY STOP - SHOULD I?
A: Before booking two excursions in one port of call, please call us.  We'll be happy to help you plan your schedule so that you pick only excursions whose schedules will work together, and with your ship's schedule, and let you know if you're cutting it too close.

Q: DO YOU OFFER TRANSPORTATION FROM THE CRUISE SHIP TERMINAL?
A: For some tours we do, such as the Los Veranos & City Tour Combo, but mostly no.  However, there are taxis swarming the terminal when you arrive.  You can take one of those, or walk out to the highway and flag one down to save money - the ones on the highway are cheaper.  It's normally only a few dollars to take a taxi, and then you can go into town and see Puerto Vallarta too.  No waiting, no shuttles, no schedules, much more freedom.  Almost all of our boat tours leave from the same marina as your cruise ship. We also have a personal escort from the pirate ship to show you to your boat.

Q: is it safe to do an excursion in puerto vallarta?
