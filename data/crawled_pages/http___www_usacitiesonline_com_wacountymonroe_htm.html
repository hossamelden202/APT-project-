Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Monroe is approximately 13,795(2000), 17304 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 1,712 (1990), 5024 (2010).
The amount of land area in Monroe is 9.923 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0.088 sq kilometers.
The distance from Monroe to Washington DC is 2455 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 71 miles. (as the crow flies)
Monroe is positioned 47.85 degrees north of the equator and 121.98 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Monroe elevation is 68 feet above sea level.

Location

 in southwestern Snohomish County on the north bank of the Skykomish River, approximately 7 miles north of the King County Boundary. The City of Everett is 17 miles northwest of Monroe on Highway 2, and the City of Seattle is about 35 miles southwest of Monroe.

Attractions

There are many horse farms, boarding and training stables and arenas dotting the countryside in every direction, as well as several dairy farms, berry farms, and corn fields.
