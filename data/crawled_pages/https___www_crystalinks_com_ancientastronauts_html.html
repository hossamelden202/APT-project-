Ancient Alien Theory



Ancient Alien Theory alleges that extraterrestrials came to Earth in the past with different agendas. Some aliens were sentient life forms - but for the most part they were artificial intelligence. Most researchers believe they were part of creating and monitoring the current version of the human experiment.

They have come and gone in spaceships, through portals and wormholes, and things we know nothing about - leaving behind enigmatic artifacts as proof of their existence here. Some believe they will return as the human experiment ends with us.

I believe ancient visitors are not programmed to return. At the end of the day - it's all a simulation set in linear time to experience emotions.
