A wild and crazy tour aboard the Marigalante, a real pirate ship that features pirate shows, singing, swordfights, and a 2-hour stop at Majahuitas Beach for snorkeling, beach volleyball, banana boat rides, kayaking, and a delicious lunch. This tour is raucous fun for the whole family.  We recommend this tour for families with kids.

The tour starts with boarding the boat and enjoying a delicious breakfast. During the boat ride to Majahuitas, you will experience audience participation games and a pirate show. Once there, the bar opens with sodas and soft drinks available on the boat, while beer and alcoholic drinks (if you choose this option) are served continuously. You can enjoy beach activities or snorkeling while the staff entertains the kids with a treasure hunt, tug of war, and other fun activities just for them. After the tour, photos and a DVD of your experience will be available for purchase.

Duration: 8 Hours Approximately.

Cruise Passengers:  Unfortunately, this activity is not available for Disney Cruise Passengers. However, it's an excellent shore excursion option for passengers on other cruises, if your ship's schedule allows for it.

Runs:  Tuesday to Sunday at 9:30 AM.

Departs From: Maritime Terminal in Puerto Vallarta. Directions will be provided with your confirmation.

Kids: All ages welcome. Kids prices apply to ages 4-11 years. Kids under 4 years old are free.
