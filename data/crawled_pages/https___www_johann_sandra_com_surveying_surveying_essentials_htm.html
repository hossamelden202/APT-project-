Self Defense

Ever been brushing through thick underbrush, only to hear that "crunch" as your machete hits a basketball-sized hornets nest?  Ever come around a bend to stand face-to-face with a grizzly?  After bolting out of the woods and racing for the nearest shelter, you still have to go back in and set or tie that monument!  Don't go in empty-handed! 

CRC Wasp & Hornet Killer Plus Insecticide
Udap Pepper Power Bear Deterrent 9.2 oz.
Udap Pepper Power Bear Deterrent 7.9 oz.

 

First Aid

You just never know when you might slice that hand open with your machete, or rip that leg on a barbed wire fence.  What if you get stung by a bee or bit by a snake?  Make sure you have what you need.  A wimpy backpacker first-aid kit just won't suffice when you're miles from the nearest hospital and you're out there every day.
