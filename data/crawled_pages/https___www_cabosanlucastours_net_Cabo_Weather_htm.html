CABO SAN LUCAS CLIMATE INFORMATION

Cabo San Lucas has an arid Sonoro Gulf Coast Desert climate, averaging over 350 days a year of sunshine.  If you have to ask what the weather will be like during your vacation, the answer is sunny.  Pick up a pair of Maui Jim Sunglasses and some major sunblock before you come down.  Because it doesn't have the tropical humidity of other vacation destinations in Mexico, and is further north, the evenings will cool off significantly, so bring a light jacket or sweater, particularly in the winter months.  Expect temperatures to be about 10 degrees cooler on the Pacific side than on the Sea of Cortez side.
