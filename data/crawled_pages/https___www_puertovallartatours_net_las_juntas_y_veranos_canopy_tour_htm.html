This is Mexico's most popular canopy tour, stretching over 2 miles with 19 zip lines, including a thrilling last run where a double line allows you to race each other!  In this lush, tropical location, you will glide effortlessly at speeds of up to 30 miles per hour, at a whopping 500 feet above the beautiful Orquideas River.

After the tour, you can relax and have lunch at Pancho's Jungle Restaurant overlooking the  river while the kids try out the water slides or swim in this lush tropical location (including rope swings, a waterfall and a water slide), sample varieties of Tequila from the bar, shop at the boutique, visit the animal sanctuary (with tropical animals you can pet!), and even purchase a video or photo of your actual tour to remember your experience forever!

One of the highlights of this tour is that you can choose if you want to use our land or sea transportation! Fulfill your need for speed and adrenaline with a speedboat ride in Puerto Vallarta waters! We'll take you on THE MOST FUN adrenaline-fueled adventure in Los Veranos!


Cruise Passengers:  Yes! This is a great choice for a cruise shore excursion. The exact pick-up time and location will depend on your chosen departure (Land or Sea Departure), but will be shown on your confirmation. The Sea Departure is the best option for cruise passengers since the meeting location is right inside the cruise terminal.

Runs:  Monday to Saturday, Three Times a day at 10:20 AM, 12:20 PM and 2:20 PM.  Exact pick-up time will depend on your hotel's location, but will be shown on your confirmation.

Departs:  Complimentary pick-up from various locations in Nuevo Vallarta, Maritime Terminal, Puerto Vallarta, Conchas Chinas and Mismaloya. If you are a cruise passenger, you can just walk to our meeting location at a walking distance from the Cruise Pier.  Exact pick-location will depend on your departure and will be shown on your confirmation.

Duration: Approximately 5 hours.

Includes: 

  • Round-Trip Transportation via Speedboat or Land
  • 19 Ziplines Over the Jungle and River
  • English Speaking Tour Guides
  • Access to the Petting Zoo
  • Water Activities at the River
  • Tequila Tasting
  • Equipment, Helmets
  • Gloves, Safety Orientation and Instruction
  • Loads of Fun!

You'll Need:

  • Biodegradable Eco-Friendly Sunscreen
  • Swimsuit and Towel
  • Tennis Shoes
  • Hair Tie for Long Hair
  • Cash for lunch, souvenirs

Restrictions: Maximum weight is 285 lbs. (129 kgs).  In addition, in order to fit into the harness, your waist must be less than 50" and your legs must be less than 28" around.  Do not drink too much alcohol before this tour - you won't be allowed to go for safety reasons.  Pregnant women or those with serious back injuries should not participate on this tour, but can join as observers on the Land Departure. There is uphill walking and climbing of stairs on this tour, so we recommend a level of physical fitness.

Kids:  Ages 5 and Up. Prices apply to ages 5-11.
