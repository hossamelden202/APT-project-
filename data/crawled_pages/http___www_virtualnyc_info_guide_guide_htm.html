The New York City guide is an excellent reference for anyone planning to visit New York. 
No place else is quite like New York. Simply stated, we have more of the best to delight and entertain than anywhere in the world: with 150 world-class museums, 18,000 restaurants of all types and price ranges, 37 Broadway theaters, a revitalized Times Square and Grand Central Terminal, and an unbelievable array of shopping. And no other city has such a diversity of people and cultures.
