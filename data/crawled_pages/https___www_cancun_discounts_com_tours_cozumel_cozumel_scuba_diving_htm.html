Cozumel Scuba Diving - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the water always warm?
A: April through November expect low 80s. From December through March, expect high 70s - comfortable all year round.  The water temperature never varies more than a few degrees throughout the year, usually between 78 and 82 degrees. You will never need more than a 3mm wetsuit to dive in Cancun, no matter what time of year.

Q: How clear are the waters of the Caribbean?
A: The best visibility is in the crystal-clear water of the Cenotes.  You can see almost forever - over 250 feet.  Second best is Cozumel where visibility ranges from a "mere" 100 feet in the winter to over 200 feet (wow) from April through September.  In Cancun, you can expect around 40 feet between September through April, and about 80 feet May to August.

Q: Can I dive from shore in Cozumel?
A: There are some places in Cozumel where you can dive from shore, there are some reefs that can be reached from shore.
