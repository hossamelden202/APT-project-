Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of West Wenatchee is approximately 1,681 (2000).
The approximate number of families is 661 (2000).
The amount of land area in West Wenatchee is 12.081 sq. kilometers.
The amount of land area in West Wenatchee is 3.95 sq. miles.
The amount of surface water is 0.474 sq kilometers.
The distance from West Wenatchee to Washington DC is 2365 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 127 miles. (as the crow flies)
West Wenatchee is positioned 47.44 degrees north of the equator and 120.35 degrees west of the prime meridian.
West Wenatchee median income is $ 44,028 (2005).
The West Wenatchee median home price is $ 189,300 (2005).

Location

 in central Washington about 60 miles from Yakima adn about 127 miles from Seattle. Other nearby communities include Wenatchee, Sunnyslope, East Wenatchee, South Wenatchee, East Wenatchee Bench, Malaga, Cashmere and Rock Island.
