CABO SAN LUCAS HORSEBACK RIDING FAQ's
Q: WHAT SHOULD I BRING WHEN GOING HORSEBACK RIDING IN CABO SAN LUCAS?
A: You will want long pants - sounds hot, but they'll protect your legs from rubbing on the horse and the stirrups.  You'll be a lot more comfortable.  Make sure to bring sunblock, you'll need it - the sun in Cabo San Lucas is very strong.  Use at least SPF 30 - preferably even higher.  You'll also want good riding shoes (tennis shoes are fine), and a hat for sun protection.  Don't forget your camera!
Q: I'VE NEVER BEEN HORSEBACK RIDING - CAN I STILL DO THESE TOURS?
A: These tours are suitable for beginners, even kids.  We have horses for all skill levels.
Q: IS THERE A WEIGHT LIMIT?
A: Yes - the weight limit for our horseback riding tours is 250 lbs. or 120kg.
Q: I'M AN EXPERIENCED HORSEBACK RIDER - CAN WE RUN WITH THE HORSES?
A: Yes, if the guide determines that you have sufficient skill level.  We do have liability to think about, of course - can't have you getting thrown off while galloping, so the standards for this are higher than you may be used to at home, but if you qualify you can gallop on the beach.
Q: CAN WE SWIM WITH THE HORSES?
A: No.  There are dangerous riptides lurking under the ocean - while this is a beautiful beach, it is not safe to swim with the horses on El Medano Beach in Los Cabos.
Q: HOW MUCH TIME SHOULD I ALLOW FOR TRAVEL TO AND FROM THE RANCH?
A: The ranch for all tours except the Pacific Beach Ride is right in Cabo San Lucas.  The Pacific Beach Ride takes place about 20 minutes from the marina in Cabo San Lucas.
