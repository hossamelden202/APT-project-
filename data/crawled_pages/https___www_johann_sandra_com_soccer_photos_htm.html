In the table below, you'll find photos from the 2003 Women's World Cup Semi-Finals Match in Portland, OR, which featured both the United States AND Sweden.  We couldn't miss this game, of course!  Sweden won, which was great, before losing to Germany, who whipped the U.S., in the Finals.  We had a great time stuffing our faces with hot dogs, and cheering on the local Portlanders, Tiffeny Milbrett and Shannon MacMillan, both of whom played for the University of Portland soccer team.  Of course, Sandra was reprimanded by the Security staff for being too loud and obnoxious!
