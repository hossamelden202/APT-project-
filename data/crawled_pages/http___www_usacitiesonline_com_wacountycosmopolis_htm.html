Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Cosmopolis is approximately 1,540 (1998), 1649 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 578 (1990), 677 (2010).
The amount of land area in Cosmopolis is 3.18 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0.381 sq kilometers.
The distance from Cosmopolis to Washington DC is 2532 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 43 miles. (as the crow flies)
Cosmopolis is positioned 46.95 degrees north of the equator and 123.76 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Cosmopolis elevation is 11 - 300 feet above sea level.
Cosmopolis average annual rainfall is 84 inches per year
The average winter temperature is 42 degrees F.
The average summer temperature is 70 degrees F.

History & History Related Items

The name Cosmopolis means "city of the world." It is next to the city of Aberdeen.

The founding fathers of Cosmopolis are David Byles, Austin Young, James and Henry Karr and Ed campbell. They began the area's first industry, a brickyard, in Cosmopolis. The site was platted in the early 1860s. It was later incorporated in 1891. The first mayor was George Stetson, who was also the general manager of the Grays Harbor mill.

Attractions

Grays Harbor Historical Seaport - See the historic shipyard which is home to the Lady Washington, flagship of Washington State. It was built right here in Aberdeen and is a reproduction of the ship sailed by Captain Robert Gray on his first voyage. He is the first American to "discover" Grays Harbor.
