Hi, our names are Johann and Sandra.  We have been married for over 25 years, and have spent much of that time traveling in Mexico and over 70 other countries.  We first visited Cancun back on January 1, 2000 for the turn of the new millennium.  What a party that was!  It left such an impression on us that we couldn’t wait to discover more and more about the area.  There was so much to do, so much to discover, and that water…wow!

We liked it so much that after many visits, we launched Cancun Discounts as a way to share money-saving opportunities with other budget-conscious travelers like ourselves.  Today, Cancun Discounts has been online for over 20 years, and is the area’s #1 tours and activities website, serving over 1,000,000 happy customers.  We offer the biggest selection and best prices on tours and excursions that you’ll find anywhere, all backed up by our award-winning customer service and our core value of commitment to excellence. 

Our team is motivated by passion for travel, thirst for adventure, and a love of sharing with others the experiences that have shaped our lives.  We are in the business of creating unforgettable vacation memories  for our guests, and helping you to have the same amazing adventures that we have been so fortunate to enjoy.  We offer our unique brand of adventures throughout Cancun, Playa del Carmen and the Riviera Maya, as well as Cozumel and Costa Maya, and we work tirelessly to ensure you leave your vacation with the most lifetime memories possible.  We hope to have the opportunity to show you the Cancun Discounts difference on your next trip to Mexico!
