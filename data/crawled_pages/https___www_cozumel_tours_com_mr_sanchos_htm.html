Mr. Sancho's Beach Club is one of the premier beach clubs in Cozumel Mexico. MR. SANCHO'S FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I VISIT MR. SANCHOS?
A: Yes! In fact, we highly recommend it as a beach break excursion. Mr. Sancho's is a great place to relax and have a fun day at the beach in Cozumel.
Q: WHERE IS MR. SANCHO'S LOCATED?
A: Inside Cozumel's protected marine park, on the southwest side of the island, on San Francisco Palancar Beach, across from the Occidental Hotel. A taxi will take about 10 minutes from the cruise piers.
Q: CAN I USE SUNSCREEN INSIDE THE BEACH CLUB?
A: Yes, but like everywhere inside Cozumel's marine park, only biodegradable sunscreen is allowed to protect the ecosystem.
