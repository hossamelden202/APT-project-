Rules of Sport

At rulesofsport.com our goal is simple - to explain the basic rules of various sports in a simple, easy to understand way. This site is for anyone who wants to learn about a new sport, brush up on some rules, or even settle an argument in the pub.

To get started, simply select the sport you're interested in from the list below. Currently all of the sports we have rules for are listed on this page, but more are on their way very soon.
