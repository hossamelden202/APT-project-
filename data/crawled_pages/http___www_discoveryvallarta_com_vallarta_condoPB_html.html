Puerto Vallarta Two and Three Bedroom Condos - Beachfront Condo Building Ref. PB
 

PB is another popular condominium building in Puerto Vallarta, with a total of 24 vacation rental condos on six levels. The condos are two or three bedrooms and all are beach-front. The condo building is located almost directly behind the Blue and Green Chairs of the gay beach in Puerto Vallarta. Amenities include elevator, reception desk, a/c in bedrooms only, ceiling fans, fully equipped kitchen, cable television, local telephone service, some units with internet connection, 24 hour security, and a nice sized common area heated pool and sundeck terrace with views of Los Muertos beach. Maid service is generally two (2) days a week, additional service for extra.

This condominium building is located just a short walk or taxi to the main South Side area fine restaurants, shopping and the exciting gay nightlife of Puerto Vallarta bars, lounges and clubs. Popular condos for their location right by the Puerto Vallarta gay beach. Please note the proximity to the ocean-front Blue Chairs gay hotel next door which offers rooftop entertainment in the evenings until approximately 10pm-11pm.

Two bedroom condos are for four (4) guests maximum, three bedroom units for six (6) persons. This condominium building does not have its own beachfront restaurant; PB pool and their beach palapas and chairs are only for use by registered guests. Following check-in all guests will be provided with a security wristband that must be worn at all times during their stay or else a PB condo photo identification card which must be in the guest's possession. The two bedroom vacation rental condos generally run around $215-$280 per night during the winter season, depending on the unit, and around $100-$140 per night during the summer months. Holiday bookings generally with seven (7) night minimum at the holiday rate. No nudity or sexual activity is allowed in any of the public common areas of the PB condo building or on the beach concession area of the condominium. Guests could be asked by the administration to leave the Playa Bonita condos if an infraction of this nature occurs.
