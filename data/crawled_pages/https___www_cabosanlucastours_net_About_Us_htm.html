Hi, our names are Johann & Sandra.  Ever since we spent our second wedding anniversary, back in 1997, in Cabo San Lucas, we have been hooked on Mexico.  We are also avowed tour junkies. Excursions are always the highlight of our own vacations, and turn an ordinary beach vacation into a celebration of the local culture, environment, and people.  When you look back on your vacation in Cabo San Lucas, the activities and tours that you chose will surely be among your most treasured memories.

When we first opened Cabo San Lucas Tours in August 2006, the idea was to provide a service not available in Cabo - the best selection of fun things to do in Cabo, at the best prices possible, with English Speaking, toll-free customer service always available for any questions.  Throw in our our No-Port No-Pay policy for cruise passengers, and we felt like we had a great way to help every visitor to Cabo San Lucas have a better vacation than they may have had otherwise.

Today, we feature a top-notch customer service staff that works tirelessly to ensure you have the best vacation experience possible.  All operations are run locally in Mexico, giving you a local expertise, while all transactions are processed safely through the USA.

We are endorsed by the prestigious American Society of Travel Agents (ASTA) and the International Air Transport Association (IATA), the largest certification agency for the travel industry in the world.  We are licensed travel agents, and are fully insured for your safety.
No worries, no stress.  You're in good hands!
