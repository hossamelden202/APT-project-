Spartacus Bath House and Sauna

 
South Side/Zona Romantica - 240 Lazaro Cardena at I. Vallarta.  Tel: 178-4299
A large four story sauna and bath house spa for gay and bisexual men in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.
Services include steam room, dry sauna, jacuzzi with massage jets, showers, relaxation rooms,
sun deck with nude sunbathing, video room, a coffee and snack bar,
palapa, pool, massages and a smoking area.
Lockers and private rooms are available. Free wireless internet.

Located directly across the street from the lively gay night club C.C. Slaughters
   and in the next block up from Paco's Ranch - see handy Map below.

As of Oct 2017 the general admission is 200-400 pesos, depending on whether you want a locker, cabin or vip.
Membership is 500 pesos per month, which then allows for discounts.

Check out their Spa Spartacus website for more information and photos.
Social: https://www.facebook.com/spartacusp

For Gay, Bisexual and open minded men 18+
Gay Sauna bath house Spartacus is the prime Men Only Sauna complex in Old Town in romantic Vallarta.
This 4,000+ square foot sauna complex spreads over four (4) floors with a large rooftop terrace, pool and Palapa
for the sun lovers, smokers and sunbathing.

Important Regulations & Rules
- Minimum age to visit Sauna Spartacus in Puerto Vallarta is 18 years.
- The sauna does not tolerate, promote or endorse rent boys in their facilities, and does not allow these activities.
- Promotes itself also as a drug-free zone.

Hours of operation: Sun-Thurs, 4pm-2am; Fri and Sat, 4pm-9am
