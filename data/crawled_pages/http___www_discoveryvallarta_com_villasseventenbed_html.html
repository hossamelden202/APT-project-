Important Information

Rates: Rates are quoted in US dollars per night. Quoted rates generally include all taxes, amenities and maid service. Maid and staff tips are not included, so please remember to be generous to the staff as I am sure that you will receive good, if not excellent, service. Note: Until confirmed, all rates on this website are subject to change without notice.

Payment Terms: Villa rentals are payable by cashier check or official bank money order, no credit cards (except through Paypal), no personal or business checks or Post Office money orders accepted in Mexico. In order to confirm your reservation, a 50% Non-Refundable Deposit needs to arrive within five-seven days. The final payment is generally due 35-65 days before arrival; the time varies from villa to villa. (Many villa owners now consider the initial deposit to be non-refundable unless the villa can be re-rented for the exact same time period and even if it is there is a service charge). Accommodations are subject to availability until your reservation is confirmed. Most villas charge a refundable security deposit, which is held in case of damages and sent back within a week of your departure, though some deposits have to be held until the next phone bill arrives. Maid service is usually six days per week, Sunday is almost always the staff day off, and national holidays. If you want a written confirmation of your rental or a rental agreement, please ask, otherwise the email correspondence and confirmation are considered sufficient.

Arrival Information: Please note that in the deposit instructions and confirmation letters, we ask that you send us your airline name, flight numbers and the arrival and departure times. This is for our records and so that the villa staff/manager can know when to expect you and thus be on hand to greet you. It is also helpful for me to have a home/work phone number in case I need to contact you regarding the villa reservation.

Tipping: Tipping of the household employees is voluntary although customary in Mexico. Tipping is always appreciated. The amount of the tip would of course depend on the service required, the length of your stay, the number of guests in your party and any special needs you might have had. To provide assistance in this matter, note that the standard tip for the staff in Puerto Vallarta villas is now considered $2-4 per day per staff member for each guest, and tips are generally given to the staff at the end of your stay.  Please be kind enough to take good tipping into consideration while planning your vacation in Vallarta.

Check in - Generally the check in time for Puerto Vallarta villa vacation rentals is 3-4pm and check out is 11am-noon.
