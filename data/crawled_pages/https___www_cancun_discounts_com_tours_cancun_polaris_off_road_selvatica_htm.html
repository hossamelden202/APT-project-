OFF-ROAD & ZIP EXTREME COMBO

ADULTS: $169.00  $149.00 KIDS (8-15): $89.00  $79.00 - SAVE 10% !

The ultimate off-road adventure! Six different adventures all in one exhilarating tour!  Fly on zip lines, traverse aerial suspension bridges, off-road thrills in your Polaris RZR, zip into a cenote where you can swim, and of course the extreme parachute jump (optional)!

Your tour begins with the world-famous Extreme Canopy Tour, one of Travel & Leisure's 35 great adventures of the world.  Fly high above the ground on a series of 10 exciting zip lines, even upside down or like Superman!

Traverse a series of suspension bridges between the treetops, and parachute from a treehouse!  Now take a Polaris RZR for a spin through an exciting obstacle course and jungle trails before cooling off with a zip line into a freshwater cenote and a refreshing swim!
