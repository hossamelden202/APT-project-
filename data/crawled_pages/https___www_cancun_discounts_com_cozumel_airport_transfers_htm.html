Q: I'm taking the Cozumel ferry.  Do you offer transfers to/from the pier?
A: Yes.  You can use our service for transportation both from the Cozumel ferry pier to your Cozumel hotel, and from your hotel to the ferry pier.  You can book this service by clicking "Book Now" above.  Just write your ferry arrival and departure information instead of the airline information.  No extra cost.

Q: I'm staying at a private home, condo, or villa.  Can I use your service?
A: Yes, we provide transportation to all private homes, condominiums, villas, hotels, and vacation rentals.

Q: How will I find my driver?  What if my flight is delayed?
A: You will see our representative holding a sign with your name on it, or the company logo.  We will provide you with a toll-free telephone number to call if there are any issues at all.  We will track your flight, and make sure that we're there to get you as agreed, even if you are delayed.  That's part of the service we provide.

Q: How will the driver know where and when to pick me up?
A: You'll tell us your flight arrival and departure information when booking - we'll be there to pick you up at the airport and we'll let you know when to be out front of your hotel when you're heading home.

Q: I'm arriving at the Cancun Airport - can you pick me up there?
A: Yes!  We offer shared shuttle service from the Cancun Airport to Cozumel Hotels and back for just $63.00 per person, each way.  This includes pick-up at the Cancun Airport, transfers to the Playa del Carmen Ferry Pier, round-trip ferry tickets (a $36.00 per person value), and transfer from the Cozumel Ferry Pier to your Cozumel hotel, and the same on the return.  More Information and Booking

Q: Do you have child car seats?
A:  You can request a car set rental in advance. Kids in a car seat pay per person price because they are using a seat.  Kids under 1 year old sitting on a parents lap and then they are free.
