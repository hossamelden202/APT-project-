VICTORIA ATTRACTIONS

During out trips to Victoria, we haven't been fortunate enough to visit all the attractions we'd like to, but that just leaves more for next time!

Most of Victoria's attractions are natural or historical.  The ambience of the town is attraction enough!  People come from all over the world to sip a cup of tea at the Empress Hotel, or to wander through the splendor of Butchart Gardens.  

Whale watching is perhaps the most popular activity on Vancouver Island, with dozens of boats leaving port each day.  We've found that you usually see gray, minke, or killer whales, and there are lots of these!  We've seen whales on every trip we've been on, at least a tail here or there.

The historical, victorian atmosphere still abounds, and it's about as close to England as you can get without being there.  Sandra's family especially enjoyed shopping in the city.
