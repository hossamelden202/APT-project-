MAUI SNORKELING TOURS
MOLOKINI SNORKELING TOURS

Maui is as beautiful underwater as it is above.   When snorkeling on Maui, Dazzling displays of butterfly fish, angelfish, tang, and grunts surround you, with needlefish, flounder, and crabs providing unusual- looking sights.   The water is warm year-round, though in winter you might want to rent a wetsuit if you plan on snorkeling for very long.  However, if you get cold, it's a good excuse to sun yourself on the gorgeous Maui beaches! 

The best Maui snorkeling can be found offshore at Molokini Crater.  A submerged volcano, the crater has been declared a Marine Life Conservation District, and fish and sealife abound. 

The Maui snorkeling tours below will take you to see the wonderful displays of fish and beautiful underwater ecosystem of Maui.
