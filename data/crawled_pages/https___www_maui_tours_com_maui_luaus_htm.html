MAUI LUAUS
* HAWAII'S BEST LUAU *

Maui has some of the best luaus in Hawaii. 

The Royal Lahaina Luau at the Royal Lahaina resort is one of the most popular.  The Royal Lahaina Luau can be paired with other exciting or romantic activities, such as snorkeling, submarine adventures, or a sunset dinner cruise.

You can't say you've been to Hawaii without at least attending one luau! 

What of the Maui Luaus are you interested in?  Click on the appropriate Maui Luau below:

Molokini Snorkeling and Luau Combo
Explore the undersea wonders of Molokini Island and Olowalu Turtle Reef and enjoy an unforgettable evening under the stars at the Royal Lahaina Luau!
   

Maui Submarine Adventure & Royal Lahaina Luau Combo
Marvel at Maui’s unique underwater paradise aboard a high-tech 48-passenger submarine, as featured in National Geographic television specials and enjoy an unforgettable evening under the stars at the Royal Lahaina Luau!
   

Sunset Dinner Cruise and Luau Combo
Cruise the calm west Maui coastline and take in spectacular sunset views aboard an elegant luxury yacht and enjoy an unforgettable evening under the stars at the Royal Lahaina Luau!
