CABO SAN LUCAS AIRPORT TRANSPORTATION FAQ's
Q: HOW FAR IN ADVANCE CAN I RESERVE AN AIRPORT TRANSFER?
A: We need you to book by 24 hours in advance of your arrival time in Cabo, so we have time to make the arrangements.  However, please try to give us as much notice as possible - it sure makes things easier. If you'll be arriving tomorrow, please call us to make your reservation toll-free at 1-877-828-2564. 
Q: HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO MY HOTEL?
A: Once you choose Private or Shared transfers in the boxes above, you will see a table on the right side of the page that will show you the zone that each hotel in Cabo is in.  The rates for each zone are shown, so it should be pretty explanatory.  However, if you don't see your hotel there you can always ask us, we go anywhere in the Baja.
Q: I NEED TO GO TO A LOCATION THAT IS NOT LISTED.  DO YOU GO THERE?
A: Yes.  It's just not always possible to list every single place.  However, if you don't see your hotel listed in the hotel rate tables on the shared and private transfers pages, you can always ask us, we go anywhere in the Baja.
Q: I'M ALREADY IN CABO, CAN YOU TAKE ME BACK TO THE AIRPORT?
A: Due to taxi union restriction, one-way departure transfers (hotel to airport) can only be arranged if you make a reservation at least 72 hours prior to your scheduled departure time, if not, you would need to use a taxi for departure-only transfers.
Q: CAN YOU TAKE ME TO MY HOTEL AND THEN RETURN ME FROM ANOTHER HOTEL?
A: Yes, if your hotel is listed as being available for round-trip transfers, then we can do this, you'll just specify the change of hotel when booking.  If the second hotel is in a different zone,  you would just book two one-way transfers and we will convert it to a round-trip transfer.
Q: DO YOU HAVE KIDS PRICES? DO I HAVE TO PAY FOR MY INFANT?
A: Unlike most transportation companies in Cabo San Lucas and San Jose del Cabo, we do have kids prices for all our shared transfers.
Q: HOW WILL I RECOGNIZE YOUR DRIVER?
A: Your driver will be with burgundy shirt with khaki pants and a sign, whether with your name or EPIC Logo.
Q: DO YOU OFFER TRANSPORTATION WHILE I'M IN CABO SAN LUCAS TOO, OR JUST THE AIRPORT?
A: We also offer a private chauffeur service that will take you wherever you want to go.  This is a great service to take your own private tour to La Paz, Todos Santos, San Jose del Cabo, or Cabo San Lucas for the day, or especially if you're staying in the Corridor and want to go into town for the night or just want to see the area.
Q: HOW FAR FROM THE LOS CABOS AIRPORT IS SAN JOSE DEL CABO?
A: The Los Cabos Airport is located approximately 8 miles (13km) north of San Jose del Cabo.
Q: HOW FAR FROM THE LOS CABOS AIRPORT IS CABO SAN LUCAS?
A: Cabo San Lucas is located approximately 29 miles (48km) southeast of the Los Cabos Airport.
Q:  I'M STAYING AT A PRIVATE HOME.  DO YOU GO THERE?
A: Yes. You will need to book a Private transfer.
Q: WILL MY DRIVER SPEAK ENGLISH? WHAT ABOUT IF I CALL YOU - DO YOU SPEAK ENGLISH?
A: Yes.  Everyone on the telephone speaks English fluently and so will your driver.
Q: I HAVE A GROUP OF MORE THAN 10 PEOPLE.  DO YOU HAVE LARGER VEHICLES?
A: Yes.  We have several sizes of luxury motorcoaches.  If you require a larger vehicle, please just give us a call toll-free at 1-877-828-2564 or e-mail us and we'll set you up.
