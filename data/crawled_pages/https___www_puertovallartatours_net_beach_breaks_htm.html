Puerto Vallarta Beach Break FAQ's
Q: ARE THESE EXCURSIONS OPEN TO CRUISE SHIP PASSENGERS?
A: Yes, in fact, these tours are highly recommended for cruise passengers that are looking for a relaxing day on the beach to complement their days on the ship. 

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE - HOW FAR IS IT TO THE DEPARTURE POINT?
A: All these cruises depart from the Marina Maritima, the same place as your cruise ship.  Just walk on over.

Q: WILL MY CRUISE SHIP ARRIVE IN TIME TO DO THESE TOURS?
A: If you arrive at 9:00AM or earlier, the boat will wait for you.  If you arrive at 9:30 AM or later, then Las Caletas will be your only option.  Make sure to allow an hour after the tour for return to your ship, and if you have any questions, just give us a call - we're always happy to help.

Q: WHAT IS THE WATER TEMPERATURE IN PUERTO VALLARTA?
A: During the summer months, the water is bathtub warm, approaching 85?F.  During the winter months, it cools down significantly, and you often find temperatures dipping into the mid-to-high 60s.

Q: HOW ARE THESE TOURS DIFFERENT FROM THE BEACH BREAK OFFERED BY MY CRUISE SHIP?
A: Other than the price, many cruise ships offer a beach break at a timeshare resort in Puerto Vallarta, where you can use the facilities of the hotel.  On this beach break, you are transported by bus to a crowded beach with hundreds of other cruise ship passengers, where you are greeted by timeshare salesmen.  With these tours, you take a beautiful cruise along Banderas Bay, followed by spending the day on a remote private beach, where only people from your tour are allowed, with no timeshare sales - just relaxation and fun.
