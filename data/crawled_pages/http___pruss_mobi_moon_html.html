Android lunar maps

LunarMap HD and LunarMap Lite include some of the maps below, and have search and other features.

Printable lunar maps

1. Notes

  • These lunar maps are automatically generated from USGS Map-a-Planet data together with IAU lunar feature data. The projections are done with a modified version of Matthew's Map Projection Software, and then labels and ellipses are added with a PerlMagick script.
  • As far as I know, USGS products are in the public domain as a work of the U.S. Government, mere data is not copyrightable, and I claim no copyright on the output of my map-generation scripts. So the maps should be public domain. Please feel free to use and reproduce as you wish.
  • All the marked maps show at least 660 features, with the higher resolution ones showing more.
  • For maps generated by the same scripts but with your custom lunar feature markings, go here.
