The Swedish Country Inn

Lindsborg, Kansas, USA

Region: Central Kansas, 65 miles north of Wichita

VALKOMMEN! This Swedish welcome will greet our guests as they enter the Swedish Country Inn located in Lindsborg, Kansas, "Little Sweden USA." In keeping with the authentic Swedish theme of the town, the furnishings were imported from Sweden. Hand-stitched quilts top our Swedish Pine beds. Bicycles, tandem and individual, are available to our guests. You will also enjoy our relaxing redwood sauna. Located in downtown Lindsborg, the Inn is within walking distance of many quaint shops and restaurants.

Our dining room serves a full Swedish buffet. Pretend you are in Sweden as you sample herring, cheeses, muffins, Swedish tea rings, knackbrod, meatballs, lingonberries and other delicacies. Our attractive lobby, a most inviting spot for casual conversation or group meetings serves complimentary beverages and assorted Swedish delicacies.

For a unique experience, try our Inn and see if you don't agree, it's a "Touch of Sweden."

The Swedish Country Inn has 19 rooms all with private baths, all non-smoking.

Your Host: Becky Anderson, (owner)
