Q: Should I do a shared or private transfer?
A: If you are one or two people, it will usually be cheaper for you to take a shared transfer.  These transfers fill up quickly and only take a few other people with hotels close to yours.  For more than two people, it is worth it to take a private transfer.  The price difference is minimal, and you have express transfer to and from your hotel. However, if you are staying in a private residence, condo, Airbnb or anything but a hotel, then you will need to take our private transfers since our shared shuttle takes you to hotels only.

Q: Can you take me from the Cancun Airport to my Cozumel hotel?
A: Yes! We now offer an option for shared transfers from the Cancun Airport all the way to your Cozumel hotel and back, including ferry tickets. However, due to Ferry schedules, your arrival needs to be before 6:30 PM and your departure after 10:00 AM. You can see that option above - it's the last rate listed. We also offer shared transfers from the Cozumel Airport as well.

Q: How will the driver know where and when to pick me up?
A: You'll tell us your flight arrival and departure information when booking - we'll be there to pick you up at the airport and we'll let you know when to be out front of your hotel when you're heading home.

Q: How will I find my driver?  What if my flight is delayed?
A: Your driver will be easily identified by holding a sign. We'll send you complete details with your reservation. You'll supply us with your flight arrival and departure time - it's our job to track the flight and make sure we're there when you are - don't worry, this is what we do.

Q: Can you do transfers within the area (not just to/from the airport)?
A: Yes, we can do that for you.  However, this service is only available with our private transfers.

Q: How many bags can I bring? Do you charge for additional luggage?
A: We estimate 1 bag and 1 carry-on per passenger. However, you can bring as many as you like for FREE (even golf clubs!). You just need to notify us in the special instructions box when you make your reservation to ensure we take a big enough shuttle.

Q: Do you have child seats? Do you charge the same for kids?
A: Kids pay the same price as adults because they occupy the same amount of seats. Kids from 0 to 2 years old will require a child seat. We can provide it for no additional charge, just let us know! Please choose the option when booking and specify your kid's age on the special instructions box.

Q: Can I bring my bicycle to Cancun?
A: Yes, you can! If you book a shared transfer, you will need to purchase an additional ticket so that we can accommodate enough room for your bike.
