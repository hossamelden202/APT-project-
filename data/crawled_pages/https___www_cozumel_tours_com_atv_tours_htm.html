ATV EXCURSIONS IN COZUMEL

There is no better way to explore the dense jungles and beautiful but deserted beaches of Cozumel's East shore than on an ATV tour.  The Eastern half of Cozumel is virtually uninhabited, and seldom visited.  There is only one road, and that ends before you reach your destination!  You'll board your ATV and ride through the wilds of Cozumel island, visiting Mayan Villages, snorkeling on remote coral reefs, and even seeing ancient Mayan Ruins and caves!  These ATV tours are great cruise excursions too - only 2 to 4 hours in length, you can see the island and also explore!

ATV TOUR FAQ's
Q: I'VE NEVER RIDDEN AN ATV BEFORE. CAN I STILL DO THE TOUR?
A: Yes, these ATVs are easy to ride, even for first-timers.
Q: ARE ALL ATVS AND DUNE BUGGIES FULLY AUTOMATIC?
A: No, we have automatic and manual ATVs and Dune Buggies.
Q: IS THERE A WEIGHT LIMIT?
A: Yes, the combined weight on an ATV cannot exceed 360 lbs (165kg). There are no weight limits for Dunne Buggy or Xrails.
Q: CAN MY HUSBAND / WIFE AND I RIDE TOGETHER ON ONE ATV?
A: Yes. No problem at all. You'll just want to book the shared option.
Q: WHAT IS THE AGE LIMIT FOR YOUR ATV TOURS? DO I NEED A DRIVER'S LICENSE?
A: Drivers must be at least 16 years of age and have a valid drivers license. Kids 7 years old and up are welcome to be passengers, though.
Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
