Waders and Wading Shoes

Don't you hate it when that last corner falls on the other side of the river?  Or maybe you're shooting the low water line on a waterway, running creek cross-sections, or as-builting a stormwater detention pond.  Chest waders are undoubtedly part of your gear room already.  If not, we've given a wide selection of both men's and women's sizes for that next submerged topo survey!  We've opted to focus on Chest waders rather than hip waders for their versatility and usefulness in the surveying industry.

Scroll down the page to view our selection.  For pricing, reviews,  and more information, click on the image or name of the item you are interested in.

Can't find what you're looking for?  Check under another category, search our site for the title you're looking for, or ask us!
