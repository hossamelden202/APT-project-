Hiking Hazards

I've always wanted to make a big list of stupid ways to die. Things like having a spider drop onto you while driving, causing you to crash your car. Well, I've never made that list (except in my head), so I guess this is the closest thing I'll get to it.

All kidding aside, there are lots of ways to become impaired or even die in the wilderness. Here's a list and discussion of some of them. By the way, Last Breath: Cautionary Tales from the Limits of Human Endurance by Peter Stark is a great book on the subject of death in the wild. Another is Off the Wall: Death in Yosemite, which chronicles the varied ways people have died in the park.

DISCLAIMER: I am not an expert on any of these topics. This is just a list and some basic suggestions. You can find out more about all these subjects on other web sites, or at your local library. And remember -- you are ultimately responsible for your own safety!

Hazards

  • Dehydration
  • Unsafe water
  • Cramping
  • Skin Cancer
  • Blisters
  • Insect Stings
  • Insect-borne illnesses - Lyme Disease, West Nile Virus, etc.
  • Getting Lost
  • High Altitude problems
  • Lightning
  • Car Accidents
  • Falling
  • Getting trapped by a boulder and having to cut your arm off
  • Getting mistaken for a deer and getting shot by a hunter
  • Drowning
  • Bear attack
  • Snake bite
  • Hypothermia
  • Volcano eruption
  • Avalanche
  • Poison Oak
  • Falling Trees and Rocks
