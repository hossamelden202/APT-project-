Hiking & Biking

Get outdoors in the fresh Wisconsin air with a nature hike or bicycle ride around this beautiful state. There are many State Parks to explore as well as bike trails, including the Elroy-Sparta State Trail. While on your hiking or biking adventure, you can identify plants and wild animals that are native to Wisconsin as well as enjoy the natural scenery of this great state.
