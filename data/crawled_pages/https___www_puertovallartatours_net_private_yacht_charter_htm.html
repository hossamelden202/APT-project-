Step aboard our newly upgraded 55? Private Catamaran for ultimate cruising comfort and style. With two spacious decks offering both sun-soaked and shaded areas, this luxurious yacht is perfect for relaxing or getting adventurous. Whether enjoying a peaceful sail or plunging into the water from one of the two waterslides and jumping platform, this tour elevates your Puerto Vallarta experience.

Fully equipped for fun, this private tour lets you set the pace. Your boat, your captain, your crew—it’s all customizable. Want to explore Sayulita? Or maybe take a leisurely cruise around the stunning Marietas Islands? It’s your call! With an All-Inclusive experience, you’ll enjoy premium water toys, expert guidance, and personalized service, making every moment unforgettable.

Sip and savor with our Premium Open Bar, offering top-shelf brands and a wide selection of drinks to suit every taste. Start your day with a continental breakfast and enjoy all-day snacks like fresh ceviche, guacamole, and chips. It’s your private oasis on the water, offering everything you need for an extraordinary day at sea.
