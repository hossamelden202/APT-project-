Puerto Vallarta Jungle Buggy Tour FAQ's

Q: WILL MY BUGGY BE AN AUTOMATIC?
A:  Yes. All jungle buggies are automatic transmission.

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - DO YOU PICK UP AT THE PIER?
A: No, the cruise ships have contracts with certain companies who pay big money to be able to pick up at the pier - that's why the cruise excursions are so much more expensive.  To get to the tour meeting location, just take a taxi from the pier about ten minutes to Puerto Vallarta. 

Q: DO YOU HAVE DUNE BUGGIES STILL OR ONLY JUNGLE BUGGIES? 
A: There are no more off-road dune buggies in Puerto Vallarta. They have been replaced by the more durable jungle buggies.    

Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
