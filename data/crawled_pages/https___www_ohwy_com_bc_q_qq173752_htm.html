The Fairmont Waterfront

Search Vancouver British Columbia

The Fairmont Waterfront offers 489 rooms in a structure of 23 floors for visitors to Vancouver. The nearest airport is Vancouver International Airport. Facilities include 24-hour security, a fitness center, a sauna, both a dining room and lounge and a business center. Amenities include data ports and a safe. Both pets and children are accepted. Those who prefer will find family rooms available.
