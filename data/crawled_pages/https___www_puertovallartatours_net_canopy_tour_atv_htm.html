Two exciting adventures in one great tour!  Combine the aerial thrills of the Rio Cuale River Canopy Tour  with the off-road fun of an ATV tour for a half-day excursion that will leave you grinning. 

The tour starts as you ride 11 of the highest and fastest zip lines in Puerto Vallarta, with magnificent views of the rain forest and thrills that will leave you exhilarated with adrenaline. Ride back to the starting location on the back of a mule (optional, about 15 minutes) while enjoying the mountain scenery along the trail. After the zip lines, you can sample different types of tequila or order food or a margarita from the on-site restaurant.

Then, you'll ride your ATV up the Cuale River into the foothills, where you'll have great vistas of the Sierra Madre mountain range. We'll make a short stop at a waterfall to relax and swim in the cool waters of the pool below before riding to the canopy tour location.

See Also: Cuale Mountain Expedition

Cruise Passengers:  Yes!  This tour is available for a cruise shore excursion!

Restrictions: Must be 18 years old to drive the ATV. Maximum weight for the zipline and solo ATV is 264 lbs (120 kg). Maximum weight per shared ATV is 396 lbs (180 kg). Pregnant women, people with asthma, heart, or back conditions are not allowed to participate. People under the influence of alcohol or drugs won't be allowed on the tour. For safety reasons, cameras are not allowed.
