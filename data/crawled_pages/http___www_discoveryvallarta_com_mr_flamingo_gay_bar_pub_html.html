Mr. Flamingo Cocktail and Martini Bar
 

South Side - 255 Ignacio Vallarta at Lazaro Cardena street
Mister Flamingo is the newest kid on the block, 
right at the intersection of Cardenas and I. Vallarta street in the middle of the gay district, 
so right next door are La Margarita and La Noche
and just down the street is popular nightclub Paco's Ranch

 Mr. Flamingo (facebook) is a stylish gay cocktail and martini bar which
features good drink prices and an upscale pub ambience; Popular 

Flamingo's is open to the street so it's fun people watching at night. Some eats available too.

Happy hour, 2pm-7pm
Daily, 2pm-3am 

Business opened late January 2016
Same owners as The Swedes restaurant and bistro on Olas Altas street,
with Rikard and Morgan.
