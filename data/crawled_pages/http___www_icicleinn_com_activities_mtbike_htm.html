About Us

Welcome to your future family vacation for years to come

Learn More

Gather the kids and the dog and escape to beautiful Leavenworth, a little slice of Bavaria nestled in the magnificent Cascade Mountains.

Experience an endless array of activities at Icicle Village Resort. Savor delicious, Northwest-inspired cuisine at JJ Hills Restaurant & Bar, enjoy a rejuvenating afternoon at Alpine Spa, or break out the putters at our fun-themed mini-golf course. Savor an incredible wine-tasting experience from our friends at Rocky Pond Winery, and experience a cornerstone of our outstanding wine-producing region in central Washington. Or, add some extra thrills to your stay and head across the street to Leavenworth Adventure Park, complete with an alpine coaster, a climbing wall, mining games, and more.

When you’re done with the day’s activities, retreat to our hotel and enjoy all the comforts of home, including complimentary breakfast, high-speed Wi-Fi, year-round heated pool, hot tub, and game room.

So join us in Leavenworth and leave ordinary days far behind in the city. We promise an incredible getaway of fun and relaxation in a town steeped with delightful German décor and gorgeous mountain scenery. And an increase in “are we there yets” on your return trip. We can promise that too.
