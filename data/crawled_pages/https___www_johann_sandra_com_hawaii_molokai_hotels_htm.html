Where To Stay In Molokai

Click here to save up to 70% on Hotel and Vacation Rentals!

Although there are few places to stay on Molokai, the different genres of lodging are well represented.   Luxury hotels, affordable accomodations, bed & breakfasts, resort condominiums, vacation rentals, and bungalows are all represented here.

There are only a handful of true hotels on the island, in stark contrast to it's neighbor island, Maui.  The Sheraton Molokai Ranch and Beach Resort  is the newest and largest hotel on the island, and Hotel Molokai offers oceanfront dining.   For information on camping on Molokai, visit our
Camping on Molokai page.  Traveling in off-season will drop your rates
sharply, as well.
