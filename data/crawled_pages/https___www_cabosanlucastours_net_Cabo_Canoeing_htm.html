Departing from world famous Medano Beach in Cabo San Lucas, you will paddle your 5-person rented canoe out to the icon of Los Cabos - Land's End. You will paddle past the yachts and cruise ships parked off the beach (an impressive sight) on your quest for Land's End.

You can pass all the famous landmarks like El Arco (The Arch), Lovers Beach, Neptune's Finger, the Sea Lion Colony and Pacific Windows, giving you some magnificent up close photo opportunities. You can also park your canoes on legendary Lovers Beach, where you can walk from the Sea of Cortez to the Pacific Ocean in only a few steps, and take a break for some hiking and relaxing, followed by a snorkeling stop at Pelican Rock. Relaxing fun in Cabo and good exercise too!


Cruise Passengers: Water Taxi from the marina to the Medano Beach is provided for $5.00 per person round-trip, paid in cash when you board the boat.
