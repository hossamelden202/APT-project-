LOCATION: 
Casa Rafael's is located in beautiful Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico. The warm, dry desert climate averages 85 degrees all year long, with 355 days of sunshine. Casa Rafael's is 100 yards from the Medano Beach which is the most popular sandy beach in Cabo San Lucas, and is a short walk into town for shopping and playing.
 

DESCRIPTION: 
Casa Rafael's was originally a single family home that has been added onto over the years and has recently experienced a major renovation from top to bottom. We call ourselves a "Boutique Hotel." We have ten rooms, swimming pool, 24 hour security, tropical birds, fish and lush landscaping.  We have a fine dining restaurant, piano bar & cigar room.

ROOM  AMENITIES: 
Mini-bars, security safes, blow dryers, luxurious terry cloth robes, air conditioning, daily maid service, room service, coffee makers and special herbal shampoo. 

CONCIERGE SERVICES: 
Full concierge services for fishing, boating, golfing, horseback riding, scuba diving, parasailing, car rentals, out-back tours, snorkeling, tennis and entertainment are available through the front desk.
 

TELEPHONES: 
We have a telephone available for collect or credit card calls.  We also have wireless Internet & E-mail access, a fax machine and bilingual secretarial services available for your convenience at the front desk.

GUEST SERVICES: 
The hotel has a doctor on call who is available for day or night emergencies. Masseuse services are available along with beauty salon (appointment required). We have laundry facilities, beach towels, beach chairs, umbrellas and box lunches.
 

RESTAURANT:
Casa Rafael's is proud to offer to our hotel guests and the public a wonderful dining experience in our elegant and romantic restaurant. We offer two separate dining areas. The Ocean Room, where you will be nestled comfortably while you experience the tranquil ambiance of one of the largest salt water aquariums in Mexico, or Poolside where you can stargaze between courses.  For those of you with demanding palates, Our chef  will delight your taste buds.  And for those with special dietary requirements he will be happy to accommodate the most rigorous diets with prior notice.  The Cigar Room, where after dinner you can adjourn to enjoy a fine Cuban cigar to complement your choice of a fine brandy, port, sherry or cognac.  The Piano Bar is waiting to soothe your ears and entertain you with your favorite piano tunes.  Whether you stay with us or not you owe yourself an experience at our restaurant.

Casa Rafael's has the most extensive wine list in all of Los Cabos with over 120 wines and champagnes to chose from, from Italy, France, California, Spain, Mexico, Chile and Germany. We also have the most extensive list of Brandies, Cognacs, Ports and Liqueurs to choose from for your after dinner pleasure.  Casa Rafael's is proud to offer you the finest and freshest Cuban cigars from the most famous cigar houses in Cuba. We carry Cohiba, Partagas, Monte Cristo, Troya, and more. We will be happy to show you our humidors.
