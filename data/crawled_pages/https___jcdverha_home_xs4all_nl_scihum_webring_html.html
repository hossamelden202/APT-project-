Science Humor Netring

Information

Welcome to the Science humor web ring. This ring is intended to join web sites with humor about science and scientists, so that you can wander forever along the science humor sites.

The official charter for this ring is: The Science humor web ring is a ring dedicated to humor about science or scientists. This includes all kind of sciences, physical or social. In this ring you can find out, what ancient language gave rise to the chemical nomenclature, what little girl professor Wiener asked where he lived, what happens if you eat Cesium, subversive uses of molecular model sets, why you should NOT allow your children to study chemistry, the real reason the chicken crossed the road, why 10 dimensions seem so cramped, inside radiology secrets, that polymer dispersities < 1 can be achieved, the newly discovered evolutionary transition between car and aeroplane, In this ring you can find out what money is, all about the H-O (Huckster-Orlon) theory of trade, what was on the secret 1989 pentagram tape, which bears have rings, how not to write a scientific paper and how the history of the Universe can be summarized in 200 words or less, ...what cell death enthusiasts are reading for pleasure these days and many other things.

A web ring is a series of linked sites about the same subject. The sites are linked with [next] and [previous] links to "neighboring" sites in the ring. If you follow the [next] link long enough you will end at the site you started, thus circling the whole ring. As an extra, people can also visit the list of ring sites, go to a random site, or visit the ring homepage (like this page). It is another way to find sites about a subject, next to link directories and search engines.

On this homepage of the Science Humor netring you can

  1. Visit the ring
  2. Join the ring
  3. Nominate somebody elses site for the ring
  4. Report a problem with the ring
  5. Alter the information about your website in the ring, find back your ringcode or password, look at statistics from the ring

If you wish to know more about how the ring was created and how big it is you can look at the history of the Science Humor netring
