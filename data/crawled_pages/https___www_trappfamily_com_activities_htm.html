Activities All Year Long

You’ll never run out of things to do at von Trapp Family Lodge & Resort. Our Stowe resort is a 2,600-acre mecca for the outdoor and nature enthusiast, with seasonal activities ranging from high-energy skiing and mountain biking to more relaxed bird watching and von Trapp family history tours. With the fabled Stowe village (and all that unique shopping) and the state’s highest mountain only minutes away, the best of Vermont's winter, spring, summer, and fall outdoor activities are all at your doorstep when you stay with us.

Call us for equipment rental availability at 802-253-5744
