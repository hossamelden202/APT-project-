Land Surveying Links

This page offers links to general surveying information and surveying in the Portland/Vancouver area.  You can also find links to all the state boards of registration and licensure and all the state associations of professional surveyors.  It is the intent of this page to provide basic, general surveying information.  For more in-depth coverage of Oregon, Washington, and the Pacific Northwest, see our pages on SW Washington Surveying or Portland/Metro Surveying.   Also, don't miss our Surveying Bookstore, the largest dedicated source of Land Surveying books on the web!  You can find books for PLS/LSIT exam preparation, history, law, GPS, GIS, AutoCAD, and more!
