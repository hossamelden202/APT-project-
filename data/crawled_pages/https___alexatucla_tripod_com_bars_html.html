Los Angeles Bar Guide
Following Are Listings For Some Of L.A.'s Best Bars, Make Sure To Check Out The Cheap Drinks Page For Special Happenings(Link Below)

1235 North Vine
1235 N.Vine - Hollywood
Great little cozy spot with some good food, interesting Saki Cocktails and great DJ Nights, mostly Thursday-Saturday.

4100 Bar
4100 Sunset Blvd - Silverlake
"Silverlake is a place that is open to everyone, gay and straight and so are we" say the owners of 4100, "Everyone is welcome here". So, check out the cleverly named, hip, mixed bar that now sits where the old Detour used to be.

3 OF CLUBS
1123 Vine Street - Hollywood
Cool neighbourhood Bar, some night with D.J.'s, always a crowd!

ACAPULCO RESTAURANTS - LOS ANGELES
You can find over 10 Acapulcos all over town, usually at great locations. Although it is a chain restaurant, each and every one has it's own distinguished character and crowd like the Silverlake Acapulcos is the party place, Fridays Karaoke's are insane and it turns into a club on the weekend. Burbank Acapulcos is going wild on Mondays for Karaoke.
Confused?! Dont be, all of them offer the best Happy Hour In Town: $2.25 Margaritas on Monday nights -Packed! Tue - Fri(4-7PM): $3.95 Margaritas, $1.oo Hand Made chicken/steak tacos(Oh so good!)& half priced Appetizers aswell Location Info @ www.acapulcorestaurants.com

ALTERKNIT LOUNGE
7021 Hollywood Blvd - Hollywood
The little Bar inside the Knitting Factory offers some great drink specials during happy hour and dancenights after dark

AKBAR
4356 Sunset blvd - Silverlake
Hmmm, how to describe this place...if you like Silverlake, you will like it, a very "Alternative " crowd, gay/str8, pretty much everything & everyone frequents this bar.
Now with a dancefloor on Friday and Saturdays!

ABBEY
Robertson Blvd - West Hollywood
By far L.A.'s most beautiful bar! Indoor/Oudoor, Cabanas, Lounges, Fountains. Just a great spot! In the daytime this is a very calm and relaxing place with some surprisingly good food and a ton of celebrity sightings!(I saw Britney and Christina there at the same time for lunch, although on opposite ends of the bar, lol)
Come Nightfall, the wild crowd comes out, gay, str8, bi, you name it. Tons of str8 girls with there gay friends, that's for sure!
To enhance the drama, The Abbey expanded again and added Icebar. A additional area with bars and places to lounge, see and be seen

ARSENAL
12012 W.Pico Blvd - Santa Monica
What used to be a run down dive bar is now one of the westside hippest spots! Comfy loveseats on the patio brings in the "smokin" crowd!

THE ASSOCIATION
610 S.Mainstreet - Downtown L.A.
(Entrance on 6th Street)
LOcated inside the Pacific Electric Lofts, the area can still feel a little shady after dark, but that makes it somewhat even more exciting. You really feel like you enter some sort of 1920's prohibition wateringhole.
The place is very comfortable, intimate and mostly frequented by the "in the know" hip crowd. Some Dj NIghts and food aswell!

BALBOA @ THE GRAFTON
8462 Sunset Blvd - West Hollywood
A well designed little hip Bar on the Strip, see if you can figuere out the names of rock stars embedded in a wall-artwork.

BAR 107
107 4th Street - Downtown L.A.
Located right smack in the middle of the beautiful restored Historic Core in Downtown L.A., Bar 107 is a gem. As soon as you walk in, you feel like you left L.A.and are in a cool bar in NYC or Chicago. Friendly vibe, great 20/30 something crowd, a killer jukebox and a little dancefloor for DJ Night's! Worth the drive to downtown.

BARCOPA
2810 Main Street - Santa Monica
Fairly new, hip and happening bar on the Westside with some fun DJ Nights too!

BAR LUBITSCH
7702 Santa Monica Blvd - West Hollywood
A russion inspired hip bar and club with red walls is deffinatly high up on the hip and celeb radar now! Incredible vodka drinks are the specialty here and DJ's start spinning and filling up the little dancefloor usually after 10 pm

BAR MARMONT
8171 Sunset Blvd - West Hollywood
A stunning, even historic place to take out of towners, always some Celebs to see, incredible expensive, but you are in L.A. to endulge, right?!

BAR NINETEEN 12
9641 Sunset Blvd - Beverly Hills
This new bar/lounge inside the plush Beverly Hilld Hotel sure has the sexy and money spending crowd out and about almost each and every day. So bring your credit cards, you will need them here. $18 Mojito(!)Nough said!

BEAUTY BAR
1638 Cahuenga Blvd - Hollywood
This bar actually looks like a beauty parlor and YES, you can get a manicure here, some dope d/j/night's as well, check the Bar-Promotions Site for more info

BIGFOOD LODGE
3172 Los Feliz Blvd - Silverlake
Always something going on, DJ's, Live Bands. Mixed & Very thirsty crowd.

BIG WANGS
1562 N.Cahuenga Blvd - Hollywood
Perfect little Sports Bar in the ever-so-hot Cahuenga Corridor in Hollywood.
This bar brings out the hip crowd!
Perfectly situated, walking distance, to all the major clubs and with cheap daily drink specials, you will always have a good time!

BLUE MONKEY BAR
5521 Hollywood Blvd - Hollywood
Somehow the "Trendy" Folks always discover a cool bar first, even if it is in a more shady part of Hollywood.
Girls and Boys in there early 20's, mix and mingle to some nice house tunes. Some Nights the DJ's play Drum n'Bass or Hip Hop!
Not really a danceclub, more a place to mix, mingle and making new friends

BOARDNERS
1652 N.Cherokee - Hollywood
One of Hollywoods best lept secrets, this bar/danceclub is a hot spot with the local crowd. After 60 years as a local dive in the heart of Hollywood, Boardners expanded with a beautiful, huge outdoor patio and a dancefloor/Vip lounge next door. The crowd is mostly unpretentious, several nights have different promotions, check www.boardners.com for info!

BODEGA WINEBAR
814 Broadway - Santa Monica
Brand new hip westside winebar. Full of the "see to be seen crowd"
Every wine is priced the same, so it's a great spot to try(or even share)some different wines from the region

BOHO
6372 W. Sunset Blvd - Hollywood
Located perfectly in the Arclight Complex, this "beerbar" has some great burgers aswell. Crowd is mostly "Hollywood Hip" but without the attitude. Basically a great place to hang out after a movie or to start your night before hitting the clubs in the area

BORDELLO
901 E.First Street - Downtown L.A.
This bar was a former brothel way back in the early 19th century, so Bordello's name just fits fine.
The area is a little run down, but getting there should not be dangerous at all. It just enhances the experience. Bordello usually turns into a live music lounge, you should call ahead and see who will be performing(213)687-3766

BROADWAY BAR
830 South Broadway - Downtown L.A.
A bar from the owners of 4100 and The Golden Gopher is a throwback into the 1940's Gangster/Mobster area. A great, low key spot that quickly catched on with the locals! A Killer Jukebox and 2 4 1 Drinks during Happy Hour(until 9PM)makes this a spot to check out day or night!

BURGUNDY ROOM
1621 Cahuenga Blvd - Hollywood
One of Hollywoods Classic Bars, every Night has a different Vibe, check Bar Promotions Site for Crowds and Music

CAMEO BAR
1819 Ocean Avenue - Santa Monica
Cool, english inspired bar inside the trendy Viceroy Hotel in Santa Monica. Great place if you want to spent some serious $

CAPITOL CITY
1615 N. Cahuenga Blvd - Hollywood
You can spent an entire evening just on two blocks of Cahuenga Blvd and call it a night! Clubs, bars, great restaurants - you can find it all here. A upscale sportsbar was missing, but no more. Capitol City fills that void and with 30 beers on tap, you sure will not get bored here during any playoff games

CAT & FIDDLE
6530 Sunset Blvd - Hollywood
The Owner past away, but the legacy will continue. A trip to Hollywood has to include this incredible Pub. Beautiful outside patio, laid back atmosphere, stylish crowd. Dont be surprised to see some Rolling Stones members hanging here. Snakebite is my drink of choice here! Packed Fri, Sat Nights

CHICO
2915 W.Beverly Blvd - Montebello
Get it on the eastside! All the Latin Homeboiz that is. This laid back gay hangout made it's name with fierce dancenights, great drinkspacials, strip contests but mostly for the no attitude "manly" guys who hang out here

CHLOE
1449 W 2nd Street - Santa Monica
This frensh inpired bar on the westside is tres chic with it's art decco style and fine cocktail tables
A true hot spot for the folks on the westside

CITIZEN SMITH
1600 N.Cahuenga Blvd - Hollywood
Technically a restaurant, but with an beautiful interior and being walking distance to all the areas nightclubs, this is the perfect spot to have some drinks at one of there 3 bars, before clubbing or afterwards, as Citizen Smith stays open tuesdays thru saturdays until 4 AM and even offers a late night lounge menu

CLEAR BAR
11916 Ventura Blvd - Studio City
What?!?! A Hip, Cool Bar in the Valley?!?! Times are changing, lol, check out this little hang out for the happening Valley Crowd. DJ's spinning on some nights too!!!

CINESPACE
6356 Hollywood Blvd - Hollywood
This supper club promises to be a movie lover's dream, an upscale restaurant, bar and screening room where diners can enjoy some food and cocktails while watching independant art house films, classics, music videos and art. Some DJ nights aswell!

CHA CHA LOUNGE
2375 Glendale Blvd - Silverlake
Seattle's coolest bar has set up shop in the "hood"! Silverlake "hood" that is.... This place is the ultimate hang out spot, no attitude, no drama, cheap drinks and I mean cheap(!!)-$2.50 drafts/$4Well Drinks-$6-7 Cocktails, no need to complain. Basically this is an offspring of the famous Cha Cha Lounge in Seattle, cool kitsch kinda decor, but any bar that has a Willie Nelson Painting greeting you is just cool! Weekend can get quite busy, if there is a line, just cross the street to the Red Lion In, a German restaurant with (of course) excellent beer for you to chill with the locals. Afterwards there probably wont be a line and you can just chill at this low down-fun-Tijuana meets tiki decor place! (Ok, the last refrence i stole from the L.A. Times review of the bar, but it is just the perfect way to describe the place, so I have to give them some credit)

CIRCLE BAR
2926 Main Street - Santa Monica
Used to be a run down place, but with a little paint and new Management it quickly became a Hot Spot on the Westside!

CRESCENT HOTEL LOUNGE
403 N.Crescent Drive - Beverly Hills
The hotel has been here since 1926, but after a hip makeover it is now reborne as a boutique hotel, which of course in return comes with a hip(ster)hang out lounge and bar. Fireplace, candlelights, cushy sofas, vibrant crowd. You get the idea. Deffinatly a place only the "in the know" crowd is frequenting right now

DERBY
4500 Los Feliz Blvd - Los Feliz
Swing dancing is still not dead, Wednesday most popular.

DOWNTOWN L.A.STANDARD ROOFTOP BAR
550 S. Flower Street - Los Angeles
This Pool Bar of the Standard Hotel is now famous for there Sunday Afternoons, $20 Cover will get you the privelege to pay $8 for a beer. The views are stunning, the crowd is beautiful, ahhhh, living in L.A.can be so nice! Weekday Nights are more relaxed!

DRESDEN
Vermont - Silverlake
Best Lounge in the City! Main attraction are doubtlessly Marty und Elayne, world-famous performers and icons of trash-jazz. The live music, at later hours audibly more off-key than before due to the performers' intoxication level! A must see!

EAGLE L.A.
4219 Santa Monica Blvd - Silverlake
Basically a gay leather bar with an amazing friendly staff. Happy Hour from 4-9 pm is more laid back and some cheap beer ($4/Pitcher!!!). But when it gets dark outside, the place can get pretty wild. Nothing for the faint of heart. Plus they have a great/packed girls night on wednesdays

El Carmen
8138 W 3rd Street - Los Angeles
If you love Tequilla, i got a place for you, This bar has 270 brands of tequila and some great tacos.

ELEVATE
811 Wilshire Blvd - Downtown L.A.
Stunning looking bar that urns into a nightclub. Nestled on the 21st floor, the views are truely spectacular. Fri/Sat can get very busy, so rather come on a wed or thur night to not risk being turned away

ESPN ZONE
L.A.Live - Downtown L.A.
The ultimate Sportsbar right in from of the Staples Center, perfect before or after a game or if you just want to catch up on some games

FATHERS OFFICE

This place is possibly serving the best burgers in town, try it, you will love them. In addition a cool, trendy crowd is hanging out at this beatiful stretch of Montana Avenue in Santa Monica. Microbrews is the norm for drinks here.

FORMOSA CAFE
7156 Santa Monica Blvd - West Hollywood
Legendary Hollywood Bar and Lounge where you can hang with the Hip Hollywood crowd.

FROLIC ROOM
Hollywood Blvd - Hollywood

GEISHA HOUSE
6633 Hollywood Blvd - Hollywood

Downtown L.A. is becoming a hip spot to hang out.

The Golden Gopher can be described as a upscale, classy A-List Bar or as the L.A.Times would write," a rock 'n' roll bar with a baroque twist! So, check out what the buzz is all about!

When you see the white exterior, you know you have found the right place. Linq is a NY Style lounge with high ceilings, plants everywhere, fireplace, and smoking room at the entrance of the restaurant. It is one of the most popular among trendsetters, like you and me (wink wink)

L'SCORPION
6679 Hollywood Blvd - Hollywood
I have been living in Hollywood for almost 10 years now. To see some shady T-Shirt shop turned into a cool bar is just a delight. You have no idea how much we need bars like this out here. No Cover, No Velvet rope, just a chill place to have fun with excellent bartenders who know how to mix you a drink. If you plan on clubbing that night or just having a chill time, maybe barhopping(?)in the area, this should be one of the spots you have to visit!
