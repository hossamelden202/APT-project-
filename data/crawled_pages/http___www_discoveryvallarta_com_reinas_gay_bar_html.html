Reinas Gay Bar
 

Emiliano Zapata - 361 Lazaro Cardenas at Insurgentes street. 
Cellular: 322-205-0433
Fun and popular gay bar and cantina in the Zona Romantica with jukebox, 
films, music videos and lots of conversation. 
Reinas (Queens) is located on L. Cardenas street across from the Guadalajara pharmacy. 
Friendly service with staff and owner Marco from the Netherlands. 
Billed as "the place to meet your prince". 

Reinas (facebook) will be celebrating their 11th anniversary on October 10, 2021, 
and as always there will be a big costume bash on Halloween night. 
Wireless internet available, pet-friendly. 

Happy hour aka Queens Tea daily, 4pm-7pm. 
Open daily, 4pm-2am
