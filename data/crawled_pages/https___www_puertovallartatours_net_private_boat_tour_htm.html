Spend your day in paradise cruising the calm waters of Banderas Bay, visiting remote beaches, snorkeling with tropical fish, sampling local Mexican specialties at beach restaurants or bars, doing watersports and taking incredible photographs of Puerto Vallarta.

Snorkel with tropical fish at the Los Arcos Marine Preserve, enjoy watersports at beautiful Las Animas Beach, hike to the waterfalls at Yelapa or Quimixto, cruise the North Bay Coastline and watch for whales (in season), sea turtles, dolphins or manta rays, discover rare endemic birds, make your own party cruise or soak up the sunset from the water... The options are limitless!
