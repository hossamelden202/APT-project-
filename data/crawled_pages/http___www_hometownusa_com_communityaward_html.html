Community Website Awards

The Hometown Community Website Awards are awarded to websites that exemplify themselves as being true community websites. The following guidelines are used to judge each site that is submitted:
  • Content
  • Structure and Navigation
  • Visual Design
  • Functionality
  • Interactivity
  • Overall Experience

Core Components - Free Local Business Listing, Community Bulletin Board, Community Calendar, Multiple School Listings, Multiple Church Listings, Multiple Non-Profit Organization Listings, Safe Resources for Children, Multiple Local Government Agency Listings, Local or Regional History, Community Photo Gallery

For the Community Website Award the site must include a minimum of 5 of the core components listed above.

 

By submitting for this award you agree to and acknowledge the following:

  • You are the individual responsible for making decisions about the website's content.
  • If you are awarded one of the community awards you will place the award logo on your top page with a reciprocal link to your hometown's page on the Hometown USA or Hometown Canada website. For example: http://www.HometownUSA.com/ca/Oakland.html. Please go to that page and copy the URL for the link.
  • Your site is free from profanity, hate content and adult content and that you plan to keep it that way.
  • Your site does not link directly to any sites that contain profanity, hate content or adult content.
  • You agree to removing the Hometown Community Website Award logo if your site does not meet the afore mentioned criteria in the future.
