City Manager's Office

Mission Statement

It is our mission to provide consistent and efficient and professional leadership and administrative services to the Cornelius City Council, city staff and the citizens of our community.

Reach out to us anytime!

We can help with questions about:

  • Projects and services that have citywide impact or don't fit within another department
  • Sustainability initiatives
  • Filing or locating a city record
  • Information we have communicated
  • Municipal elections
  • Public meetings and civic engagement
  • Third-party utility providers, such as electricity, gas, or garbage
  • Social services grants
  • Our long-term goals and strategy and community vision plan
