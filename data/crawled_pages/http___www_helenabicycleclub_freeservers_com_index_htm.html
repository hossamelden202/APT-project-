Welcome to the Helena Bicycle Club web site.  The members of the Helena Bicycle Club are enthusiastic about bicycling whether it be as a mountain biker, commuter, roadie, bicycle advocate, or bicycle racer.   The Helena Bicycle Club is always ready to support riders of all types. The club's motto is:

"No one is ever left behind!"

Come join us on one of the club rides.  We're sure you'll find a ride to your liking.  Some of the rides/events we offer include:
One Helena Hundred - our annual 100-miles or 100-kilometer ride on Saturday, July 21
Double Divide Ride - our annual two-day, road bike ride over two mountain passes on August 25-26
Evening rides during the work week
Weekend mountain bike and road bike rides
"Members Only" rides
Progressive Dinner ride on Saturday, June 9
Historical Helena Bicycle Ride on Saturday, July 28
Monthly Program/Business Meeting

We look forward to having you join us on one of our rides.

 

The Helena Bicycle Club web site is maintained Karen Thompson, and is under construction. This site will continue to be updated.  If you have any ideas or pictures in jpg or gif format, then  e-mail them to the newsletter editors, Karen and David Thompson.  The web site was last updated on June 4, 2001.
