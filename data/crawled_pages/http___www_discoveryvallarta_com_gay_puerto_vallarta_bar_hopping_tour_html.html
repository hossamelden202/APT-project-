Gay Vallarta Bar Hopping Tours
 

South Side - 141-A Pulpito at Olas Altas street.  Cell: 044-322-148-4960

Make some new friends from all over the world, and come experience the best 
of Puerto Vallarta's gay nightlife! 
Currently, Vallarta is considered Mexico's #1 gay-friendly vacation destination, 
so make the most of your night out safely and conveniently. 
If you don't have anyone to go with or don't know where to go, check out 
these Gay Bar Hopping guys who promote themselves as "the VIP party tour"

    No lineups...guests get VIP access and jump the queue!
    No cover charges... it's included
    No waiting for drinks...your drinks will be waiting for you.
    No waiting on the bill or change...it's included as you are a VIP! Walk in and out.
 
The gay bar hopping staff is joined by local drag queens, porn stars, gogo boys 
and other entertainers who help bring fun and good memories to these enjoyable tours. 
From small Mexican cantinas, martini lounges, all-male strip clubs, dance clubs, 
drag shows and circuit parties, Puerto Vallarta has it all. 
 

Very good reviews and word of mouth for this walking tour.
Top-rated on TripAdvisor
Certificate of Excellence winner for 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016
 

Gay owned with gay staff, mostly 30-40 something crowd
Popular with tourists and locals alike since January 2011.

Several different tour options are currently available. 
Offering private tours and bachelor parties as well.

The classic Gay Vallarta Bar Hopping tours which have been famous since 2011.
Available with or without gourmet dinner,
you simply choose a date and select your preferred alcohol package.
Your bilingual tour guide orders, serves and pays for your drinks & tips the waiters at all venues. 
No waiting, no lineups, and no cover charges.

- Top Shelf Tour with meal $129 USD or Top Shelf without meal $99 USD
- National Brands and Beer Tour $114 USD with meal or $72 USD no meal

Gay Cabaret Hopping still visits the bars 
but they've add a delicious gourmet meal and a live cabaret show of your choice 
(with VIP Access and preferred seating) at either the Palm Cabaret, 
ACT II Main Stage or Red Room Cabaret.
Many tours include a personal meet with the performer and 
a photo opportunity. All tickets $129 USD.

Go to their Gay Vallarta Bar Hopping or their Facebook page
for more pictures and complete information on tour, dinner and cabaret options.
