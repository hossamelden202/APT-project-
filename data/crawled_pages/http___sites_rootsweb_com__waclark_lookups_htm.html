References and Volunteers

To request a lookup from any of the references, click on the address of the owner and ask them politely. Please use the name of the reference as the subject of your message and place your e-mail address in the body of the message.

To volunteer to do lookups, send the following information to ALICE ALLEN: Your Name, your E-mail address, Title of Reference, Author of Reference

Please keep in mind that all Reference material must NOT be copyrighted or written permission must be obtained from the author or publisher prior to being used as a reference.

As you can see, volunteers are needed.
