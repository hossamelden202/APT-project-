Here are some links that social work graduate school applicants might find interesting:

  • Social Work School Rankings of the Top 80 Schools (2000)--these rankings were obtained from U.S. News and World Report's Rankings, but you should be extremely cautious about how you interpret them. These rankings mean nothing at very close ranges (e.g., the 20th-ranked school may or may not be better than the 25th-ranked school), and besides, if you are only interested in a Masters (MSW) degree, employers usually won't care what program you got your degree from as long as the program was CSWE-accredited.
  • Browse Social Work and Social Services Jobs Online to find out more about the types of jobs that are available in the field.
  • A list of CSWE-accredited BSW and MSW programs, categorized by states in the U.S.A.
