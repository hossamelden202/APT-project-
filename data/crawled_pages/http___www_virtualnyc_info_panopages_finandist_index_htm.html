Financial District The Financial District, the center of some of the World's big money transactions, sits below Chambers Street, in Lower Manhattan. Money 
isn't the only attraction for this neighborhood. On the East side, find the South Street Seaport, and Fulton Fish Market. On the West, you'll find Trinity Church, and Battery Park City.
