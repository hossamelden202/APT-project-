UNIQUE ADVENTURES FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I DO THESE ADVENTURES?
A: Yes! All of the adventures shown in the top section are great options for cruise passengers. The ones in the lower section are too long or start too early for most cruise ship passengers, they are better suited for Cozumel hotel guests.
Q: IF YOU HAD TO CHOOSE...WHICH ADVENTURE SHOULD I DO?
A: The answer to that is of course "it depends on which one sounds most interesting to you". For people who can't swim, the Atlantis Submarine offers a great way to see Cozumel's coral reefs. For hotel guests looking for the thrill of a lifetime, the whale sharks are a must-do. However, they are only around in the summer. If you have the time, Coba: A Mayan Encounter is a great all-around adventure, as is Jungle Maya. If time is shorter, then get a thrill by trying your hand at snuba diving or scuba diving, or if you prefer to stay dry, check out the motorcycle tour!
Q: WHAT IS THE WATER TEMPERATURE IN COZUMEL?
A:The Caribbean waters of Cozumel are wonderfully warm all year round. The temperatures vary between 77° F in the winter to 82° F in the summer months.
