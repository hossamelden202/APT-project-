Globes

Like wall maps, globes can emphasize political or physical features. Ball sizes range from 9" to 32" and are available with blue or parchment colored oceans, illuminated or opaque. Less traditional versions show the earth as it appears from space, or are made from transparent acrylic.

Please note that globes are not returnable after 45 days from shipment date. All globe returns require advance return authorization from the GCB Order Desk (800-358-6013; [email protected]). There is a 20% restocking fee for any globe returned.
