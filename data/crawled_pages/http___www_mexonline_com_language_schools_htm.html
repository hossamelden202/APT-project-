 Spanish Language Schools in Mexico

Learning a foreign language is no easy task but is well worth the effort. There's no better way to successfully absorb a foreign language than to immerse yourself in its culture. Mexico has many Spanish language schools that offer top-notch instruction. Along with classes, subject-specific workshops are available as well.

Language schools & workshops are listed under the state or city in which they are located. Please see the Mexico Universities Directory for additional educational institutions.


All of Mexico

Spanish55
Private Spanish lessons around your schedule. We specialize in teaching Spanish to native English-speakers. Learn Spanish one-on-one with a certified Spanish tutor over Skype.

Cancun

Spanish with Laura & Skype
I'm Laura Olachea, a knowledgeable, skilled and professionally qualified Spanish teacher. Students like my classes because they are dynamic, informative, entertaining and practical. I offer Spanish classes via Skype and in person, customized to meet your level and to achieve your language proficiency goals. Whether you are fluent, and wish to learn the fine points or advanced grammar, or you know only a few words, I'll work with you to create a lesson plan specific to your needs.

Chiapas

SanCris Spanish Language School
SanCris Spanish Language School is located in the heart of San Cristobal de las Casas, the cultural capital of the state of Chiapas, Mexico. Located at Dr. Jose Felipe Flores #73 in a lovely neighborhood called Barrio de Guadalupe. Our Spanish Language School is run by experienced local teachers. We offer a personalized learning program with one-on-one and group instruction to students of all levels. All of our teachers are professionals with extensive knowledge of grammar and experience teaching.

Guadalajara

IMAC Spanish Language Programs
If you are looking for quality Spanish language schools, IMAC is recognized as one of the finest Spanish Language schools in Latin America. Offering programs backed with over 40 years of experience, we have the best selection in Spanish Language courses, locations and extra-curricular activities.

Guanajuato


Los Cabos

Spanish In Cabo
Spanish In Cabo is a school and cultural center in Los Cabos, Baja, Mexico. With over 10 years of experience and recognition, we offer a wide variety of Spanish classes in San Jose and Cabo San Lucas, both in person as online. This means that students, both young and old, can take regular Spanish courses, immersion courses that include activities outside the classroom, as well as a variety of activities designed to strengthen and reinforce what is seen in class like our traditional cooking class that we do every Friday.

Mazatlan


Merida

La Calle Spanish School
La Calle is a foreign adult Spanish school located in the center of Merida, one of the safest place in Mexico. The Spanish school is close to shops, restaurants, touristic places and transport to go to the principal touristic attractions of Yucatan. We offer Spanish courses in groups or private.

Learn Spanish in Mexico City
We offer high quality Spanish Classes in Mexico City. Are you looking for Individual Spanish Lessons in Mexico City? Our Certified Spanish Instructors are at your disposal. You can choose your own schedule and decide on the location and duration of your classes. Moreover, you can decide what type of Spanish Classes you would like to take.
Walk Spanish Mexico City Language School
Walk Spanish is a total immersion Spanish school located in Mexico City. We offer Spanish courses n Polanco, Roma and Condesa based on complete immersion. This means our students learn outside of the classroom and trhough a set of activities. Our lessons are suit for beginner, intermediate and advanced students from all nationalities interested in learning Spanish.

Morelia


Oaxaca

Becari Language School
At the Becari Language School, we specialize in offering many different options for any person interested in learning our beautiful Spanish Language. To study at Becari is not only a new language but also to enter another culture. We are a medium size school which prides itself on careful and sustained attention to each student regardless of his or her level of proficiency with a staff of qualified teachers who have ample experience in the field of Spanish as a second language.
Instituto Cultural Oaxaca
The Instituto Cultural Oaxaca is teaching Spanish as a second language since 1984. The school offers intensive programs to students from all over the world in the lively cultural setting of Oaxaca. The Instituto is located in a welcoming 19th century estate house surrounded by private gardens in the colonial center of the city. Spanish language classes with native speakers are conducted entirely in Spanish to motivate the students to develop their language abilities, creating proficiency in communication.
Spanish Immersion School
The Spanish Immersion School is committed to teaching you Spanish and to sharing with you the wonderful diversity of Oaxaca. We do this by holding classes in important cultural settings around the city and by hosting students on amazing excursions to points of interest all around the valley.


Playa del Carmen

Agora Language Center
Learn Spanish at Agora Language Center in Playa del Carmen! We are a small size school and our experienced, friendly teachers provide quality teaching for students of all levels. We work in small groups for faster progress. Study in the morning, enjoy the beach in the afternoon (10 minutes from the school) and join our activities in the evening. On weekends, explore Mayan sites, colonial towns, cenotes and stunning islands. Learn Spanish and discover Mexico and its rich culture!
Taan Spanish School
TAAN, which is the Mayan word for Speak, is a Spanish language school located in Playa del Carmen, Mexico. The school, opened in 2010, features 6 classrooms teaching 6 levels of Spanish for foreigners, which includes lessons for beginner, intermediate and advanced students. Our school is located 5 minutes walking from the beach and centrally located in Playa del Carmen, where there is no need for transport.
