WinMorph is a high performance morphing and warping software. With millions of downloads, WinMorph has a wide userbase, have been used in movies, roadshows and other projects.

  • Freeware: Distributed as freeware for business or personal use. However if you want to redistribute, you need to inform the author.
  • Platform: Available only on Windows.
  • Architecture: Works standalone, and also as a plug-in for Wax.
  • Performance/Quality: One of the fastest warping/morphing software available, with very high quality output and user control.

The WinMorph Google Group is a good place to discuss tips and tricks.

Download

You can get WinMorph 3.01 from Download.com or Snapfiles.com.

As the first step after installation, please read the WinMorph UserGuide (a link available in the start menu program group of WinMorph).

Features
  • Professional quality warping and morphing tools.
  • Works both as a standalone application and as a plug-in for the Wax video editor to get best of both worlds.
  • Intuitive shape-based morphing for both ease of use and high degree of freedom.
  • Advanced keyframing, blending and distortion tools for total control.
  • Can do dynamic morphing (also called video morphing).
  • Input file formats include JPEG,BMP,PCX,PNG,PBM,TGA,TIFF.
  • Output file formats include JPEG,BMP,PNG,TGA,TIFF,AVI,MPEG and SWF.
