Silver Falls State Park

Sandra took me to Silver Falls State Park in Silverton, Oregon for my birthday in May, 2004.   She packed a wonderful picnic lunch, with baguettes, cheeses, strawberries and cream, dessert, and of course, a bottle of wine.  We found a wonderful little picnic spot right alongside the creek, and enjoyed a peaceful, relaxing lunch. 

After lunch, and reading the Sunday paper, we packed up and went for a walk to check out 177-foot South Falls, the tallest waterfall out of ten in Silver Falls State Park.  We weren't dressed for hiking, and the weather wasn't the best, so we didn't hike to any of the other waterfalls.  Maybe next time. 

We had a wonderful time, just relaxing and having a little couples time.  What a great birthday!  Thank you, Sandra!  You can click on the photos below for a larger image.
