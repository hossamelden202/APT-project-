Come with us for a great adventure tour from north Puerto Vallarta, we'll take you to the Higuera Blanca hills for a great zip line adventure in a great scenic guided tour, you'll enjoy a canopy circuit that includes 10 different double cable slides and a suspended bridge you can go through, from the top you'll enjoy amazing views of the peninsula.

You will be amazed by the magnificent views of the Punta Mita Peninsula and the surroundings, as well as the local flora and fauna, keep an eye open for wild boars, deer, or even the beautiful Guacamaya parrots.

Cruise Passengers: This tour is not recommended for cruise passengers as the nearest pickup is in Bucerias. For cruise passengers you can check El Eden Canopy.

Runs: Daily at 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 3:00 PM. Exact pick-up time will be specified on your confirmation.

Duration: 3 Hours Approximately, Including Transportation.

Departs From: Meeting locations in Nuevo Vallarta, Bucerias, and Punta Mita. Hotel pickup is also available from select Riviera Nayarit hotels for $5.00 USD per person. Exact meeting instructions will be shown on your confirmation.

Includes:

  • Round-Trip Transportation from Meeting Points
  • English-Speaking Adventure Tour Guides
  • 10 Lines Canopy Tour
  • Bottled Water
  • Tequila Tasting
  • Safety Gear
  • All Taxes

You'll Need:

  • Biodegradable Sunscreen
    •
  • Insect Repellent
  • Comfortable Clothes and Shoes
  • Extra Cash for Lunch, Photos, Souvenirs, etc.

Kids: 6 years old and 4 ft (1.20 m) tall to participate. Kids same price as adults.

Restrictions: For safety reasons, pregnant women, people with back or neck problems, heart conditions, balance, or dizziness conditions are not allowed to participate in the tour. Maximum weight to do this tour is 260 lbs (118 kg). Cameras are not allowed for the safety of our guests.

Note: This ranch is located about an hour from Puerto Vallarta. Due to its location, it is recommended for guests staying north of Puerto Vallarta, or if you are looking for a fun way to get to know the northern beaches of the bay. It is available for everyone, of course, but it's just a bit of a drive.
