Garbo Piano and Jazz Bar
 

 South Side/Zona Romantica - 142 Pulpito.  Tel: 322-223-5753 
An upscale ambience and a well-dressed crowd has made this piano and jazz bar-club
a popular choice for tourists and locals alike. Elegant "minimalist Deco". 
Often with live music starting after 10pm with various entertainers including Piel Canela/Cinnamon Skin 
 and Bobby Broadway and his show tunes. 
Or with Ron Pass who has a quick wit and wide ranging song list of classic rock, 
Broadway tunes and standards to entertain the clientele. 
Very good word of mouth and reviews at TripAdvisor,
"...always a fun, lighthearted, friendly crowd visiting..." 

Garbo in spanish means stylishness, class, grace, elegance of manner. 
Voted #1 Puerto Vallarta martini bar in 2008, 2010 and 2011, etc, 
and a local fun spot for music, drinks and conversation since March 2002. No cover. 
Caters to a more mature crowd - noted for its cocktails, atmosphere and service.

Friendly owners German and Javier to serve you.

For more info visit their Garbo Bar Vallarta page on Facebook

Just down the street from Coco's restaurant and around the corner
from cabaret-bar The Palm.  Daily, 6pm-2am
