"Stories from Alaska and the Northwest Coast"
(last updated on Jan. 2nd, 2002)


Since the beginning of time, the "First Peoples" have passed down their stories from generation to generation. These are the verbal histories of noble and culture-rich societies and are not to be taken lightly. Some tell tales of heroism and significant events while others show the human frailties and humor that we all share. All have lessons to be learned.
