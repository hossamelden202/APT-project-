Bed and Breakfast Homes of Montana

 

by Janet Ollila Colberg
 

Trade Paperback

Published by Summer Kitchen Press, 1993

  101 pages

ISBN # 1560442735

Price: $9.95

Booknotes

  

--FREE US media mail--

This guide to 38 of Montana's finest bed and breakfast homes will help you find the perfect "home away from home" in the Treasure State.

Special features include:
  -- homes in all six regions of Montana
  -- historical notes on the areas surrounding each bed and  breakfast
  -- more than 140 photographs of featured homes
  -- mouthwatering recipes from each host family
  -- names, addresses, more!

NOTE.  This is the first edition and it contains historical research not found elsewhere.  The 2nd edition, called Montana Bed and Breakfast Guide and Cookbook, is also available through Helpbooks.com or Amazon.com.
