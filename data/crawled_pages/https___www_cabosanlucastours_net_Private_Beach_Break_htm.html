La Islita (The Little Island) is your private floating beach break in Cabo San Lucas. This private tour is available for up to 14 passengers at a really small cost, you can choose the schedule that best fits your needs, we have Sunset and Day tours, very popular with cruise ship passengers in particular that wish to have a very private intimate experience in Cabo.

La Isla is fully decked out with sun beds, lounge chairs, restroom, and shaded areas.  By day, we will anchor off a beautiful beach, where you can swim, paddleboard, snorkel or explore the clear waters of Cabo. Take amazing photographs of the Arch, walk to Lovers Beach, or snorkel with tropical fish.

You can enjoy a private Sunset adventure, we offer drinks and food at an extra cost, but you can also bring your own beers, wine or your favorite drinks. On board, you can lounge all day if you wish, just soaking up the sun, sipping on your favorite drinks. This is a 3 hour tour but you can get additional time at an extra cost.  This is a great idea for groups for any occasion.
Cruise Passengers: Yes! This excursion is available to you and will work with most cruise schedules. We will adjust the start time to work for you.
