PRIVATE TOURS IN COZUMEL

When staying on Cozumel, it is often frustrating to try to find a public tour to one of the incredible sights that exist on the mainland Riviera Maya, particularly the Mayan Ruins sites. Tours often leave so early that cruise ship passengers are not able to catch them, and hotel guests sometimes have to find the ferry bleary-eyed in the wee morning hours. It's with you in mind that we've developed these totally customizable private tours. You'll have a private driver for a day, to take you sightseeing in the Riviera Maya, and will have nearly limitless ability to customize your tour to fit your particular interests. You can set the start time,and decide when you'd like to return to Cozumel!

COZUMEL PRIVATE TOUR FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP, CAN I TAKE THESE TOURS?
A: Absolutely! We highly recommend the Tulum, Cancun, Coba, and Riviera Maya tours for cruise ship passengers. Chichen Itza is too far away to be able to fit most cruise schedules.
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP, DO YOU PICK UP AT THE PIER?
A:No, these private tours are on the mainland, so you'll need to ride the Cozumel Ferry over to Playa del Carmen to meet the tour. Allow a couple of hours round-trip for this.
Q: THE LOCATIONS I AM INTERESTED IN ARE NOT LISTED. WHAT SHOULD I DO?
A:We can take you anywhere you want to go. Anywhere between Cancun and Tulum in the Riviera Maya can be visited on our Private Tour of the Riviera Maya. Just pick the amount of time you'll need, and let the fun begin! You can also Request a Custom Quote.
Q: I WANT A PRIVATE TOUR OF COZUMEL ISLAND. DO YOU HAVE THOSE?
A:For groups, most of our tours can be done as private tours, including Jeep Tours, Punta Sur, etc. Just give us a call or drop us an e-mail for a quote. We don't yet offer the private driver for a day service in Cozumel, but it's coming very soon! Request a Custom Quote.
Q: I HAVE A BIG GROUP COMING. CAN YOU SET SOMETHING UP SPECIAL FOR US?
A: Absolutely! We can arrange any activity for a group of any size. We can also arrange very special custom tours for couples or any size of group. Request a Custom Quote.
Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
