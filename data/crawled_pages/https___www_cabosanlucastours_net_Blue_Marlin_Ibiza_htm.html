The world's most famous beach club has hit Cabo!  Famed the world over for it's signature white teepees, lush beds, and luxurious ambience, Blue Marlin Ibiza is a jet-set utopia and the hottest beach club in Cabo.

Set on fabulous Medano Beach, just steps away from the beautiful Sea of Cortez, you can spend your day sipping mojitos around the beautiful swimming pool, basking in the glorious Cabo San Lucas sunshine, cruising the bay in a glass bottom boat, kayaking, eating and drinking cocktails, and of course, the best people watching in Cabo. 


Blue Marlin Ibiza doesn't allow small kids, which makes it a great luxury retreat for couples, and it is located right in the heart of the action of Cabo, making it convenient to sample different restaurants and bars, to do water sports, or to walk into downtown or to the marina after the break is over if you like.

Cruise Passengers: Yes! This tour is great for a shore excursion. Just a short taxi ride from cruise pier.
