Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Fife is approximately 3,864 (1990), 9173 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 2,152 (1990), 3642 (2010).
The amount of land area in Fife is 7.554 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0 sq kilometers.
The distance from Fife to Washington DC is 2464 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 29 miles. (as the crow flies)
Fife is positioned 47.23 degrees north of the equator and 122.36 degrees west of the prime meridian.
