COZUMEL BOTTOM FISHING

Bottom fishing is a great way to catch some of Cozumel's best eating fish.  Bottom fishing is normally done by drifting offshore and dropping lines straight down and deep, often hooking Red Snapper, Triggerfish or Reef Fish - all excellent eating fish.  We'll even clean and fillet your catch so your hotel or restaurant chef can prepare it for you if you like.  Reserve Now
