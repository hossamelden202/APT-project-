Glide effortlessly over the treetops of the set of the Arnold Schwarzenegger Movie, "Predator".  Fly high over waterfalls, forests, and jungle with the greatest of ease on over 2 miles of zip lines.

This tour has no tree platforms or bridges to cross - all zip lines are from platforms on natural ground.   You are welcome to stay around after the tour to swim in the waterfall pool or dine at the El Eden Jungle Restaurant.  A photo of you on your tour is available for purchase after the tour.  Free Tequila Tastings and tour top off the activity.

Cruise Passengers:  Yes! This tour makes a great shore excursion. A very popular tour!

Runs: Daily at 10:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 2:00 PM. Exact pick-up time depends on your hotel or cruise ship but will be specified on your confirmation.

Duration: 5 Hours Approximately

Departs From: Directions will be provided with your confirmation.

Includes:

  • Round-Trip Transportation from Meeting Locations

  • English Speaking Tour Guides

  • Entrance Fee to El Eden Ecopark

  • Tequila Tour & Tasting

  • 11 Zip Lines

  • All Safety Equipment & Orientation

  • Lockers

  • Use of the Swimming or Dinning Area at El Eden Jungle Seafood Restaurant (Food is Not Included in the Tour Price)

  • All Taxes

You'll Need:

  • Biodegradable Eco-Friendly Sunscreen
  • Swimsuit and Towel for Swimming
  • Camera
  • Tennis Shoes
  • Hair Tie for Long Hair
  • Cash for lunch, souvenirs
  • Light rain gear during rainy season - it might not rain, but the jungle could likely be wet.

Kids: Ages 5 and Up. Kids are the Same Price as Adults.

Restrictions: Maximum Weight Limit is 250 lbs (115 kg). This tour is not recommended for pregnant women, people with back or heart problems. However, there will be photos available for purchase.
