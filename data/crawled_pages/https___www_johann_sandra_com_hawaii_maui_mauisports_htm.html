Maui is like a fantastic playground for children and adults alike.  The windsurfing capital of the world, Maui is also a paradise for hikers, scuba divers, snorkelers, and athletes of all kinds.  The horseback riding is some of the most exciting to be found anywhere, and activities from camping to thrill sports like sky diving are abundant all over the island.
