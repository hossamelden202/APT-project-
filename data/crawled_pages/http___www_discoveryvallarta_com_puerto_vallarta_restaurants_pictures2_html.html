Good word of mouth for one of the newer Puerto Vallarta Mexican restaurants in town
 El Mole de Jovita with gay owners Sergio Mucino (rip) and Roberto Gomez
and a menu that includes tasty birria, menudo, quesadillas, chilaquiles and tacos.
Inside or sidewalk seating at 220 Basilio Badillo, since 2009.
Just down the street from Joe Jack's Fish Shack. Tel: 322-223-3065

 


Some of the staff of the The Swedes Bistro located at 154 Pulpito street and Olas Altas.
Popular for its ambience, food and bar views for many years. Image thanks to Morgan and Rikard.
 


At gay-friendly Daiquiri Dick's along Los Muertos beach

 

 
Delicious food at popular India Gate restaurant at 124 Allende Street
just one block up from the downtown Malecon boardwalk. Photo from October 2020.
Tues-Sat, 5pm-10pm and on Sun, 2pm-9pm


 


Pipi's in downtown off the Malecon at 807 Guadalupe Sanchez, at Pipila street.
Tex-Mex specialties include chicken, fish, steak and shrimp fajitas. Since 1986.
Currently #56 of 814 restaurants in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico
 


The owners of the Bistro Teresa downtown restaurant, Laura and Mirasol, from Mexico City

 

 
The staff of the Salud Super Food located at 534 Olas Altas street.
Popular for its breakfast and brunch with fresh veggies, fruit, smoothies and juice. Image thanks Super Food.
 

 


famed Cafe des Artistes in el Centro at 740 Guadalupe Sanchez, shown here in December 2014.
The Gourmand Magazine 2011 Guide to restaurants and fine dining named chef Thierry Blouet
as one of the 10 most influential chefs in the world
and the des Artistes as the best restaurant in all of Mexico.
 


Currently one of the most popular upscale dining spots in town, Tre Piatti
serving Italian cuisine, at 292 Lazaro Cardenas in Old Town.
Recommended by friends.
 


Long time friendly service in the Romantic Zone at Coco's Kitchen (facebook)
at 122 Pulpito street. Especially noted for the good breakfast and brunch.
 

 

 
One of a favorite Mexican restaurants in Old Town, No Way Jose.
For fine dining out at 5 de Febrero #260, the cross street is 
Ignacio Vallarta on the South Side Romantic Zone.

 

 
the Planeta Vegetariano in downtown Puerto Vallarta is located at 270 Iturbide street.
For all-you-can-eat vegetarian lunch buffet for only 85 pesos, dinner is a la carte.
Excellent reviews.

 

 
 El Coleguita Mariscos / Seafood Buddy with waiter Alonso.
Located in the Marina on Popa Street, local 17, in front of Condominiums Marina del Rey.
El Coleguita in the Marina & in Nuevo Vallarta is know for its reasonably priced seafood since 1989

 


Coco Tropical dining on Los Muertos Beach, located at the end of Basilio Badillo street
and in the same block beachfront as well-known Daiquiri Dick's
Pretty much straight clientele as far as I can see

 


There's a romantic and beautiful tropical atmosphere at the River Cafe which is located along the banks
     of the Cuale River in downtown Vallarta. Excellent ambience, food and staff.


 


Friends out together at the old Hot Frida's on Olas Altas street
