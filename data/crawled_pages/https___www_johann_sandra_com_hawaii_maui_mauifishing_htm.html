Sportfishing on Maui

MAUI FISHING GUIDEBOOKS
 FROM THE HAWAII BOOKSTORE
 The web's largest selection of Maui Books

The fishing around Maui ranges from very good to excellent.  Most of the fishing boats are berthed at Lahaina Harbor, while others are at Maalaea Harbor.  The best times of the year for marlin are generally July thru September and January thru March (when the generally larger females arrive).  August is the optimum month.  Rough seas can keep boats in for a few days during December and early January, but by February all are generally out.

A private charter will set you back anywhere from $600-$1000 for 8 hours, while a shared vessel can be had for a mere $135-$185pp.  For more information on fishing on Maui, check out the links in the table below.  Before making any reservation, we recommend checking out our Discounts & Bargains page to save a bit of cash.
