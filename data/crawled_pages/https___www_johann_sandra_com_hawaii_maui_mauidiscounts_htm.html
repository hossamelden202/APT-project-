Maui Coupon Books and Discount Cards

This is the place to come to save a little money when visiting Maui, Hawaii.  These brokers can offer discounts on many tours, hotels, dining, and activities throughout Hawaii.  We bought an Entertainment Book, which paid for itself during our stay four times over, and an AOA Gold Card, which paid for itself two times over.   Other discount cards include the ISE Student Discount card, which offers great savings for students visiting the islands, and the Lahaina Cannery Mall coupon book.

It's important to comparison shop for the tours and activities that you've already decided that you're going to do on your trip, and it's always a good idea to visit the official homepage of the tour or activity to get the real scoop on what exactly you're paying for and a bit about the company itself.  When purchasing a discount card, make sure to check the list thoroughly to make sure that the attractions you want are participating in the program. 

Maui Tour, Activity, and Attraction Discounts

You can also save big on tours and attractions through a discount broker such as Tom Barefoot's Discount Tours.  They book so many tours that they are offered big bulk discounts from the tour operators in exchange for their services.  Through these brokers, you can book tours and activities on all the islands of Hawaii.  Other well-respected tour brokers on Maui include Hawaii Active and Maui Tours.

Discounts on Airfare, Hotels, Car Rentals, Condos, Hostels, and Hawaii Cruises

There are several sites that offer incredible airfare, hotel, and car rental discounts of up to 70% off retail prices.  We always use our own.  Other popular sites include Travelocity, Expedia, Orbitz, Hotwire, and Priceline.com.  Sites such as Site59.com and 11th Hour Vacations specialize in last-minute getaways, and there are great discounts on cruises available from agencies like Cruise Direct and Cruises Only.

For REALLY cheap lodging, you can find Maui Hostels at Hostelword.com, or save on Maui Condos and Vacation Rentals at a site like CondoSavers or TravelNow.

Hawaii Shopping Discounts

Hawaii City offers thousands of distinctly Hawaii Gifts, books, videos, jewelry, and much more.  If you can think of it, they probably have it.  The Hawaii Bookstore is the best place on the web to shop for Hawaii Books, including guidebooks, fiction, non-fiction, cookbooks, biographies, Hawaii videos, Hawaiian Music, and any other media you can think of about Hawaii.  There are separate sections broken out for each specific island and activity for ease of searching for what you want, including Maui Books!

Another excellent source of shopping discounts is the Lahaina Cannery Mall's coupon book, which you can use for many discounts for the shops inside the mall.
