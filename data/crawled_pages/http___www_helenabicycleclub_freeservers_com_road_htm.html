Welcome, Roadies!  Here's a listing of upcoming road rides hosted by the Helena Bicycle Club.  For the club's road rides all styles of bikes are welcome; however, if you are riding a mountain-style bike, it is recommended that your bike be equipped with "slick" tires to decrease your rolling resistance on the pavement.  These rides are NOT races but rather gatherings of people with a common interest - BICYCLE RIDING.  You may ride at your own pace.  So come on out, join the ride and enjoy the camaraderie.
