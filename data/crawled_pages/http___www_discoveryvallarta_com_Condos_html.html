To confirm your reservation, the 50% non-refundable deposit needs to arrive within four-six (4-6) days, so please plan accordingly. The final payment is generally due at 35-40 days before arrival, though some condos and villas require final payment at 60-65 days before arrival or longer, and especially for holiday rentals. A few condos (and all villas) require a fully refundable security deposit, which I hold for my clients in case of damages or breakage, and which I send back the security deposit within 7-10 days of the client's departure. If you reserve at less than 40-50 days before arrival, then full payment is due at the time of reservation. Maid service varies from 1 to 6 days per week, with no service on Sundays and Mexican holidays. If you wish to receive a written confirmation of your rental or a rental agreement, please ask, otherwise the email correspondence and confirmation are generally considered sufficient.
