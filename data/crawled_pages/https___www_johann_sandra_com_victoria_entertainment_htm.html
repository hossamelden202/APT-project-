Victoria Entertainment and Night Life

Though not renown for its wild night life like some of it's well-touristed counterparts, Victoria is a city of 350,000 people, and as such, it does offer an array of options for your evening escapades.  We have yet to sample any of the nightclubs, opting instead to relax in our jacuzzi or enjoy a late night dinner on the waterfront.  However, there are certainly options.

Most of Victoria's nightclubs double as live music venues attracting a great variety of acts.  Perhaps the area's biggest draw is the Strathcona Hotel, which hosts multiple bars and the Legends night club.  We wanted to go there, but there was a line down the street, so we went to dinner at the Wharfside instead.

You can save money on entertainment of all kinds with the Vancouver Island Entertainment Book, which also has 50% off savings on hotels, events, entertainment, attractions, tours, and dining.
