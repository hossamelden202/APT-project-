Join us for a great adventure tour from Puerto Vallarta, which includes horseback riding and canopy. We'll take you to Punta Mita for an adrenaline-filled fun trip in the back mountains of Higuera Blanca. You will glide from 10 double cable slides, then go through a suspended bridge.

You can enjoy a relaxing horseback riding adventure that will take you to the hills where you can see the local flora and fauna, our tour guides will be glad to explain all the questions you might have about Puerto Vallarta. You can ensure you'll enjoy a fantastic eco-adventure excursion that is a great fit for the whole family.

Cruise Passengers: This tour is not recommended for cruise passengers due to its distance from the cruise pier. If you are on a cruise ship, we recommend Rio Cuale Canopy Tour instead.

Runs: Daily at 9:00 AM and 12:00 PM. Exact pick-up time will be specified on your confirmation.

Duration: 6 Hours Approximately, Including Transportation.

Departs From: Meeting locations in Nuevo Vallarta, Bucerias, and Punta Mita. Hotel pickup is also available from select Riviera Nayarit hotels for $5.00 USD per person. Exact meeting instructions will be shown on your confirmation.

Includes:

  • Round-Trip Transportation from Meeting Points
  • Bilingual Adventure Tour Guide
  • Canopy Tour
  • Horseback Riding
  • Safety Gear
  • Bottled Water
  • Tequila Tasting
  • All Taxes

You'll Need:

  • Biodegradable Sunscreen
    •
  • Insect Repellent
  • Comfortable Clothes
  • Good Walking Shoes
  • Extra Cash for Lunch, Photos, Souvenirs, etc.

Kids: 6 years old and 4 ft (1.20 m) tall to participate. Kids same price as adults.

Restrictions: For safety reasons, pregnant women, people with back or neck problems, heart conditions, balance, or dizziness conditions are not allowed to participate in the tour. Maximum weight to do this tour is 260 lbs (118 kg). Cameras are not allowed for the safety of our guests.

Note: This ranch is located about an hour from Puerto Vallarta. Due to its location, it is recommended for guests staying north of Puerto Vallarta, or if you are looking for a fun way to get to know the northern beaches of the bay. It is available for everyone, of course, but it's just a bit of a drive.
