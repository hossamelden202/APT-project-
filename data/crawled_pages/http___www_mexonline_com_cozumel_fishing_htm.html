Cozumel Charters brings you all-inclusive deep sea fishing, bottom fishing, fly fishing, and snorkeling tours in Cozumel, Mexico. Our tours include beer, soft drinks, water, fishing gear, tackle, bait, snorkeling equipment, and even transportation from your hotel or cruise ship!

We offer half-day and full day private deep sea and bottom fishing charters, as well as private two-person bonefishing trips into the lagoons of Cozumel. Our half-day trips make excellent cruise shore excursions, whether you are reeling in a marlin or sailfish, or just snorkeling in our underwater wonderland.

We offer private 3 reef snorkeling tours - just you and your group. No crowds, no waiting for clear water, no overfilled party boats. Just your group with our expert guides, exploring Cozumel's coral reefs.
