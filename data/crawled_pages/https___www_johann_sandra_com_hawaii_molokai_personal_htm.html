by Ray Riegert
512pp., pub. 2002

Throughout this site, you will find valuable information from a variety of tour agencies, visitors bureaus, and private organizations.  Though this information is very important, some of the most valuable information when planning a trip comes from the people who've been there.   

You'll find people's personal trip reports, recommendations, and experiences, along with message boards and newsgroups where you can ask questions and share experiences with other Molokai visitors.
