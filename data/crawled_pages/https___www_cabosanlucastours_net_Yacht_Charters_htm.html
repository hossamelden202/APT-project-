CABO SAN LUCAS YACHT CHARTER FAQ's
Q: CAN YOU SET SOMETHING SPECIAL UP FOR MY HONEYMOON / WEDDING?
A: Absolutely. We have a whole range of private yachts for charter to suit all budgets and group sizes.  We can customize them any way you like, to make your experience as luxurious as you can imagine, or keep things simple, it's up to you.  Romantic dinners for two, private yachts for birthday parties or wedding rehearsal dinners, weddings at sea, bachelor parties, whatever your special occasion is, we can arrange a special day or evening for you.   Request a Custom Quote
Q: DO YOU CHARTER TO GAY GROUPS?
A: Of course.  Everyone is welcome on any of our tours, including our yacht charters. Let us set something special up for your group.  Request a Custom Quote
Q: CAN YOU DO A MULTI-DAY CHARTER, PERHAPS TO ANOTHER CITY?
A: You bet!  Request a Custom Quote and we can quote you a trip to another Mexican city, or a multi-day charter for your group.
Q: DO YOU OFFER BAREBACK RENTALS?
A: No. We offer only crewed yacht charters.  Bareback rentals are not allowed for insurance reasons.  But, this way you have someone to wait on you hand and foot all day, or all trip long.  You are welcome to assist the captain in sailing the boat, and you will still have all the freedom of a bareback charter, just without the responsibility!
Q:WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
