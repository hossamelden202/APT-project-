Government Programs

This is a really great program for women facilitated by Indiana DNR. Be sure to check out the many workshops they offer for a wide variety of outdoor activities and skills. Learn about things like fishing, shooting sports, canoeing, camping, mountain biking, horseback riding, wilderness survival and much more. Plus, have fun and meet wonderful people!

 

Consumer Outdoor/Sport Shows

Each January you'll find the Indy RV Expo at the Indiana State Fairgrounds in Indianapolis.
Each January in South Bend you'll find the Valley RV & Camping Show in South Bend. You'll even find some closeout models at blowout prices.
Taking place in January, the Outdoor Sports, Lake & Cabin Show in Ft. Wayne features boats, water sports, RVs, motor sports, hunting & fishing, vacation and travel, log cabins, hiking, biking and more.
Indianapolis Boat, Sport & Travel Show Taking place each February, the Indianapolis Boat, Sport & Travel Show features boats, RVs, everything fishing, travel and tourism and much more.
Indiana Deer, Turkey & Waterfowl Expo Find out all about the Indiana Deer, Turkey & Waterfowl Expo, a consumer hunting show taking place during the Indianapolis Boat, Sport & Travel Show each February.
Indiana Motorcycle Expo Find out all about the Indiana Motorcycle Expo, a consumer motor sports show taking place during the Indianapolis Boat, Sport & Travel Show each February.
February in northern Indiana brings the Fort Wayne RV & Camping Show. Find loads of RVs plus campgrounds and more.
 
Every August you'll find RVs galore at the Midwest RV Super Show in Elkhart, the RV Capital of the World.
 
Fall Boat & RV Show Each fall you'll find a huge selection of boats and RVs at great prices at the Indiana State Fairgrounds in Indianapolis.

 

Environmental Protection Organizations

Friends of Muscatatuck River Society

They work to improve and maintain the Muscatatuck River. They have river cleanups, and they do some recreational paddling.
Indiana Audubon Society

Indiana's oldest conservation organization has a truly stunning site. It is very comprehensive and useful. It rivals this site in terms of breadth and depth of information, with loads of information about birds.
Indiana Lakes Management Society This group promotes and encourages the understanding and comprehensive management of lakes and reservoirs and their watershed ecosystems.
Save the Dunes Council They are dedicated to preserving and protecting Indiana Dunes along Lake Michigan.
The purpose of Save Maumee Grassroots Organization is to preserve, protect and improve the ecosystems of the Upper Maumee River & Maumee Watershed by increasing public awareness through advocacy, collaboration, education, and hands-on projects.
Sierra Club - Hoosier Chapter

They have several different groups within the state including, Heartlands Group, Five Rivers Group, and Dunelands Group.

They are an environmental organization that also goes on a lot of different types of trips.
Wildcat Guardians This group of people is dedicated to protecting and improving the health and beauty of Wildcat Creek.

Paddling Clubs & Resources

This is a really great site about canoe camping. It has a listing of the Top 40 U.S. rivers to enjoy float trips of a week or longer and lots of other information about canoe camping.
Cincy Paddlers Based out of Cincinnati, Ohio. They are an online group that plans a lot of trips and has a large member base.
Fort Wayne Canoe & Kayak Mostly racers, they paddle Tues. & Thurs. at 5:30 pm at Shoaff Park - St. Joseph River & Cedar Creek
Fort Wayne Paddle Club Designed to connect kayakers in the Fort Wayne area. They also provide an opportunity to learn about local paddling spots and small regional trips.
Friends of Muscatatuck River Society

They work to improve and maintain the Muscatatuck River. They have river cleanups, and they do some recreational paddling.
Hoosier Canoe & Kayak Club This is the biggest club in the state, and it is very active. Several of the members have sent in some trip reports.
IYAK - Indiana Kayakers

Primarily a recreational group, IYAK plans trips all over Indiana, and even a few out of state.

Indiana Kayak & Canoe Racing

 The Indiana Division of the United States Canoe Association.
Indy Yaks Kayak Fishing Association

This is Central Indiana's premier kayak fishing association. The group was formed with one thing in mind..."fish, and fish often, from a kayak or similar vessel".
Michiana Outdoors Michiana Outdoors Recreation Enthusiasts is a very informal group. No membership is needed - just show up to paddle! Their mission is to meet new people, and encourage folks to enjoy the great natural resources in the Michiana area.
Lots of reference information for paddling and much more.
Northwest Indiana Paddling Association

They are a group of 200+ members that is working to open up water trails in Northwest Indiana.

South Bend Paddling Club This is an informal group of individuals, families, and youth from the South Bend area with an interest in paddling activities. They gather for weekly group paddles, mainly on the St. Joseph River, but also to different lakes and rivers in the region. This group is for both the amateur and the competitive paddler.
The South Bend Adventure Club This is an active club that does all kinds of outdoor trips throughout the Michiana area and beyond. Be sure to also check out their nice Outside the Bend blog.
Viking Canoe Club This is an active club that focuses on whitewater. They are actually located in Louisville, KY.
West Michigan Paddlers Based in West Michigan. They are an online group that plans a lot of trips.
