Molokai Discounts & Bargains

 This is the place to come to save a little money when visiting Molokai, Hawaii.  These brokers can offer discounts on many tours, hotels, dining, and activities throughout Hawaii.  We bought an Entertainment Book, which paid for itself during our stay four times over.  

A discount travel broker like Cheap Tickets, Orbitz, Hotwire, or TravelNow can make comparison shopping a breeze.  They automatically search thousands of travel options to find some really incredible deals on vacation packages, airfare, car rentals, hotels, and even all-inclusive packages.  Don't just shop at one, though, because the deals are different from place to place and agency to agency.  You don't want to miss the one for you!  Priceline.com allows you to put a price on how much you want to pay, which can lead to phenomenal savings, but also puts some restrictions on your travel itinerary.  If you are a bit flexible, give it a shot.  You might be glad you did!  Another way to score real savings is to try E-Bay, the world's largest auction house.  You can bid on travel packages, airfares, car rentals, and more. 

You can also save big on tours and attractions through a discount broker such as Tom Barefoot's Discount Tours.  They book so many tours that they are offered big bulk discounts from the tour operators in exchange for their services.  Through these brokers, you can book tours and activities on all the islands of Hawaii.

It's important to comparison shop for the tours and activities that you've already decided that  you're going to do on your trip, and it's always a good idea to visit the official homepage of the tour or activity to get the real scoop on what exactly you're paying for and a bit about the company itself.  When purchasing a discount card, make sure to check the list thoroughly to make sure that the attractions you want are participating in the program.

If you are visiting more than just Molokai, the Entertainment Book is definitely on the top of the list.  You can save big on restaurants, activities, hotels, and more on all the Hawaiian Islands.  If you are spending your entire vacation on Molokai, check out some of the offers that await you!
