Seattle Parks - Best Seattle Beaches

There are many wonderful beaches in the Seattle area. Best Seattle beaches include: Alki Beach (West Seattle - white sandy beaches, great view of the Seattle skyline), Discovery Park and Golden Gardens

Seattle Visitor Information

One could spend days, of course. Best Seattle attractions include: Seattle Pike Place Market (a favorite for the people scene, savoring bites from the vendor food stalls and small street cafes), Seattle Center (Seattle Space Needle, Experience Music Project, Pacific Science Center), Seattle Art Museum, Seattle Aquarium, Seattle Underground Tour, Argosy Tours, Woodland Park (the oldest zoo on the west coast- !!) - fabulous for strolling, people watching.
Tacoma Visitor Information

Again, too much to choose from, but best Tacoma attractions include: International Glass Museum, Washington State History Museum, Tacoma Art Museum, Point Defiance (Zoo and Aquarium, botanical gardens, 14 miles of lush trails through old-growth forest, Owen Beach) and the ferry to Vashon Island. Tacoma has the longest stretch of public beach access of any city in the U.S. - walkways, piers, great restaurants, views, microbreweries along Ruston Way in Tacoma WA.
Tacoma Parks

Favorite parks include Point Defiance of course, Lakewold Gardens, Titlow Beach and Brown's Point (fun watching fisherman pull out one salmon after another during season). Great view looking back at Tacoma, Mount Rainier and the cargo ships anchored in Commencement Bay.
