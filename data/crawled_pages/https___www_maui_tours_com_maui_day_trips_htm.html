We offer the best Maui Day Trips and Excursions that you'll find on the web.  We use only the most reputable and professional guides, and we offer big savings over other booking agencies.   Your Maui Day Trip or Excursion is bound to be a good one, whether by helicopter, catamaran, ferry, or airplane.

Day Trip - Maui to Oahu & Pearl Harbor
Join Polynesian Adventure Tours as you travel from Maui to the island of Oahu and experience Pearl Harbor, the Arizona Memorial, USS Missouri and much more!
   

Day Trip - Maui to Oahu & Polynesian Cultural Center
Fly from Maui to Oahu and join Polynesian Adventure Tours for a sightseeing tour of Oahu’s most popular scenic attractions and Hawaii’s #1 visitor attraction, the Polynesian Cultural Center!
   

Molokai Adventure Tour
Cruise from Maui to Molokai, “The Most Hawaiian Island,” and spend the day exploring tropical trails, beautiful white sand beaches, and dramatic views with a local guide, or on a self-guided tour!
   

Expeditions Lanai Ferry

Ultimate Adventure in Paradise

Volcano Air Tours
Journey with Volcano Air Tours on a Maui volcano tour. See the beauty of Maui from the air and travel over to the Big Island of Hawaii for a tour of the Kilauea Volcano!
