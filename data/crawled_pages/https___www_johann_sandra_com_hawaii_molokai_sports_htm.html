Molokai Sports & Recreation

Although Molokai is relatively undeveloped for tourism compared to the larger islands, it still offers many recreational opportunities.  You'll be so busy snorkeling, scuba diving, surfing, hiking, and mountain biking, that you may forget about sunning yourself on the beautiful beaches!
