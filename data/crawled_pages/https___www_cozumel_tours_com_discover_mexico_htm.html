DISCOVER MEXICO COZUMEL

Discover Mexico is a unique, one-of-a-kind theme park and museum located in Cozumel Mexico.  Here you are swept through the diverse culture, history, and architecture of the great country of Mexico, and have a chance to explore the many tourist attractions of the country in one place. 

The tours of the park are guided, so your tour guide will help explain and introduce you to the wonders of Mexico, as you traverse miniature scale models of the famous sites and places in Mexico, watch interactive films, and explore native art, handicrafts, and ancient artifacts from around Mexico.  This is a fun introduction to the culture for the whole family.

DISCOVER MEXICO FAQ's

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I VISIT DISCOVER MEXICO?
A: Yes!  It's easy to visit the park during a cruise stop.  If booking the park entrance only, we recommend that you plan a second excursion for the rest of the day - maybe a beach break - since the park will only take you a couple hours to tour.  We've even set one up for you, the Chankanaab combo.  A great shore excursion idea.

Q: WHERE IS DISCOVER MEXICO LOCATED?
A: About 7 minutes south of downtown, a 10 minute walk from the Puerta Maya cruise pier.  From the International Pier, it's about a 1 minute taxi ride.  The address is Km 5.5, Carretera Costera Sur, Cozumel.

Q: BY THEME PARK, DO YOU MEAN AMUSEMENT PARK?
A: No, more like an interactive museum really.  The park is an easy way to experience Mexico's history and culture through exhibits of the nation's highlights and architecture, video screens and films introducing you to Mexico, and an art gallery featuring native artifacts and handicrafts.
