 Puerto Vallarta Gyms and Health Spas

A growing number of these gyms/spas/massage/fitness centers in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico noted here are gay-owned (**) that I am aware of: Acqua Spa, Blue Massage Spa (photo below), Pump Gym at the luxury gay guesthouse Casa Cupula, Spartacus Sauna, The Wellness Room PV, and the new Vaman Spa. Power Fitness Gym, Pump, and The Fit Club offer gym workout facilities located in the South Side or Old Town area. The Fit Club is probably now the most popular option on this side of town. Several of the facilities also offer pilates and yoga which have become somewhat popular in recent years as people are now more concerned about overall lifestyle, diet and health. Please call the particular person, Vallarta gym, studio, massage therapist, day spa, yoga or workout locale for details because lessons, hours and prices do change from time to time and try as I might I can't keep it all up-to-date. In addition, now many of the Nuevo Vallarta and local hotels and luxury resorts offer their own massage facilities and spa services, so you may wish to contact them as well.

Acqua Spa - South Side/Zona Romantica - 450 Constitucion at F. Rodriguez. Tel: 322-178-0856  **
A unique professional spa experience for men only. High quality of service and facilities. The Aqua Spa for Men all male staff "is dedicated to making your stay an unforgettable experience." Changing rooms, various massage techniques, facial, Vichy bath, Turkish body scrub. Trip Advisor #1 spa in Bahia de Banderas (Banderas Bay) in 2018; also currently top rated in the Romantic zone. "Excellent massage. Friendly staff and super professional massage therapists. The spa is super clean and well run. Totally recommend this place for some relaxation." Book by phone or online through their automated system where you can check out their menu of services. See the Aqua Spa (Facebook page) too for more info, photos and reviews. Street parking and lot available.  Daily, 10am-8pm.

Blue Chairs Spa - Los Muertos Beach - calle Malecon and Almendro #4.  Tel: 222-5040
Certified therapists offering professional massage services of deep tissue, shiatsu, swedish, facials, reflexology, stretching, lymphatic drainage and special spa treatments such as clay, chocolate, seaweed wrap. At the well-known Puerto Vallarta Blue Chairs gay resort-hotel. 

Blue Massage Spa - Zona Romantica - 411 Olas Altas, upstairs, Tel: 322-222-6034 and at 332 Basilio Badillo, Interior B, Tel: 322-365-1916  **
"The premier spa in the Romantic Zone of Puerto Vallarta. Your vacation won't be complete until you are pampered by one of our professional massage therapists." (photo left) You can choose not only massage but also treatments such as facials, sea salt scrubs, reflexology, Reiki, or clay treatments. House service is available. Gay owned and operated, visit their Blue Massage Spa website for further info. Good body work, friendly service; rated among the best. "Edwin was wonderful. Blue Massage staff very nice and answer all my questions. Very clean and professional. Great rates and easy set appointments. Highly recommend."  Daily, 9am-8:30pm 

Central  Gym - Downtown/Centro - 932 Morelos street.  Tel: 322-222-9472
The main physical fitness center in downtown Vallarta, up one block from the McDonalds at the northern end of the Malecon and 1/2 block over from Hidalgo Park. Plenty of modern cardio and strength training machines, treadmills, free weights and dumbbells, and some Cybex and Hoist equipment. Trainers are available. As of February 2022, the Central Gym Centro (visit their facebook page) charges 100 pesos per day and 500 pesos monthly. Clientele is a mix of locals and snow birds  Mon-Fri, 7am-10pm; Sat, 7am-5pm. 

Curves for Women - 178 Ave. Los Tules, locals 8 & 9-D in Plaza Caracol.  Tel: 225-6063
Popular Puerto Vallarta gym and weight control spa for women, with circuit machines and workout area. Approximately 400 pesos per month, but varies. Curves for Women recently moved over from the South Side to the Plaza Caracol shopping mall.  Mon-Fri, 7am-12:30pm & 3:30pm-8:30pm; Sat, 8am-Noon.

DavannaYoga School  - Downtown - 542 Matamoros at Corona street.  Tel: 322-147-7008
Traditional style yoga studio school in Vallarta. Bilingual yoga shala certified by the Yoga Alliance. Classes are held in a renovated historic home with high ceilings and natural floors. Offers all levels group classes and private instruction. Featuring Vinyasa flow, Hatha, children's yoga, morning/sunset, and yogic arts classes. Events include a monthly Kirtan (chanting and singing). With yoga instructors Anna Laurita, Sam Cress, Willis Johnston, Emily Cesca, Leona Downey, Alejandra Suarez, Andrew Dowdy as well as Kirtan master Raoul Diaz de la Garza. Further information on Davannayoga visit their yoga studio site. Mon, Weds & Fri, 9am-9pm; Tues and Thurs, 7:30am-6:40pm; Sat, 10am-1pm.

FitneZone Gym - Col. Residencial Albatros - 625 Prisciliano Sanchez.  Tel: 322-276-4568
Very large gym and studio space on three different floors with state-of-art-equipment: free and integrated weights, equipment for cardiovascular, abdomen and legs, boxing bags and showers. FitneZone (facebook) is not just a gym but also offers zumba, Tae Bo, boxing, and nutritional coaching services. Reasonably priced at 100 pesos for one day, 400 pesos per month, 3 months for 700 pesos, etc. Mostly Mexican/national clients; air conditioned. Located next to the Hotel Real Fluvial since 2011, with parking available.  Mon-Fri, 6am-12am; Sat, 7am-6pm.

Gold's Gym - North Side/Hotel District - Pablo Picasso street, Centro Commercial Villas Vallarta.   
Now Closed. Offering life fitness, hammer strength, iron grip and cardiovascular equipment. This well-known Puerto Vallarta gym also with jacuzzi, lift training, sauna, classes in aerobics, muay thai, pilates, ritmo latino, spinning, tai chi, yoga and zumba. Personal trainers, wireless internet and a juice bar. Probably the most popular and best bodybuilding workout gym in town at present and very well equipped. Golds Gym was certainly a gay Puerto Vallarta favorite. A short 10-15 minute taxi ride from the South Side-Zona Romantica. Located across the street from Plaza Genovesa. 850 pesos monthly. Mon-Fri, 6am-11pm; Sat, 7am-7pm; Sun, 9am-4pm.

Turkish coffee is served while you pause between services, to invigorate your senses and open you fully to the experience at hand. Currently by appointment only. For further full details visit their Hamam PV website or see their page at facebook.  Mon-Sat, 10am-10pm
