Key to the City will publish information about your city under the following topics at NO COST:

  1. CITY PROFILE - items such as: population, elevation, climate, type of community, etc. (no charge)
  2. Your CITY MOTTO or SLOGAN (no charge)
  3. A brief CITY HISTORY - approximately one (1) typewritten page (no charge)
  4. COMMUNITY EVENT - date, place, time, sponsor of event and a brief description (no charge)
  5. CITY GOVERNMENT (no charge)
  6. LIBRARY (no charge)
  7. SCHOOL (no charge)
  8. ORGANIZATION or CLUB (no charge)
  9. COMMUNITY NEWS (no charge)
  10. HISTORICAL EVENTS - biographies, events, families, stories, legends, etc. (no charge)
  11. NOTED NOTABLES - famous and notable people who are from the area or have some ties to an area. (no charge)
  12. INTERESTING FACTS (Miscellaney) (no charge)

  13. IT HAPPENED HERE - prominent events related to a specific area (no charge)

TO SUBMIT INFORMATION:

  • PLEASE, include the name of the community and the state when submitting information.
  • E-mail the information using our regular email address: newinfo@usacitiesonline.com
  • Check everything you submit for accuracy and readability
  • All submissions must be accompanied by the following information or the item can not be published: Name, Position or Office (if applicable), city name and Address of Submitter. The Position or Office is always applicable if the submittal information is about, or for a group or organization.

email to: newinfo@usacitiesonline.com

We thank you for your interest. Please tell others in your city.

All submissions become the property of Key to the City and can not be returned.
