Our Maui Experience

December 18, 2001

We rolled out of bed at 5:00AM, racing to catch the ferry to Lanai at 6:45AM.  We sped up the coastline, getting stuck behind some SLOW cars, and nearly missed the ferry.  We left the Jeep in a No Parking Zone, hoping it wouldn't be towed, but there was no parking, and the ferry was leaving!  Visit our Lanai site to read about our experience on Lana'i.

When we arrived back in Lahaina that evening, we were drenched from the ferry ride, but we decided to head into Kobe's Japanese Grill for dinner, where they greeted us with flames and amazing cutlery skills with a sense of humor.  The food was outstanding as well.  We headed into the dive shop next door to look for new fins for me, as our dive off Lanai was tomorrow.  After seeing the prices, I decided to call B&B first, and they had found my fin!  We motored back to Kihei and picked it up, and got rested up for tomorrows dive.
