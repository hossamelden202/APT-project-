Radio-SkyPipe II - An Internet Enabled Strip Chart Recorder

*

 

  • Collect data on your PC using your sound card or a simple analog to digital converter.
  • Store, retrieve, and edit strip chart files with header information secured within the file itself.
  • Share your real-time data with others over the internet even if you use a dial up connection.
  • Send live data to multiple recipients.
  • Send and receive data simultaneously from multiple sources.
  • Use in Stand Alone mode for local use only.
  • A built in chat window allows you to communicate with others during observations.
  • Can be used for radio astronomy, seismology, weather, any scientific or industrial monitoring task.
  • Automate real world tasks using LabJack ADCs, control industrial processes, home heating, solar power.
  • New (2013). Connect directly to your Arduino projects and graph your data!.

Requirements:

  • Windows NT/2000/Xp/Vista/Win7/Win8/Win10  

  • 16 bit Sound card and/or an Analog to Digital Converter for Stand Alone or Server operation.**

  • 512 MB of RAM recommended.

  • Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0 or better.

  • Internet connection if want to share data on line.

  • Local geomagnetic field strength.

  • Various RF field strengths.
