Design, Construction & Trades

For more information, please contact us at:
Design, Construction and Trades Department
Email: construct@cscc.edu
Phone: 614-287-5949
Davidson Hall - Suite 205 (second floor)

Scholarships Exclusive
to DCT Students

Hours of Operation

Monday -- Thursday:  8:00 AM to 5:00 PM for in-person inquiries

Friday:  8:00 AM to 5:00 PM for virtual/email inquiries

 

Prospective Students:  We encourage you to call and schedule an appointment with our faculty and advisors. They are more than happy to meet with you to discuss our programs, degrees, and certificates and help you find the plan of study that is right for you.

Business Owners:  In addition to our college-credit degree and certificate programs, we have a long history of working with regional businesses to provide customized training solutions tailored specifically for their needs. Sessions can be held on our campus or at your location ... whatever best suits your needs.
