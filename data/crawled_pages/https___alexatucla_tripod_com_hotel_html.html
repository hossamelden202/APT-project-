LOS ANGELES : HOTELS
THE VIEW, THE PROUD, THE COOL PLACES TO STAY!

SUNSET TOWER
8358 Sunset Blvd - (323)654-7100
You are inside a 1920s Art Deco Hotel, in the middle of the busy Sunset Strip, the Hotel-Bar and Restaurant is called Fenix, which is one of the hippest places...HOW COOL IS THAT??? To top it off, the Pool overlooks Los Angeles and is a great place for Sunday Brunch , even if you dont stay there! Also hosts a cool Club on Fridays's check clublistings for more info!

THE STANDARD
8300 Sunset Blvd - (323)650-9090
A great, hip place to stay in L.A. ...Everyone working here looks good, (even the gardner, aehm, "landscape artist")decorated with 60s kitsch, a cool pool, and amazing people to talk to, the lobby fishtank is filled with a live model , a barbershop and a 24 hrs. DOWNTOWN STANDARD
550 S.Flower Street - Downtown L.A.
With the same trendy features as it's West Hollywood sister, this new Hotel quickly became the place to stay. The rooftop Pool and Bar area is a must do on everyones L.A.list now. The rooms are minimalist, comfortable and the lowest rates start at $99. Even if you live in L.A.and want a vacation within the City, I would suggest checking the one out!

THE CHEAP(ER), BUT STILL GOOD PLACES !

THE CADILLAC HOTEL
8 Dudley - Venice Beach - (310)399-8876
Built in 1914 to accommodate the booming vision of Abbot Kinney, The Cadillac has become a Venice landmark. The hotel borders Santa Monica and sits on the famous boardwalk, overlooking the Pacific Ocean . Recently the hotel has been renovated to meet the modern needs of any traveler. Rich with history and character, the hotel welcomes you with the bohemian charm the city was built upon. Guests can experience the surf and sun, along with all the unique arts, music, and special performances that has made Venice a top California destination.
The guests are mostly international, europeans who like to explore and have fun while on vacation. Lots of friendships have been made here!

HOTEL CARMEL
201 Broadway - Santa Monica - (310)451-2469
2 Blocks to the Beach - 1 Block to Third Street Promenade - 2 Hot Nightclubs in the Building (217 and Bar Vida) - The Best Value in Santa Monica! Rates starting at $64, you cant beat that, considering the average room rate in Santa Monica is $200 and up!I usually put my parents in here, so u know it's all good!
