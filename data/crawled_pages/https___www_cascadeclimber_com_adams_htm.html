Mt. Adams from near Trout Lake. The South Spur route follows just inside the right skyline.

Mount Adams, at 12,276', is the second highest point in Washington State. Though somewhat similar in appearance to its northerly cousin, Rainier, the standard route (South Spur) on Adams is much less of an undertaking than the standard route on Rainier.
