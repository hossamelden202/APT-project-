THREE CORNER ROCK LOOKOUT
R.I.P. 1938-1955; 1955-1973
Likely developed by the then Columbia National Forest, the first structure was a 2-story L-4 cab in 1938. This was replaced by a DNR cab in 1955, followed by an identical DNR cab in 1973. This cab was sold and removed by a private party in 1982.
Washington State Division of Forestry/DNR
9 miles northwest of Stevenson
Skamania County, Washington
Elevation 3550'
