MAUI PARASAILING TOURS

Maui Parasailing is a unique experience, where you're strapped to a parachute, sitting in a harness, and are whisked away by a speed boat, lifting you to hundreds of feet in the air.  The view is breathtaking, and it's an experience you'll always remember. 

Soar above it all with an experienced crew and the latest in lift-off and landing technology. Fly away in ease and comfort. You may be a likely abductee. Alone or with a companion, it can happen to anybody! The young, the elderly and the physically challenged.

UFO Parasail, one of the pioneers of Maui parasailing,  utilizes state-of-the-art winchboats, to float you on and off the back of the boat for safe, dry landings (single or tandem). Enjoy a scenic boat ride while waiting your turn to rise above it all.

Observers are welcome; No experience necessary.
