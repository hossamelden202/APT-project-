MAUI HANA TOURS

The road to Hana is a breathtaking jaunt along the North coast of Maui, winding through hairpin curves over countless 1-lane bridges, all the while surrounded by fantastic topography, lush tropical forests, endless waterfalls and scenic vistas.  It'll take you most of the day, but it is truly one of the most beautiful drives anywhere.

We offer the best Maui Hana Tours that you'll find on the web.  We use only the most reputable and professional guides, and we offer big savings over other booking agencies.   You can ride in air conditioned buses and vans, while viewing nature's majesty, with knowledgable tour guides who know Maui inside and out, giving you a very beautiful, fun, and interesting look at the island of Maui, Hawaii.
