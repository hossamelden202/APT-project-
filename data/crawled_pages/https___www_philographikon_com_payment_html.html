VAT tax for shipment within the European Union (EU )

The VAT tax for shipment within the European Union is 7% ( seven per cent ) of the purchase price. This price must be added to the purchase price for all our EU customers.

For oil paintings, watercolors, acrylic, chalk and ink drawings, the VAT tax for customers in the European Union is 19% ( nineteen per cent )
