Elevate Your Experience Highlights

In-Room Dining

In-Room Dining offers farm-to-table breakfast, lunch, and dinner all within the comfort of your guestroom. With 24-hour service, our locally sourced dishes may be enjoyed at any time of day.

Poolside Cabana Rental

The Ritz-Carlton, Marina del Rey has two private cabanas that overlook the waterfront pool. All rentals are available for hotel guests only and include: All-day food & beverage service. One chilled fruit presentation. One refrigerator stocked with assorted non-alcoholic beverages. One bottle of California Champagne (Domaine Chandon). Access to HDTV.
