Farewell to Mount Shuksan
From the base of the summit pyramid, we looked back and watched the sunrise on Mount Baker. The rock pinnacle at left is Point 7935, the most prominent of the many outcrops marking the west edge of the Sulphide Glacier. Just to the right (west) of it is a steep drop of over 3000 ft down to Lake Ann and Shuksan Creek in the valley below.
