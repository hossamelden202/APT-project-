PUERTO VALLARTA WATERSPORTS FAQ's

Q: WHAT IS THE WATER TEMPERATURE LIKE IN PUERTO VALLARTA?
A: During the summer months, the water is bathtub warm, approaching 85?F.  You will be very comfortable in just your swimsuit.  During the winter months, it cools down significantly, and you often find temperatures dipping into the mid-to-high 60s, so if you're visiting during those months you might want to consider a shorty wetsuit for your watersports, though if it's sunny most people still just wear a swimsuit. 

Q: WHAT AGE DO MY KIDS HAVE TO BE TO RIDE THE WAVE RUNNERS?
A:  They can do our wave runner rentals with an adult from 6 years old, .  However, they must be 16 years old to drive solo without an adult.
