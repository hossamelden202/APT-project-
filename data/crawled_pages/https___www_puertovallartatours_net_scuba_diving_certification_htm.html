Puerto Vallarta Scuba Diving Certification FAQ's

Q: I'VE NEVER BEEN SCUBA DIVING BEFORE, BUT WANT TO TRY.  WHAT OPTION SHOULD I PICK?
A: We would recommend starting out with a Discover Scuba Diving Course if you've never tried diving before.  One hour and you're ready to "try before you buy".  That way if you don't like it (though we're sure you will), you haven't wasted all the time and money for a full certification course.

Q: I'VE TRIED DISCOVER SCUBA DIVING AND IT WAS SO FUN!  I WANT TO GET CERTIFIED, WHAT IS MY NEXT STEP?
A: We offer two great options for you.  The first is to take your PADI course right here in the warm sunshine of Puerto Vallarta.  That is our Open Water Certification course, and it will certify you from start to finish to scuba dive for life.  The second is to do our Referral Course, where you will do all your coursework at home before coming to Vallarta, then do your required 4 open water dives to finish out your certification in Puerto Vallarta.  Much more fun than diving in your frozen lakes up north!
