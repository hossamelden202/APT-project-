Blondies Gay Bar
 

South Side - 115 Pulpito at Amapas street, and at 258 Basilio Badillo.  Tel: 322-222-0275
Loft and slush bar featuring frozen cocktails and drinks made with fresh fruit,
fresh squeezed juices, and top shelf spirits bar
for classic cocktails and slushies.
Just up the street from Puerto Vallarta's principal beach, Los Muertos

"Where all good stories begin. Fresh music Fresh drinks Fresh air"

The Blondies Loft stylish gay lounge cocktail bar has pleasant chill out music,
ocean breezes, Bay views and some great Vallarta sunsets from the second floor Loft.
A cool pop art deco style.

They have a delicious charcuterie selection
Appetizers and snacks include cheese, meat and fruit plate selections 
Pet friendly; wireless internet available

Their second Blondies location at 258 Basilio Badillo street features premium frozen and craft cocktails,
with a retail store for all things Blondies - fashion/beach gear

Blondies (facebook) and Los Otros Bondies are welcome additions
to the Romantic Zone neighborhood, I wish them all the luck!
Since September 2015
Gay owned and gay staff, straight friendly mixed clientele

Happy hour, 4pm-6pm
with premium slushies at 50 pesos, 20 peso beers 
and 50% off a selection of wines.
Daily, 10am-2am
