A. BEST ALBUM:



1. RADIOHEAD - "OK Computer"

2. THE VERVE - "Urban Hymns"

3. DEPECHE MODE - "Ultra"

4. BELLE & SEBASTIAN  - "If You're Feeling Sinister"

5. PAVEMENT  - "Brighten The Corners"
Just outside the top 5: Built To Spill - Perfect From Now On, Papas Fritas - Helioself, Stereolab - Dots and Loops, Yo La Tengo - I Can Hear the Heart Beating As One, Motorpsycho - Angels and Daemons at Play, Primal Scream - Vanishing Point, Morrissey - Malajusted.

Comments: Radiohead a given winner with loads of votes. The rest - a pretty close call.

 



B. BEST SINGLE:
