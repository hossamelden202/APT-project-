Here is one more expense that you just don't need during your trip.  Have you ever traveled abroad, left the kids at home, and then called from your hotel to check up on them?  I bet you were flabbergasted at the rates that hotels charge for international long distance!  Expensive hook-up fees, high per-minute rates, or worse yet, no international long distance at all!  Even if you use your calling card provided by your local phone company, the rates are probably astronomical.  Cell phones won't work in Cancun without arranging with the phone company before your trip, and even if you arrange first, the rates are still very high.

We have the solution.  There are many calling cards that specialize in international long distance.  Rates run as low as 9 cents per minute from Cancun Mexico to the U.S.!  That's less than we pay to call across the river to Portland!  Make sure you take one with you just in case you need it.  You don't want to get stuck having to call home from the airport without a calling card.

Listed below are some of the major international calling cards for you to check out.  Each of these companies has many different cards so you can be sure to get the one that best suits your calling style.  Our personal preference is the Global Caller from Zaptel, since it's the cheapest you can get without using a computer, but you can choose the one that fits you.
