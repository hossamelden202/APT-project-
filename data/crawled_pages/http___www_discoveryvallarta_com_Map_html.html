NOTE: The map above shows only the Puerto Vallarta Downtown or El Centro (along the malecon) and the South Side areas
(south of the Cuale River), totaling perhaps 1/10 or less of the entire area of the city & municipality of Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.
The airport and hotel district are located a mile or two north of downtown, while the southern most neighborhood
of the town, known as Conchas Chinas, is just to the south of the gay beach at #1.
