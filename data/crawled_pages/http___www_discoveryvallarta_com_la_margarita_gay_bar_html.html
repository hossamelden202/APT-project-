La Cantina Margarita Gay Bar & Lounge
 

South Side - 257 Lazaro Cardenas at Ignacio Vallarta street.  Tel: 322-222-1367
La Cantina Margarita is located across the street from popular gay disco CC Slaughters
and next door to club La Noche, so it's on the Lazaro Cardenas circuit 
that includes Paco's Ranch, Mr. Flamingo, CC Slaughters, Industry 
and others in that exciting two-three block area.

Trendy cocktail bar cantina La Cantina Margarita is noted for the circular bar inside 
and its colorful and friendly ambience. 
Now open to the street, so there's outside sidewalk seating.

Good reviews and word of mouth; since October 2014. 
Wireless, no smoking.

Happy hour from 2pm-7pm every day and margarita of the day for 30 pesos. 
Daily, 2pm-3am

Gay owned, gay staff, mostly 30-40 something crowd.
Popular with tourists and locals alike since early Autumn 2013.
