Oregon Wine Country Pages

Welcome to our pages on Oregon's Wine Country!  We have only begun to become nouveau wine connoisseurs in the past couple of years, and Oregon is one of the best places around to try new wines and some of the best Pinot Noir you can find anywhere!   The sites we've listed below have great resources about Oregon Wine, from vineyard maps to reviews and descriptions of vineyards and wineries.  We've also prepared our own reviews of the wineries we've been to so far!  To read our reviews and see a few photos, click on the arrow below.  To view our Oregon Wine Slide Show, click on "View Slide Show" below.
