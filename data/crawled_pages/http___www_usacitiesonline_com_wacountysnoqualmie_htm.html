Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Snoqualmie is approximately 1,610 (1997), 10670 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 637 (1990), 3547 (2010).
The amount of land area in Snoqualmie is 7.944 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0.069 sq kilometers.
The distance from Snoqualmie to Washington DC is 2441 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 62 miles. (as the crow flies)
Snoqualmie is positioned 47.52 degrees north of the equator and 121.81 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Snoqualmie median income is $ 36,000 (1994).

Attractions

Snoqualmie Falls - an internationally known, beautiful waterfall which cascades down 268 feet.

Mt. Si, the second most climbed mountain in Washington State!

Snoqualmie is 20 miles down Snoqualmie pass from a well-known ski resort called: "The Summit at Snoqualmie."
It has everything from beginner to expert runs, high speed quads and even a tubing center. It also has one of the best half pipes, and snowboard parks.
In the spring The Summit is the host for Board Stiff. Put on by the local Radio station. It has a big air contest and live music.
