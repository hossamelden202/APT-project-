Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Arlington is approximately 10,640 (2000), 17926 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 1,600 (1990), 6563 (2010).
The amount of land area in Arlington is 14.266 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0.01 sq kilometers.
The distance from Arlington to Washington DC is 2469 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 86 miles. (as the crow flies)
Arlington is positioned 48.17 degrees north of the equator and 122.13 degrees west of the prime meridian.

Location

 12 miles north of Everett about halfway between Seattle and Bellingham
