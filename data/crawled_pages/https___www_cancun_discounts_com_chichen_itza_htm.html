To make the most of your incredible experience at Chichen Itza, you'll want to be sure to hook up with one of our extremely knowledgeable and informative guides.  We took this tour ourselves with a guide and we learned so much about the Mayan culture and the wonder of the architecture, astronomy, and advanced civilization of the Mayas.

Located 149 miles from Cancun towards Merida, it extends approximately 2.5 miles from north to south, and arose at the end of the Classic period in the year 900 AD until the start of the Post-classic in 1200 AD. Rediscovered in 1842, it is not until the twentieth century that its restoration begins along with its resurgence as one of the most important and valuable sites of Mayan culture and basically humanity.
