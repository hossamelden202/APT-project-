Short on time, but want to get in some snorkeling during your day in Puerto Vallarta? We'll head directly for one of the best snorkeling areas in Puerto Vallarta, the Los Arcos Marine Park, or if the water is a bit rough on your day, we'll head for the protected cove of Colomitos instead, so you can have the best experience.

When you arrive at the snorkeling location, your guide will explain to you about the ecosystem, how to use the snorkeling equipment, and will use a chart to share with you the types of fish you'll likely be seeing underwater.  He'll also explain to you about the park's exclusive inhabitants - many types of seabirds such as pelicans and the rare blue footed booby, found only in Vallarta and the Galapagos Islands.  You'll spend as much time as you like, up to 1.5 hours, snorkeling with beautiful multi-colored tropical fish and other amazing marine life, and your guide will be with you throughout your snorkeling experience.

Cruise Passengers: This excursion is flexible to fit all cruise ship schedules.  You can be picked up on the cruise pier for a $70.00 fee, payable in cash the day of the tour, if you want this option add a note in special requests when booking.
