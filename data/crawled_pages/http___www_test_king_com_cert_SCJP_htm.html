Oracle Corporation is US-based leading global corporation that deals with computer technology. It is headquartered at Redwood City in California, USA. The company has gained worldwide recognition for manufacturing and trading computer hardware and enterprise software including database management systems.

Ellison, Bob Miner and Ed Oates co-founded Oracle Corporation in 1977 as Software Development Laboratories (SDL). In 1982, RSI took it's current name Oracle Systems Corporation matching to its groundbreaking innovation Oracle Database.

Technology

Oracle Corporation has innovated and manufactured series of products regarding computer technology. The most popular products of the Oracle Technology family includes, WebCenter (WCE) Products, Oracle Databases, Application Server (AS) Products, Business Intelligence Technology Products (like, BI Suite EE plus, BI, BI SE1, SE, etc.), Hyperion BI Technology Products, Collaboration products, Identity Management (IDM) Products & Tools, Application Products, etc.

The company also develops essential tools for database development as well as systems of customer relationship management software (CRM), middle-tier software, supply chain management (SCM) software, enterprise resource planning software (ERP), etc.
