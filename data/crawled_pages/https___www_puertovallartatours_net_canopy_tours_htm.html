Q: IS THERE A WEIGHT LIMIT FOR THE CANOPY TOURS?
A: The weight limit for El Eden, is 250 lbs.  For the Rio Cuale Canopy Tour is 242 lbs, and 220 lbs for the Cuale Mountain Expedition.

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE - HOW DO I GET THERE?
A: Take a yellow taxi to the canopy tour meeting location specified on your confirmation ticket.

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE - WHAT TIME SHOULD I REQUEST?
A: We recommend cruise passengers try to book the 11:00 AM time or later. The ships are often not on Puerto Vallarta Local time, which is CST.  They are often still on ship time, which is MST, one hour earlier, plus it takes time to go through security and get off the ship.  It's better safe than sorry.
