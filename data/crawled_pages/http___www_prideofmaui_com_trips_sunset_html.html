Why Choose Us?

We’re a world-renowned Maui tour company offering exceptional snorkeling and whale-watching tours. We’re highly rated for our state-of-the-art catamarans, expert crew, and prime snorkeling and whale-watching locations, making us the best choice for Maui snorkeling and Maui whale-watching tours!

Our team! We are a family-owned and operated business with over 40 years experience in Hawaiian waters. Our Captains and many of our crew have been with us for decades. We are organized experts dedicated to providing the safest and best experience possible. Our team is passionate about sharing their knowledge and providing an unsurpassed level of guest service. We also stand out due to our commitment to sustainability and environmental preservation. We use eco-friendly practices, provide informative and educational experiences, and actively support marine conservation efforts in Maui.

We visit pristine Maui snorkeling spots like Molokini Crater and Coral Gardens. Our guests will experience crystal-clear waters, a rich marine ecosystem, and opportunities to encounter colorful fish, turtles, and other marine life.

The best time for whale watching in Maui is from December to April (peak season is February and March), when the North Pacific humpback whales travel to Maui for their annual winter migration. Guests can witness these magnificent creatures breaching, tail-slapping, and socializing during this period.

The whales most commonly seen by visitors on a vacation to Maui are the North Pacific humpback whales. Many other species of whales are known to frequent the waters surrounding Maui though, like the False Killer Whale, Pygmy Killer Whale, Pilot Whale, and Orca. There have also been recorded sightings of Sperm Whales, which are extremely rare!

Pride of Maui offers tours suitable for guests of all ages and skill levels. Snorkeling equipment and guidance are provided, making it an accessible and enjoyable experience for beginners and experienced snorkelers alike.

What skills are recommended for participants? Participants are advised to have basic swimming skills for a more enjoyable experience.

What is the level of difficulty of the snorkeling tour? The tour is designed to be accessible, allowing most travelers to participate comfortably.

Our guests enjoy a comfortable and well-equipped catamaran with amenities like a spacious sun deck, an indoor cabin area, a freshwater shower, snorkeling gear, and flotation devices. We also have a professional and friendly crew who will provide safety instructions and assist with snorkeling equipment. Don’t forget to ask about SNUBA®!

To book a tour with Pride of Maui, you can make your reservations on our site or call us at 1-808-242-0955.

If you need to cancel a boat tour, please make sure to do so at least 48 hours in advance to receive a refund. Reservations that result in a no-show will not be eligible for a refund.

Hawaii is known for its “micro-climates.” It can be raining hard where you are, yet sunny and calm at the harbor and our destinations. With more than 40 years in the business, we are your best resource for the weather. Our captains are seasoned veterans with extensive local knowledge. If we have not called you, please show up at the harbor with the expectation to go out, even if it is raining or windy. If the conditions are unsafe to operate, you will be given the option to reschedule or cancel with a full refund. All of our destinations are weather permitting.

We accommodate pregnant guests regularly. Please ask your doctor if it is safe for you to be on a boat during your pregnancy. Our vessels are large and stable, but each pregnancy is different and your doctor knows you best.

Diamond Parking Services operates the parking. The lot closest to our slip has a machine that only takes cash. Please note that no change is given back. You can also pay with a credit card by phone by dialing in or using a QR code. The cost of parking is approximately $1 per hour.
