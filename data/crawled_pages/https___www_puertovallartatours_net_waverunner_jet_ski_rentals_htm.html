Spend a day at the beach in Puerto Vallarta, riding wave runners and jet skis!  Enjoy the thrill of the waves in one of these babies and spend a fun packed day at our beautiful beach. After you're done with the waverunners, you can  go to a nearby local seafood restaurant to enjoy a delicious meal and, if you're in to more excitement, you can also go Parasailing or ride a Banana Boat the same day since it's conveniently at the same place! This activity is a perfect choice for a shore excursion for cruise passengers.

Your wave runner rental reservation is a great money-saver with our discount price and is available for you in many different schedules to best fit your fun beach day! No need to hunt around for watersports, we will show you where to go, and absolutely no haggling - just our low discount price all the time!
