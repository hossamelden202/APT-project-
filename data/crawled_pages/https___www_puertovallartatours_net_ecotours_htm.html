ECOTOURISM IN VALLARTA

The tours listed on this page are true Ecotours.  The activities are led by expert marine biologist, biologist, and naturalist guides who don't just show you where to go and leave you alone, they take the time to help you really learn and experience the natural environment of Puerto Vallarta Mexico. These are educational tours fit for kids and adults alike - a true chance to interact with animals and nature. They take care to nurture and care for the environment, not just use it for a tourist attraction.  A portion of your tour cost goes to foundations to support the wildlife and environment of Puerto Vallarta too.
