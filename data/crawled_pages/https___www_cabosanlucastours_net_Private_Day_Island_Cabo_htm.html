La Isla (The Island) is the premier floating island in Cabo San Lucas. You can charter it privately for a private floating beach break and snorkeling tour during the day - popular with cruise ship passengers in particular - or for a luxurious sunset cruise.

It is also available for functions like wedding receptions, rehearsal dinners, family reunions, corporate retreats and more. La Isla has just the right boat for your party, accommodating up to large parties of 24 people! We offer fully decked out with lounge-style seating, shaded areas, restrooms and a full kitchen on board, staffed by an English-speaking wait staff with options for food and drinks on board.
Cruise Passengers: Yes! This exclusive private charter is perfect for a cruise shore excursion! This charter is flexible to fit all cruise ship schedules.

Departs:
The Marina in Cabo San Lucas. Exact Meeting Instructions will Accompany your Ticket.
