Combine all the off-road fun of our Viking Jungle Trek with the thrills of the Rio Cuale Canopy Tour! Start off from Puerto Vallarta and drive your unique 4x4 Yamaha Viking up into the foothills of the Sierra Madre, where fantastic viewpoints, remote swimming holes and of course an amazing network of massive zip lines await you.  The longest zip line is a whopping 1300 feet long and 600 feet high!  Your Viking can take up to 6 passengers and is fully automatic for your convenience.

Departs From:Tour offices located at Rio de Plata #246 Col. Lopez Mateos, Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco. Directions will be provided with your confirmation.

Restrictions:  Maximum rear seat capacity is 450 lbs for Vikings.  This only applies to the back seat, not the front seat. Vikings are fully automatic.

Kids:  Drivers must have a valid drivers license.  Kids ages 4-10 years old ride free but must pay $36.00 in cash onsite if they want todo the canopy tour.  10 and up are the same price as adults.  Kids must be at least 6 years old to do the zip lines.
