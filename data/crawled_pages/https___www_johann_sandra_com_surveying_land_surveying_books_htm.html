Welcome to the biggest dedicated source of Land Surveying Books on the web!  These pages include surveying books from all facets of land surveying and geomatics, including law, datums and adjustments, GIS/LIS, GPS, surveying history, biographies, foreign surveying, technical papers and reports, construction and quantity surveying, test preparation, hydrography and photogrammetry.

Land Surveying Books are an essential element of self-study for the Professional Land Surveyors exams, Land Surveyor In Training Exam, Civil Engineering Exams, and for valuable information about Land Surveying History and Principles.  We also carry a large selection of AutoCAD and Land Development Desktop / LDD Books for the surveying professional.

To visit a section of our bookstore, just click on the links at left.  To read more about a selection, read reviews, place an order, or view pricing and availability, click on the photo or text link.  All orders are processed through a secure server with 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption.
