VALLARTA LAND ACTIVITIES

The highlight of Puerto Vallarta is its beautiful setting, sandwiched between Banderas Bay and the vast jungles of the Sierra Madre. These activities will allow you to explore the lands of Puerto Vallarta as well as the jungle and the surrounding communities. In between boat rides, snorkeling tours, fishing, and scuba diving, you'll love the opportunity to experience Puerto Vallarta by land.  Remember, every reservation gets a free bonus - the Puerto Vallarta discount card! See our land activities below.

   
ATV Tours
