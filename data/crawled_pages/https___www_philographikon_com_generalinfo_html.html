General Information

Our Online offer represents only a very small portion of our inventory. If you don't find what you are interested in, please contact us by any means. We shall reply to your inquiry as quickly as possible.

All our prints are guaranteed to be authentic antiquarian woodcuts, engravings or lithographs printed at our about (ca) the stated date. Coloring, if present, is by hand and described as "original"(at the time of printing or shortly thereafter) or "modern" (20th century). Dimensions of prints are stated in centimeters and inches to the nearest fraction of the engraved surface, height before width.

All offered material has been carefully researched, documented and described. We gladly send you photographs, if there is no image on the Internet or if you so desire. Some hardly visible minor repair work might have been done. Prints have normal age toning. Special faults are mentioned.

The images shown here have been scanned, sometimes from the original print, sometimes from photos. The margins are wider than shown on the images. These images are only to give an impression of the apperance of a print. If you wish other information, please contact us.
