Lift Ticket Discounts and Deals Tips

Finding lift ticket deals for skiing or snowboarding can save you money on your winter sports adventures. Here are some tips and strategies for finding discounted lift tickets:

  • Early Bird Discounts: Many ski resorts offer discounts if you purchase your lift tickets well in advance.
  • Multi-Day Passes: Buying multi-day lift tickets can sometimes result in a lower per-day cost. Consider purchasing a multi-day pass if you plan to ski or snowboard for multiple days during your trip.
  • Online Discounts: Purchasing direct from the ski resorts for online discounts and promotions.
  • Buy Online and Skip the Line: Purchase online in advance and skip the lift ticket window line.
  • Group Discounts: If you're traveling with a group of friends or family, inquire about group discounts. Many resorts offer reduced rates for larger groups.
  • Discount Cards and Memberships: Some ski resorts offer discount cards or memberships that provide savings on lift tickets and other amenities. Examples include the Epic Pass, Ikon Pass, and many other various resort-specific membership programs.
  • Midweek and Non-Peak Days: Lift ticket prices are often lower on weekdays and non-peak days compared to weekends and holidays. Consider planning your trip during these times to save on lift tickets.
  • Ski and Stay Packages: Look for ski and stay packages offered by resorts and lodging providers. These packages often include discounted lift tickets when you book accommodation.
  • Reloadable Cards: Some ski resorts offer reloadable lift ticket cards that can be used for multiple visits. These cards may offer discounts when you reload them for additional days on the mountain.
  • Student and Military Discounts: If you're a student or a member of the military, inquire about special discounts. Many resorts offer reduced rates for these groups.
  • Local Deals and Coupons: Check with local businesses, ski shops, and restaurants near the resort for coupons or deals on lift tickets. Sometimes, these establishments offer discounts in partnership with the resort. Costco sometimes offers early season discounts to resorts in Western Canada.
  • Multi Mountain Passes: These multi-destination passes offers an expanding array of options and cost savings for the avid skier and snowboarder who is looking to ski beyond their local mountain. They often cover resorts in different regions or even countries, offering a wide range of skiing experiences. Check out the vast array of options on our Multi Mountain Multi Day and Season Passes page.
  • Season Passes: If you plan to ski or snowboard frequently at a specific resort, consider investing in a season pass. Season passes often pay for themselves after a few visits and provide unlimited access for the entire season.
  • Keep in mind that lift ticket deals may vary from one ski resort to another and can change throughout the season. Be sure to check the resort's official website, as we provide here, and do some research to find the best deals for your specific destination and dates of travel.
  • Be aware that some resorts limit the number of tickets sold on any given day. Book ahead to ensure you dont miss out on specific days.
