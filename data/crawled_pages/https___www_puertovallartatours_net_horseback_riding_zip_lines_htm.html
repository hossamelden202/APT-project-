Join us for a great adventure, an all-new super combo eco-adventure tour that will surely become one of the hottest excursions in Puerto Vallarta!  Come and enjoy a great horseback riding and canopy eco tour.  This tour will give you the perfect opportunity to experience the natural wonders the Sierra Madre mountains in Puerto Vallarta and best of all is just 40 minutes away!

We will start out tour with a 2 hour riding tour through the mountains up and down trails, crossing rivers and seeing the beautiful jungle of Puerto Vallarta, this is a great way to relax and absorb a gorgeous area south of the Sierra Madre, our tour guides will take you through jungle and small towns all the way into the mountains.  To top it all of, you'll have time to visit popular waterfalls were you can swim if you want or just hang out and enjoy the wonderful views.  A super fun way to spend a day in Puerto Vallarta!

The fun is only just beginning though, now is time to take on one of the most popular activities in Puerto Vallarta: a canopy tour.  Fly through 13 awesome zip lines across mountain tops and caverns,  at great speeds and enjoy the view from up high!  WOW, right?  You'll have some of the funniest and friendliest tour guides in the business, leaving you just saying YESSSSS!!!

Now that your adventure is over you can finally sit down and have a delicious lunch and have some drinks from the open bar and as part of our cultural approach you will receive a small Mexican cooking class on how to make a mexican salsa and also you can make your own hand made tortillas to along with your lunch. This combo is a great choice for the whole family to enjoy a day of adventures in the wild Puerto Vallarta!
