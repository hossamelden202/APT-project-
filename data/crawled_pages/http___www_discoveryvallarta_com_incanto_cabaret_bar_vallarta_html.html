Incanto Cabaret, Restaurant, Bar and Cafe
 

South Side - 109 Insurgentes street. 
Tel: 223-9756

Incanto is located along the Cuale River at the East Bridge,
with indoor and outdoor seating,
and across the river from the famed restaurant Le Bistro.

Incanto cabaret, piano bar and cafe, owned by long-time local Tracy Parks
(who used to schedule and run the entertainment at cabaret-bar The Palm)
takes its name from the charming and elegant setting along the Cuale River
that divides Old Town from downtown in beautiful Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.

Incanto (facebook) features an eclectic offering of world-class entertainment
in both their lower level 90-seat cabaret theater, 
and in their more intimate piano bar space on the 2nd floor. 

Classic cocktails and delicious offerings from our cafe will make Incanto
a destination where you'll want to linger before and after the show,
especially on one of our two riverside terraces. 

Mornings on the riverside terrace at Incanto right along the Cuale River features 
their signature coffee and delicious & creative breakfast selections,
often with live piano music.
