La Cueva Gay Cantina and Bar
 

South Side - 5 de Febero street #260.  Tel: 322-142-6934 and 322-141-2150 
La Cueva (The Cave) has moved recently over to 5 de Febero street but still in the Romantic Zone 
Now located next to the entrance of the well-known No Way Jose restaurant

Previously La Cueva was located for many years at 414 Olas Altas street on the South Side 
practically across the street from Apaches martini bar

 La Cueva cantina is a fun, neighborhood Mexican 
cowboy-style gay cantina bar, 
with friendly Julio and Juanito as two of your bartenders,
definitely consider wearing your cowboy hat!
The place is spacious with a pool table, video screens, wireless internet
and plays pop English and Spanish music. Pets are allowed. 

Please check their website/Facebook page for up-to-date details etc

The original Gay Bingo with great prizes starts late October through mid-April 
every Thursday at 7pm-9:30pm

Karaoke every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday at 7pm.
for 70 pesos enjoy carne asada and a drink


National drinks at 50 pesos; beers normally at 25 pesos.  
Hours: Mon-Sun, 6pm-2am

Gay owned with a gay staff, mostly 40-50 something crowd.
Popular with locals and tourists alike at this new location on 5 de Febero street
Recommended for its friendly and pleasant relaxed atmosphere.
