Sailing on Maui

MAUI SAILING GUIDEBOOKS
 FROM THE HAWAII BOOKSTORE
 The web's largest selection of Maui Books

They say you haven't really seen Maui until you've seen it from the sea.  The jagged peaks of the West Maui Mountains overlook the beaches of Kaanapali and Kapalua, and Haleakala looms large over the island.  The sun is setting behind you, filling the sky with a brilliant display of light, painting the ocean pink, orange, and magenta.   

There are many sailing operations that operate either single-hull sailboats, or catamarans.  Most offer snorkeling tours, champagne sunset tours, whale-watching tours, or some kind of booze cruise.  Some also offer dinner cruises, or just invite you to spend the day on the water, perhaps visiting the neighboring island of Lana'i.  You can even rent your own boat or take sailing lessons on the island!  The dependable afternoon trade winds on Maui make sailing an incredible experience, and you can count on it day after day.
