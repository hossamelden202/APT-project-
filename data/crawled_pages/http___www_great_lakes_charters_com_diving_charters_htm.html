Great Lakes
Diving Charters







The Great Lakes provide plenty of open water for scuba diving excursions. Explore the Great Lakes from an alternate point of view; from beneath the waves.

For maximum safety and enjoyment we recommend using a dive charter.
The Captains are *U.S. Coast Guard Licensed and the vessels are State Inspected and loaded with the latest in safety and navigation equipment.

The Captains are highly experienced and know the sites of interest. Accommodations are available for groups of all sizes.
