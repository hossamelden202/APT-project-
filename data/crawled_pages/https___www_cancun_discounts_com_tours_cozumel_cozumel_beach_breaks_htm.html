Cozumel Beach Breaks - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I'm on a cruise ship - Can I do these tours?
A: Absolutely!  These excursions are designed specially for cruise customers, but they are open for everybody.  We offer great options for cruisers who are anxious for some sun and sand after being on the water for days, these beach breaks are a welcome treat!

Q: Can I visit these beaches on my own?
A:  Yes, but in addition to cover charges, these beach breaks also include things like your transportation, activities such as horseback riding, snorkeling or clear kayaks, plus drinks and food. They are excellent deals considering all the things included that you don't have to worry about.

Q: Which beach club is closest to my cruise ship?
A: That depends on which of Cozumel's three piers you're coming into. Nothing is more than 15 minutes away in Cozumel, it's a small island.
