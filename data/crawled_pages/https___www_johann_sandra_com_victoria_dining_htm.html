VICTORIA DINING

Victoria is renown for its London-style pubs, but there are many other choices here as well.  From the tourist-laden harborside eateries to small cafes, you'll have no problem finding somewhere to fit your taste.

During our last visit, we tried out Spinnaker's Brew Pub, which turned out to be quite excellent for a pub.  It was much classier than the usual brewpub, and offers live music on the weekends.  We also tried the Wharfside, which was overpriced and touristy, but after looking at a blandly prepared menu for the longest time, we were pleasantly surprised by the quality of the meal.  Also harborside is Milestone's, which has a family-style restaurant above a harborside open-air bar.  Their fish n chips and drinks were quite memorable.

Dining here is more expensive than in most Canadian cities, but If you're interested in saving money, the Entertainment Book offers 50% savings on dozens of area eateries.  For more information on dining in Victoria, visit the links below:
