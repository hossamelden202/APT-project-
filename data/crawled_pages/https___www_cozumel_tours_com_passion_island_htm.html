Visit this fantastic getaway on a private island on northern Cozumel for a day of relaxation away from crowds. Your adventure begins with a bus ride through downtown and over to Bahia Ciega. Once there, a ferry will cross you over to Isla Pasion, where a white sand beach and iridescent green and blue Caribbean waters will greet you. Sun bathe, let the gentle breeze rock your hammock, shop on our open air mini mall, swim, play, read; enjoy!

Passion Island Beach Break Highlights
  • Get away from it all with a special day on what must be the most beautiful beach in Cozumel!
  • Enjoy a delicious barbecue lunch, consisting of fish, chicken and of course guacamole!

  • Beach facilities include hammocks, palapas, beach chairs, shopping, swimming, water trampoline, beach volleyball, restaurant, kids activities, snorkeling and a kids club with babysitter!
