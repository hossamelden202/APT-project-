Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Renton is approximately 45,920 (1997), 90927 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 19,243 (1990), 36009 (2010).
The amount of land area in Renton is 42.077 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0.73 sq kilometers.
The distance from Renton to Washington DC is 2460 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 45 miles. (as the crow flies)
Renton is positioned 47.48 degrees north of the equator and 122.19 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Renton median income is $ 46,000 (1994).
