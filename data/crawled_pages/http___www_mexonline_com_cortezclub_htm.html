The Cortez Club is La Paz's premier diving and watersports center
offering a 5 star level of service, quality and professionalism to all its clients



Established since 1995, The Cortez Club has become the premier Scuba Diving, Sport fishing and water sports center in Baja California. With its operations based at La Concha Beach resort it expanded to the newly developed Costa Baja Marina & Resort and soon to be located at both Playa De La Paz and Maravia resort.

The Cortez Club prides itself on service and combined with the beauty that the Sea of Cortez has to offer, provides you the client, with everlasting memories. We offer a wide range of aquatic activities including: scuba diving, sport fishing, whale watching, and a range of water sports.
