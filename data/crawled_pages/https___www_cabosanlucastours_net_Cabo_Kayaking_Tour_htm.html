Take a fun sea kayaking tour to Land's End, where you can see the mighty waters of the Pacific Ocean meet the warm blue Sea of Cortez.  You'll glide silently by Pelican Rock and enjoy snorkeling near the Sea Lion Colony at El Arco (The Arch) and Lovers Beach (Playa del Amor). A wonderfully relaxing way to spend a couple of hours in the warm Cabo San Lucas sunshine. .

Once you've paddled along the coastline, plunge into the crystal-clear waters for a guided snorkeling experience. Explore the rich marine life that calls the Sea of Cortez home, known for its incredible diversity and natural beauty.
