Welcome to the Port of Ilwaco

Located on the Southwest coast of Washington, the Port of Ilwaco is nestled just inside the Columbia River bar. A working fishing village in a scenic and tranquil setting. Our 800-slip marina is idyllic for both recreational boaters and commercial fishermen alike. There is guest moorage available year round, as well as a back in launch, two small boat hoists and two fuel docks. Additionally, the Port of Ilwaco has both a 50-ton traveliift as well as a 75-ton travelift, a self/full service work yard and dry boat storage facilities.
