Free Updates

Updated versions of The Enhanced Edition of Earthquake 3D for Windows are available free of charge to registered users. To request an updated version, please send the following information to updates@wolton.net with the subject line EQ3D Update.

Include the following: 1) Your name 2) The e-mail address used when originally purchased. 3) Any features or suggestions that you would like to see added in future versions.

Updates may take several days to process. To speed things up, you can send your request on a forwarded copy of the original Earthquake download email that you received when you purchased (or updated) this program.
