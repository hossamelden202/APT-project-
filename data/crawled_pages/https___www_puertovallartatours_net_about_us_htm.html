Hi, our names are Johann & Sandra.  We first visited Puerto Vallarta back in 1998, and we were immediately enchanted.  During our first trip, like we always do, we signed up for several tours.  As usual, they were the highlight of our trip, offering us a unique insight into the culture and people of Puerto Vallarta, as well as really letting us see the area, including many beautiful hideaways.  We were enamored, and knew Puerto Vallarta was the place for us.  If we were enjoying it this much, we wanted to help other people to experience this great place as well!

We started Puerto Vallarta Tours in 2004, with a mission of offering visitors to Vallarta an easy and safe way to enjoy all the experiences we've grown to love.  We are a locally run and managed company in Puerto Vallarta, offering you local expertise.  If you have any problems, you can just stop by.  On our website, you can see all the great activities and excursions that are offered here, with an easy and secure online booking system and the best toll-free, English speaking customer service in the business, in case you have any questions or concerns.

In order to open these great excursions up to more people, we also decided to discount every tour in Puerto Vallarta, .  With all these savings, suddenly these great activities are opened up to more people, and this allows you to do even more great and fun things during your vacation.

We offer Puerto Vallarta's only No-Port, No-Pay policy, offering cruise passengers peace of mind, as well as savings of up to 60% off shore excursions.  We are the only agency in Vallarta to be endorsed by both the prestigious American Society of Travel Agents (ASTA) and the International Air Transport Association (IATA), the world's most recognized travel certification.  We are fully licensed and insured for your safety.

No hassles, no worries - we're here to help!
