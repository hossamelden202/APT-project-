Anonimo Video Bar

South Side - 157 Rodolfo Gomez at Olas Altas street.  Cell: 044-322-206-4439 or 150-4772 
Small, friendly and familiar place, one the newest gay bar in the Olas Altas area. Beer at 20 pesos, well drinks
at 35 pesos, daiquiris and margaritas at 30 pesos - one of the cheapest in town.

Anonimo (facebook) has 
a festive, talkative crowd with two televisions showing music videos.
Now with a second floor rooftop patio area and cozier third floor wine bar.
 
Just up from Verona restaurant and around the corner from The Palm cabaret show club. 
Owners Roger and Bryan.
Sometimes with live music or karaoke, but
please stop by to check on this.
Air conditioned inside, wireless, pet friendly.

Gay-owned with gay staff, straight friendly. 
Drink specials 35 pesos, with the beers at 20 pesos


Happy hour, 6pm-9pm
Daily, 3pm-2am
