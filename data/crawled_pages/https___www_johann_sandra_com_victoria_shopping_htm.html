Victoria Shopping

Don't Forget Your Guidebook!
Experience the history and charm of Victoria.

Victoria is a shopper's delight.  You can spend days just browsing the variety of touristy gift shops around the Inner Harbour and Government Street.  I think this is the main reason why Sandra's family wanted to come to Victoria! 

Most shops and department stores close at 5:30, M-S, but are open later on Thursdays and Fridays.  The touristy shops are all open on Sunday.  Government Street is the main drag for tourists, while Market Square and Johnson Street offer additional shopping.  Department stores line all the routes into the city, including all the chain stores you can shake a stick at.

The shopping here is subject to the U.S. exchange rates of the time, but the good news is that if you buy over $50 per receipt, you can get all your GST tax back when you leave the country.  Another way to save money is to pick up an Entertainment Book, where you can save 50% at a wide variety of shops.
