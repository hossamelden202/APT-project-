Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.

The approximate number of families is 4,992 (1990), 6980 (2010).
The amount of land area in Anacortes is 28.17 sq. kilometers.

The amount of surface water is 4.435 sq kilometers.
The distance from Anacortes to Washington DC is 2500 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 101 miles. (as the crow flies)
Anacortes is positioned 48.49 degrees north of the equator and 122.62 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Anacortes elevation is 100 feet above sea level.

The Anacortes median home price is $ 171,100 (2000).

Location

 At the end of Hwy 20 on Puget Sound near the Pacific Ocean and the Canadian border. It is on Fidalgo Island. It is about 20 miles from Bellingtham and 64 miles from Seattle. Other nearby communities include Bay View, La Conner, Ault Field, Oak Harbor, Marietta-Alderwood, Burlington and Coupeville

Anacortes Community Events

Each year in July
The Annual Shipwreck Day and Flea Market is held in mid-July each year with almost 300 booth spaces displaying all types of good including food, crafts, white elephants, plants, homemade items and much more. A Classic Car show and other events occur at the same time.
