      Washington State Vital Records Office Information

        Washington Dept. of Health
        Center for Health Statistics
        P.O. Box 9709
        Olympia, WA 98507-9709
        (360) 236-4300 Info
        (360) 236-4313 orders
        Fax: (360) 352-2586

        Make check or money order payable to Department of Health. There is a $13.00 charge for every ten year search (death, marraige, or divorce only) In the event no record is found, an $8 search fee will be retained for each certificate.

        Fax and phone request fee starts at $24.00.

      Index to Washington County Vital Records Offices

        Most Washington state counties offer certified copies of birth certificates from 1937 to present. Select the county where the birth occurred and contact them directly for more information.

        To obtain birth and death records before July 1, 1907 or marriage and divorce records before January 1, 1968 contact the county auditor/clerk where the event occurred. Marriage records should be available from the the county auditor where the event occurred. For death, marriage and divorce records after these dates contact the State Office
