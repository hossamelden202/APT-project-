JET SKI & BEACH BREAK COMBO

Located on one of Cozumel's best beaches, we offer an awesome discount on wave runner rentals!  Zoom up and down the Cozumel coastline, doing cookies and touring the beach-lined shores of this cozy island aboard your own Wave Runner!

Also known as jet skis, you can rent these 2-person wave runners by the half-hour, or if you want a longer ride, just book more than one half-hour interval.  After you're done with watersports excitement, relax at one of Cozumel's best beach clubs!

Your Jet Ski rental includes complimentary access to this popular spot, where you can enjoy sunbathing, swimming, and beachside amenities. Combine your wave runner rental with other activities like snorkeling or our awesome all-inclusive deal which includes food and drinks all day long.
