Highland Park Trails

Directions: From I-65 take Hwy 62 east approx. 2 miles. Turn left onto SR 3 (at the Dairy Mart), then left onto Plank Rd. Turn right onto Spruce Dr. and park at the dead end.

(Check out the interactive map above.)
Length: Approx. 2.5 miles
Trail Type: Single track
Skill Level: Intermediate
Trail Description: The loop trail has a wealth of small climbs, turns, dips, berms, fast technical sections and lots of big jumps.
