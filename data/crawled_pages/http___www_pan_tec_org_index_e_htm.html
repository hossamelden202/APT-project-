Professional Association for Underwater Technology

Diving with mixed gases and rebreathers means opening up new paths,
by meeting the special requirements this is done in a responsible way.

As far as known P.A.N. is the first global non-profit tec-diving organisation,
it is governmental registered in Rüsselsheim (a town in Germany), the reg.# is 636.

The „Professional Association for uNderwater technology“ is open to all experienced divers from all over the world.
