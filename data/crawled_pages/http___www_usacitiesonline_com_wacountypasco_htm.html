Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Pasco is approximately 20,370 (1990), 59781 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 7,698 (1990), 17983 (2010).
The amount of land area in Pasco is 59.056 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 1.219 sq kilometers.
The distance from Pasco to Washington DC is 2282 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 192 miles. (as the crow flies)
Pasco is positioned 46.25 degrees north of the equator and 119.12 degrees west of the prime meridian.
Pasco average annual rainfall is 12.5 inches per year
Pasco average annual humidity is 55

History & History Related Items

Pasco was named by Virgil C. Bogue, a construction engineer for Northern Pacific who helped build a railroad in the Andes Mountains near Cerro de Pasco in Peru.
