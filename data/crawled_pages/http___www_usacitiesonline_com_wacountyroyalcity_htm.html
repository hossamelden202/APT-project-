Statistics & Facts

The Washington state capital is Olympia.
The population of Royal City is approximately 1,556 (1997), 2140 (2010).
The approximate number of families is 327 (1990), 486 (2010).
The amount of land area in Royal City is 3.262 sq. kilometers.
The amount of surface water is 0 sq kilometers.
The distance from Royal City to Washington DC is 2318 miles.
The distance to the Washington state capital is 160 miles. (as the crow flies)
Royal City is positioned 46.89 degrees north of the equator and 119.61 degrees west of the prime meridian.

Location

 in central Washington, southwest of Moses Lake, off Highway 26 about 15 miles east of the Columbia River and 20 miles south of Interstate 90.

Economy & Industry
In the past it has been dry crops, wheat, corn, potatoes and sugar beets. Currently the main stay is that of apple orchards. Thousands of acres of apple trees have been planted over the past few years.

Royal City Community Events

July - 2nd Friday-Sunday each year
Community Days celebration starts the second Friday of July and goes through Sunday. BBQ dinner, talent show, parade, fun in the park, gymkanna horse competition, and golf tournament.
