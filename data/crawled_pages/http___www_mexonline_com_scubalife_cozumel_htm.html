You, our guest
When making your reservation, you can do this on our easy-to-navigate booking platform via our website. Upon check-in, we will go over those details and ensure you?re ready for a wonderful day on the water. We are always deciding our dive sites on the day of your trip to analyze where is the best spot to meet your desires, and of course according to the day's conditions.



Bringing your own gear? We will take care of it for you!
If you?re traveling with your gear, you can count on us. We will tag it - clean it - store it - and have it set it up on board for you daily. We have a temperature-controlled facility where we can safely stow your gear until your next dive.

Our boats, our guests, and our staff
We pride ourselves on small groups, and paying the most attention to our divers and their needs. We put no more than 8 divers on board, with 2 PADI Divemaster & our Captain.

Our boats are rated for 12 passengers, 2 crew & a Capitan, so if you are coming as a larger group, we can accommodate that too - but only on a Private Charter. Regular trips are a maximum of 8 divers.



Bottom Time & Nitrox
We love diving - so why stop when you still have air? We guarantee your dive up to 70 minutes, or 800PSI, meaning - everyone has a chance to dive their tank. If diving Nitrox, we offer 32% or 36% in both 80cuft tanks or 100cuft tank.

Our Fleet
We have two top-of-the-line custom-built Scuba Life Cozumel boats, made for diving - you will notice the difference. Kailani & Leilani are powered by twin 150 HP Yamaha 4 stroke ultra-low emission outboards. They have been designed and outfitted with the comfort and safety of scuba divers in mind. Both boats are equipped with DAN Emergency O2 Kit, First Aid Kit, Solas Rated Life Jackets, GPS, Bluetooth and Nautilus VHS Marine Radios.
