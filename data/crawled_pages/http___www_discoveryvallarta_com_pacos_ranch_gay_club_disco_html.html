Paco's Ranch Disco and NightClub
 

South Side - 278 Ignacio Vallarta at Lazaro Cardena street.  Tel: 222-7667, 222-8147 

 Paco's Ranch (website) formerly known as Club Paco Paco, is located around the corner
from fun disco La Noche and just down the street from popular nightclub CC Slaughter.

 Paco's Ranch (facebook) features good drink prices, a good-size dance floor,
cantina space and regular drag shows pretty much 
every night of the week except Mon and Tues, at 12:30am and 3am.
Happy hour, 10pm-Midnight. 

The large main bar area which is usually busy
and there are multiple areas to hang out, dance and chat the night away. 
Upstairs you can get a bird's eye view of the action below. 
Expect fun, glitter and drag. Popular and recommended.

Originally the club was called Paco Paco, and is the longest running 
and best known gay bar in town. 
All the locals, straight and gay, know its name, location and reputation.

Always has been one of the top late night entertainment spots for the 
gay community in Puerto Vallarta Mexico.
Daily, 10pm-6am
