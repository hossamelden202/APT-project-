Cozumel Parks - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which park can you recommend me, I travel with family?
A: We recommend Xcaret, that is a great choice for families, it offers great activities for kids, lots of things to see and do.  Xcaret is really a spectacular place.  We also recommend Xel-Ha, a great place to spend a day, where you can relax, do some snorkeling, and enjoy lots of activities, but not as many as Xcaret offers. 

Chankanaab Park is a great choice for anyone on a cruise ship or staying on Cozumel and doesn't want to have to ride the ferry over to the mainland, also a great choice from People in visiting Playa del Carmen.  Chankanaab is conveniently located in the Island.  If you want to do a dolphin swim, you can start planning your excursion.

Discover Mexico offers you a unique cultural experience, it is a great theme park that will show you the cultural history of Mexico, this tour offers you the chance to combine with another excursion, you can make a combo with Chankanaab Park. Rio Lagartos and Sian Ka'an, both incredible nature preserves, great for birdwatching or an eco-adventure.  All great choices for cruise ship passengers and hotel guests.
