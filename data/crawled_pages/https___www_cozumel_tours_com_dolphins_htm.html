COZUMEL DOLPHIN SWIM FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I DO THESE EXCURSIONS?
A: Yes! All cruise passengers can do these excursions and save huge over the cruise ships!
Q: MY CRUISE SHIP IS SELLING A DOLPHIN SWIM FOR $199.00! IS IT THE SAME?
A: Believe it or not, this is probably the Swim Adventure. The same one we sell for $104.25. You're getting the same service, just paying a WHOLE lot less!
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - HOW DO I GET TO THE DOLPHINS?
A: There are taxis waiting for you at the pier as you deboard the ship. Just hop in one and ask the driver to take you to Chankanaab Park (or you can just say "Dolphins").
Q: CAN I RELAX AND ENJOY CHANKANAAB PARK BEFORE AND AFTER MY SWIM?
A: Absolutely! Your dolphin swim includes the entrance fees to Chankanaab Park, so you can just show your voucher and enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and exploring at the park all day long if you like. Park hours are between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM..
Q: HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO GET TO THE DOLPHINS FROM THE CRUISE PIER?
A: Well, there are three cruise piers in Cozumel, so the answer is "it depends". However, even the furthest one should take no more than 15 minutes, and cost no more than $10.00 for a taxi.
Q: MY HUSBAND JUST WANTS TO COME AND WATCH, NOT SWIM. CAN HE DO THAT?
A: Of course! Observers are welcome, free of charge, however, they will need to pay entrance into Chankanaab Park if they are not swimming. The fee is $21.00 USD for adults and $14.00 USD for kids ages 3-11. Kids under 3 are free.
Q: I'M NOT A GOOD SWIMMER, CAN I STILL SWIM WITH THE DOLPHINS?
A: Yes! You don't need to be a strong swimmer. You'll have a lifejacket, so you'll just float around and dog paddling is quite sufficient for this activity. If you're scared of the deep water, the Encounter may be for you.
Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
