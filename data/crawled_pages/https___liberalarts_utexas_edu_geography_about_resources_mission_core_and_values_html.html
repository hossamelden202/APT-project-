Mission, Core, and Values

Paralleling and expanding those of the University, the Department of Geography and the Environment has its own Mission, Core Purpose, and Core Values.

Mission

To provide the highest levels of geographic teaching, learning, and mentoring while engaging in cutting?edge research, and applying this knowledge at scales ranging from local to global.

Core Purpose

To explore and understand bio?physical and social environments, and their interactions and transformations, thereby contributing to a more just and sustainable world.

Core Values

Exploration – An international vision
Inquiry – Field work is a fundamental activity
Critical Thought – An attitude that everything is subject to questioning Respect – Foster equality, fairness, ethics, principles, appreciation Collaboration – Ideas must be developed in a community of scholars Motivation – Intellectual curiosity is paramount
