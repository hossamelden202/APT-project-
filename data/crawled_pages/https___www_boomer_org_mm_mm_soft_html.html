MultiMedia and CAI Software in Pharmacy

MultiMedia

Computer Assisted Instruction

All sizes for the Macintosh files are the approximate size of the .sit archive.
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
    • A HPLC tutorial and demonstration [66K] written by Keith Brain at the Welsh School of Pharmacy [HyperCardTM (Macintosh).] Please let Keith know if you are using this in your teaching (brain@cardiff.ac.uk).
  • Pharmacokinetic Simulation
    • One Compartment Model - IV Bolus [33k] written by David Bourne[HyperCardTM (Macintosh)]
    • One Compartment Model - IV Infusion [22 K] written by David Bourne[HyperCardTM (Macintosh)].
    • One Compartment Model - Oral [33 K] written by David Bourne[HyperCardTM (Macintosh)].
    • pH Partition Calculation (Brodie D Value) [10k] written by David Bourne [HyperCardTM Macintosh].

    Please let me know if you notice any errors. David Bourne

  • Pharmacology
    • Antiepileptics in Pharmacology [656 K] an Adobe Reader presentation of an Adobe PersuasionTM slide presentation by Bill Riffee
    • Seizure Movie [2,288 K] as a DirectorTM, stand-alone, movie by Bill Riffee
    • Sodium Channel Animation [192 K] (Macintosh .mov file) by Bill Riffee
  • Pharmacy
    • Pharmacy Gabber [645 K] teaches generic-trade equivalents and pronunciation of pharmaceutical names, by Cole Kenneally. A version [60 K] which requires HyperCardTM is a smaller download.
  • Physical Pharmacy
    • Acid Base Equilibria [77 K] by Richard Prankerd, Department of Pharmacy, University of Queensland. This HyperCardTM stack is a self-testing module on acid base equilibria in water. Includes identification of functional group, acid or base, and type of acid or base.
    • Langmuir Trough Simulation [44 K] by Richard Prankerd, Department of Pharmacy, University of Queensland. This HyperCard stackTM is a simulation of three Langmuir trough experiments. Richard suggests using the right arrow instead of the 'Start' button. The attached ReadMe file provides more information.
    • Micelle Formation [77 K] by Richard Prankerd, Department of Pharmacy, University of Queensland. This HyperCardTM stack is a simulation of micelle formation.
    • Single Punch Tablet Press [22 K] by Richard Prankerd, Department of Pharmacy, University of Queensland. This HyperCardTM stack is a simulation of a single punch tablet press.
  • General Purpose
    • TestActive Design your own computerized interactive tests in minutes. This program converts exams (files) written with any word processing programs to a computerized/self-grading exam. By ClinPharm International.
