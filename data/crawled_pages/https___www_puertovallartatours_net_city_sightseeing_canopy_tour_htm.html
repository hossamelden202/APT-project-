Come with us and discover the charm of this beautiful city, through a fun and exciting tropical adventure aboard our air conditioned transportation, while a certified bilingual guide explains the most significant details of this region's history, culture and traditions. We will pass through the Conchas Chinas area, and stop at a vista point in Mismaloya to take wonderful pictures of the beach.

Then we go to El Eden, where you can enjoy a fantastic journey over the trees on a beautiful canopy circuit.  We offer you canopies 3.5 km high, with a total of 12 ziplines designed with the highest safety standards, and an unforgettable experience on this extreme adventure.

After your canopy adventure you can take in the beauty of this natural paradise, where we can swim, have a tropical drink, or enjoy a delicious seafood platter (not included in the price). Finally on the way back we make a stop at a small tavern where we get to taste some of Mexico's finest Tequila and learn about the traditional Tequila making process.
