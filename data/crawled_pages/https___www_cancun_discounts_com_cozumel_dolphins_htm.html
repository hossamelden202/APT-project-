OUR RECOMMENDATIONS

Royal Dolphin Swim - This is the activity we recommend for nearly everyone.  The foot push and dorsal tow are exhilarating and will leave you smiling and laughing for the rest of your vacation. 

Dolphin Swim Adventure - This is the activity sold by the cruise ships.  We primarily recommend this for people whose budget doesn't allow for the Royal Swim.

Dolphin Encounter - We recommend this activity for all kids under 3' 7" tall, as they are too young to do the other activities.  This is also a good activity for adults who don't feel comfortable in deep water, as well as those who are on a tight budget, but don't want to miss out on interacting with the dolphins.

Dolphin Trainer for a Day - This is the ultimate dolphin swim in Cozumel.  Lasting for 3.5 hours, you get a behind-the-scenes look at what it's really like to have the dream job of dolphin trainer.  Only for ages 12 and up, this is a thrilling experience for adults and teens alike.

Chankanaab Park - If you do a dolphin swim in Cozumel, we recommend you plan to spend the rest of your day at Chankanaab Park.  Your entrance fee is included, and you can relax, snorkel, swim, or do beach activities, as well as eat and drink.  It's a relaxing and enjoyable day, and as long as it's free, you might as well use it!

IMPORTANT: It is very important to reserve your Cozumel dolphin swim online before you leave for Mexico.  This is a hugely popular activity, and especially in high season, if you wait until you get to Chankanaab Park and try to sign up, you will almost certainly not get to swim with the dolphins.  We offer you every choice possible above, and with us you can get special prices on packages as well as every possible promotion and sale price.

HOW TO GET TO CHANKANAAB PARK:

All of the dolphin swimming activities are located inside Chankanaab Park in Cozumel.  It is conveniently located just a few minutes taxi ride from all of Cozumel's cruise ship docks, as well as an easy taxi ride from Cozumel Hotels.  Taxis are everywhere in Cozumel, and are lined up at your hotel or cruise ship port ready to take you where you want to go, very easy and inexpensive.
