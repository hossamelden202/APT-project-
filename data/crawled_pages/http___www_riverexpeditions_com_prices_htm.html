Whitewater Expedition price includes wetsuits, booties, life jackets, local shuttle, professional guides, all put-in and take-out fees, hearty riverside lunch and riverside mocha bar. Skagit River Eagle Expedition price includes all of the above except wetsuits and booties. As part payment, all participants will be required to sign a participant agreement, release and acknowledgment of risk.

Wetsuit Discount

To enhance safety and comfort, we require that each participant wear a wetsuit and booties on all whitewater trips. We now include these in the trip price. We need to note your height, weight, and shoe size for the wetsuits and booties. You may take a $10 discount if you have your own wetsuit and booties. Shorties (wetsuits without a full pant) do not meet safety standards for whitewater swimming and thus do not qualify for the discount.

Group Discount

Every 10th paying passenger goes free. So, a group of 10 pays for nine, a group of twenty pays for 18, a group of 30 pays for 27, etc.
