Puerto Vallarta Condo Vacation Rentals - Beachfront Condo Building Ref. PM
 

Puerto Vallarta condominium building PM is located on popular Los Muertos beach within a short walking distance of the South Side's many fine restaurants, stores and lively nightlife. One of the Zona Romantica's newer condominium buildings with 33 units, it is the smallest of the beachfront buildings. These one & two bedroom condominiums are fully air conditioned. The building features reception desk, 24 hour security, elevator, common area rooftop dipping pool and sundeck terrace, and high speed wireless throughout the building. On the rooftop there is a common area laundry room with two washers and dryers, and a BBQ grill for use by guests. Maid service is three times per week. There are a number of palapa-covered beachfront restaurants within a stone's throw of this popular beachfront Puerto Vallarta condominium. Note: These Puerto Vallarta condos normally rent only Saturday to Saturday during the winter high season from November through the end of April.

This condo building is a short stroll to all South Side restaurants and shopping and quite close to gay clubs and bars such as Manana, Paco's Ranch, Apaches and Los Amigos. Clicking on the reference number will take you to a page with more photos of that condominium.
