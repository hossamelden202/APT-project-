Join the team of professional biologists for an experience you'll never forget.  Aboard a custom boat, designed by the legendary Jacques Cousteau for the sole purpose of whale watching and used by National Geographic researchers, you'll have the best opportunity to see the humpback whales of Puerto Vallarta up close.

Our Greenpeace-style boat is specially designed for whale watching, and is fully licensed for whale watching trips.  Our boats seat up to 12 passengers, creating a close-knit, small-group environment, and proceeds from the tour benefit the Humpback Whale Research Program at the Whale Watching center.

Snacks and beverages are provided with your tour, as you race out to sea to witness one of nature's most spectacular events - the calving and breaching of the humpbacks. Our enthusiastic and professional staff will use hydrophones to listen to the humpback's famous songs, and GPS to track their migration patterns.  Awesome!
