Scuba Clipart:

Fun Diving Pictures For You to Enjoy!

Who doesn't love scuba clipart? They're fun, lighthearted and can put a smile on anyone's face.

And, let's face it, who doesn't need a little light-hearted entertainment these days? 

Here is a collection of these cartoon like pictures for you to enjoy. So when you need a break, come on back and take another look at these pieces. If we can't be diving, we might as well have fun thinking about it.

Scuba Clipart Collection

This is one of my favorite pieces because it reminds me of a dive we did in the Keys. A barracuda was swimming straight for me but I didn't see it. My husband pointed it out and the next thing he knew, we were dancing underwater. I took him and turned him to the barracuda. Yep, I was giving him up. Luckily, he found it funny and was laughing hysterically.
