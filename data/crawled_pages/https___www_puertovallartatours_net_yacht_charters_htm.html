Puerto Vallarta Boat Rentals and Yacht Charters FAQ's

Q: I HAVE A LARGE GROUP - DO YOU HAVE BIGGER BOATS?
A: Yes!  We can charter any of the boats you see on our website for your group on a private basis.  That includes our day cruises and our sunset cruises.  Need a custom quote for your large group? Contact our groups department and they will be happy to assist you.

Q: WILL I SEE WHALES?
A: Whales are a likely sighting between late November and early April.  The rest of the year you are likely to see dolphins, giant manta rays, or sea turtles though.

Q: I'M ON A CRUISE - HOW DO I GET THERE?
A: Our private yacht charters all depart from the Marina Vallarta area, very near your cruise ship.  Exact directions will accompany your confirmation.

Q: DO YOU PROVIDE FOOD, DRINKS OR SNORKELING EQUIPMENT?
A: That depends on the boat.  Click the links above and you can see what is included with that boat.  Many people like to visit a remote beach and have lunch at a beach club or local restaurant or chill on the beach with a cool one, and of course you're welcome to bring your own food and drinks as well if you prefer.  Otherwise, just ask and we can arrange it.
