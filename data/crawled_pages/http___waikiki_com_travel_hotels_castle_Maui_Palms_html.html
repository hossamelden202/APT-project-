Maui Palms Hotel
Kaahumanu Avenue
Kahului, Maui, Hawaii 96732

Maui Palms Hotel is located on the shores of Kahului Bay, approximately two miles from Kahului Airport, and near the major attractions of the Valley Isle. Ideal for budget minded guests. Complimentary shuttle service is available between the hotel and Kahului Airport (6:30 am to 9:00 pm). A courtesy phone for the shuttle is conveniently located at the airport. Built in 1953.

Fresh water swimming pool and sun deck, tennis courts nearby, 18 hole Waiehu Golf Course, Pukalani Country Club and Silversword Golf Course nearby, 20 minute drive to Hookipa Beach Park (famous for windsurfing), Kanaha Beach Park (swimming and windsurfing beach) within 2 miles. East West Dining Room serves buffet lunch and a Teppanyaki Japanese Buffet for dinner. Cane Knife Lounge serves cocktails, with Karaoke Thursday and Friday. Banquet and meeting rooms also available.
