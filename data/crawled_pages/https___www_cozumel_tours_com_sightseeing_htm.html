SIGHTSEEING AND ISLAND TOURS FAQ's
Q: I'M ON A CRUISE SHIP - CAN I DO THESE TOURS?
A: Cruise passengers are welcome on all of these tours. The private tours are really great for cruise passengers, as you can totally customize them to any schedule. No worry about your ship arriving too late, because we'll be there to meet you.
Q: ARE THE JEEPS AUTOMATICS?
A: All dune buggies and Jeeps are manual shift.
Q: DO I NEED A DRIVER'S LICENSE TO DO THESE TOURS?
A: A driver's license is required to drive, but not to ride as a passenger.
Q: DO ANY OF THESE TOURS VISIT MAYAN RUINS?
A: The Jeep Tours and Private Van Tour visit San Gervasio and El Caracol Mayan Ruins in Cozumel. The other tours visit the small site of El Cedral. The private tours in the Riviera Maya can visit any Mayan Ruins you like, Coba, Chichen Itza, Tulum, Ek Balam, or even Ruinas del Rey in Cancun.
Q: WE HAVE A LARGE GROUP, CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE ALL OF US?
A: You bet!  We can handle groups of all sizes.  If you have over 20 people in your group, Contact our Groups Team for our VIP personalized service to fit your group with exactly the experience you're looking for and at our exclusive discount pricing.  We can also create customized experiences just for you.  If you can dream it, we can do it!
