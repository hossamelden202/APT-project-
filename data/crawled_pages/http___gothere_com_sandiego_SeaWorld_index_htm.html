NEW IN 2012!
Manta, a mega-attraction that will seamlessly transition guests from the awe of watching
rays in underwater flight into the sensation of actually being one, is
scheduled to open May 26. Swooping over sea and sky, Manta will
not only inspire riders with amazing underwater animal habitats
featuring bat rays and other amazing marine life, but also take them
on an exhilarating ride on a coaster shaped like a giant manta ray!
Gliding, flying and diving through more than a dozen twists and
turns, a maximum speed of 43 mph, and a 54-foot drop, riders will
feel as if they are a ray, taking flight from sky to sea — so close at times that the Manta’s
wings skim the waves.
