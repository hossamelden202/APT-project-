United States

Getting Support

  • Online: Go to My Oracle Support to find answers in the Oracle Support Knowledge Base, submit, update or review your Service Requests, engage the My Oracle Support Community, download software updates, and tap into Oracle proactive support tools and best practices. (You must have purchased support, have an active cloud subscription, or have an active cloud trial license to access My Oracle Support).
  • Call 1.800.223.1711 for technical or non-technical questions regarding your supported Oracle products, product downloads, My Oracle Support portal, or related support questions. For questions concerning your support license or support contract, please see the “Renewing Support” section.
  • Oracle Hospitality and Food & Beverage (MICROS) customers in the United States call 1.800.937.2211 for product related support inquiries
  • Oracle Retail (MICROS) customers in the United States call 1.800.223.1711 for product related support inquiries
  • Local Government (NetSuite for Government or Public Safety Suite) customers in the United States call 1.800.633.0769 for product related support inquiries
  • Hearing impaired customers in the U.S. who need to speak with an Oracle Support representative may use a telecommunications relay service (TRS); information about TRS is available at https://www.fcc.gov/trs, and a list of phone numbers is available at https://www.fcc.gov/general/trs-state-and-territories. International hearing impaired customers should use the TRS at 1.605.224.1837. Once in contact with Oracle Support an engineer will handle technical issues according to normal service request handling processes.

  • For questions regarding on-premises support renewals, log a Renewal Service Request, Chat or call 1.888.545.4577.
  • View pricing, Lifetime Support Policy, terms & conditions, technical support policies for more information regarding Oracle Premier Support.
