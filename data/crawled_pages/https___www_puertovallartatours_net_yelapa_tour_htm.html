This is a totally unique adventure in Puerto Vallarta.  Run only as a private tour (only you and the guide), this exciting and enlightening adventure will take you snorkeling, hiking, and on a cultural excursion to the small village of Yelapa and it's beautiful waterfall - all in one awesome day!

The tour begins with a stop at Los Arcos, where you can admire the unique rock formations and sea birds.  We'll pass by Boca de Tomatlan, Las Animas, and Quimixto, before reaching Majahuitas, where we'll enjoy snorkeling in the unique coves.

After snorkeling, we'll continue to the small village of Yelapa, known for its beautiful seasonal waterfall, fine beaches, and artistically-oriented population.  Your guide will take you on a short hiking tour of the village and jungle, to the waterfall where you can swim if you like, or just take photos. You can hire watersports like parasailing or jet skis if you like, or just relax on the beach.  Wow - what a great way to experience Puerto Vallarta!

Cruise Passengers:  Yes! This tour is perfect for a cruise shore excursion!  Pick your own start time for this tour!  You can be picked up on the cruise pier for a $70.00 fee, payable in cash the day of the tour, if you want this option add a note in special requests when booking.
