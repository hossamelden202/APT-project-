World Bank Group Archives

The Archives preserves the institutional memory of the World Bank Group and provides public access to records of the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (IBRD) and the International Development Association (IDA).

About Us

  • The Wolfensohn Legacy 30 Years On: Advancing Transparency and Development

    The WBG Archives is launching a series of product releases and events to celebrate President Wolfensohn’s legacy and to draw attention and facilitate access to records from his presidency.

  • Exhibit

    Rising Together: The Creation and Legacy of the World Bank Group-IMF African American Association

    In recognition of Black History Month, the World Bank Group Archives highlights the formation of an internal staff organization over thirty years ago and explores its evolution and influence within the Bank-Fund and local communities.

  • Description

    Transportation Development Sector description published

    The new series-level description includes more detailed information, a hierarchical view of the fonds' series, and inventory lists!

  • Profile

    New! Development Reflections: Mike James - Exploring Health and Fitness at the World Bank Group

    The newest subject of our Development Reflections series is Mike James! James discusses the role of fitness in the World Bank Group and the changes he has seen as head of the Fitness Center.

  • Discovery Tool

    New Events Added to the World Bank Group Historical Timeline

    The WBG Archives has added new events to our Historical Timeline, including historical events, such as first development loan and President Wolfensohn's "Cancer of Corruption" speech, as well as more recent events covering President Malpass's presidency.

  • Discovery Tool

    Take a Look! Country Historical Profiles Website Launched

    The Archives' new Country Historical Profiles website offers a dynamic view of WBG membership over time and profiles of every Bank Group country member, past and present. Profiles include membership information, first loans, important events, economic reports, lists of Country Directors and Executive Directors, and much, much more!
